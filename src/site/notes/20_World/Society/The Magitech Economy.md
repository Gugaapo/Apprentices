# The Magitech Economy

The global economy of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] is undergoing a massive transformation, driven by the [[20_World/Society/Technological Revolution\|Technological Revolution]]. The traditional gold-and-silver standard is rapidly being replaced by a system backed by magical resources.

## Currency and The Mana Standard
While gold, silver, and copper coins are still used for everyday transactions, the true wealth of nations is measured in [[60_Items/Materials/Crystalized Mana\|Crystalized Mana]]. High-grade mana crystals are the fuel for modern magitech, powering everything from trains to factory wards. National banks hoard mana crystals, and large-scale mercantile transactions are often settled in raw, refined, or crystalized magical energy.

## The Banking System
The rise of magitech required massive capital investment, leading to the creation of powerful centralized banks. These institutions, often backed by the wealthiest [[40_People/Factions/Houses/Noble_Houses_Overview\|Noble Houses]] or international consortiums, provide the loans necessary for building new academies, laying magitech rail lines, and funding large-scale dungeon expeditions.

## Industrial Hubs
Cities situated over [[50_Magic/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]], such as [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]], have become economic powerhouses. The density of ambient mana allows for cheaper production of magitech goods and easier refinement of magical materials. This has caused massive urbanization, as mundane workers and aspiring mages alike flock to these cities seeking wealth and opportunity.
