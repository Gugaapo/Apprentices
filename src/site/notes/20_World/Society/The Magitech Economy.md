# The Magitech Economy

> - **What it is:** The rapidly evolving global economy of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]], driven by the fusion of magic and technology and increasingly backed by magical resources.

The global economy of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] is undergoing a massive transformation, driven by the [[20_World/Society/Technological Revolution\|Technological Revolution]]. The traditional gold-and-silver standard is rapidly being supplemented — and in major transactions, replaced — by a system backed by magical resources.

While gold, silver, and copper coins are still used for everyday transactions, the true wealth of nations is increasingly measured in **[[60_Items/Materials/Crystalized Mana\|Crystalized Mana]]**. High-grade mana crystals are the fuel for modern [[20_World/Society/Technological Revolution\|magitech]], powering everything from trains to factory wards to communication networks. National banks hoard mana crystals, and large-scale mercantile transactions are often settled in raw, refined, or crystallized magical energy.

## Industrial Hubs

Cities situated over **[[10_System/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]]s**, such as [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]], have become economic powerhouses. The density of ambient [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] allows for cheaper production of magitech goods and easier refinement of magical materials. This has caused massive urbanization, as both mundane workers and aspiring mages flock to these cities seeking wealth, education, and opportunity.

## The Banking System

The rise of magitech required massive capital investment, leading to the creation of powerful centralized banks. These institutions — often backed by the wealthiest [[20_World/Society/The Noble Houses\|Noble Houses]] or international consortiums — provide the loans necessary for building new academies, laying magitech rail lines, and funding large-scale [[20_World/Society/The Dungeon Delving Industry\|dungeon expeditions]]. The banking system is one of the few institutions where [[40_People/Factions/Houses/Noble Houses\|Noble Houses]] and the rising merchant class cooperate, albeit uneasily.
