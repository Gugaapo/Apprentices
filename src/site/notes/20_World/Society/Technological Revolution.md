# Technological Revolution

> - **What it is:** The explosive transformation of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]]'s society through "magitech" — the fusion of magical power sources with mundane engineering.

[[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] is currently undergoing a massive, explosive technological revolution that is rapidly reshaping society. For millennia, magic reigned supreme on the battlefield and in industry, effectively stunting the development of mundane mechanics. Why build a better plow when a shaping mage can move the earth with a gesture?

The [[20_World/History/Timeline/06 The Splinter Wars\|Splinter Wars]] fundamentally changed this paradigm. The mass production of terrifyingly effective **firearms** proved that technology could rival — and sometimes surpass — arcane bloodlines on the battlefield. In peacetime, this realization sparked the invention of **magitech**: the fusion of magical power sources with mundane engineering to create devices and infrastructure accessible to anyone, not just mages.

## Magitech and Infrastructure

The continent is now increasingly connected by a massive web of advanced, magically-powered **steam trains** tearing through the wilderness at unprecedented speeds. Long-distance **communication networks** allow near-instant message relay across entire nations, centralizing bureaucratic and military power. Advanced **printing presses** have triggered a drastic spike in public literacy and the rise of mass [[20_World/Society/Media and Propaganda\|media]].

Magitech devices are everywhere in modern life — from enchanted lamps and heating systems to industrial manufacturing tools. For the average citizen, "magitech" simply means magic items for everyday use, not arcane weapons or combat spells.

## Ecological Impact

This rampant industrialization is causing severe ecological damage. Factories rapidly deplete natural resources. The demand for stable, potent magical fuel has caused a massive spike in the harvesting of [[60_Items/Materials/Crystalized Mana\|magical materials]] and mana crystals, pushing many magical species to the brink of local extinction and driving hunters deeper into the lethal [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]].
