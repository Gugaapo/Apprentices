# Technological Revolution

[[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] is currently undergoing a massive, explosive [[20_World/History/Timeline/08_The_Modern_Era\|Technological Revolution]] that is rapidly reshaping society. For millennia, magic reigned supreme on the battlefield and in industry, effectively stunting the development of mundane mechanics. 

## Magitech and Infrastructure
The [[20_World/History/Timeline/06_The_Splinter_Wars\|Splinter Wars]] fundamentally changed this paradigm. The mass production of terrifyingly effective firearms proved that technology could rival arcane bloodlines. In peacetime, this realization sparked the invention of "magitech"—the fusion of magical power sources with mundane engineering.

The continent is now increasingly connected by a massive web of advanced, magically-powered steam trains, tearing through the wilderness at unprecedented speeds. Telegraph lines allow for near-instant communication across massive nations, centralizing bureaucratic and military power like never before. Advanced printing presses have triggered a drastic spike in public literacy.

## Ecological Impact
This rampant industrialization is causing severe ecological damage. Factories rapidly deplete natural resources. Mining operations strip mountains bare. Crucially, the demand for stable, potent magical fuel to power this revolution has caused a massive spike in the harvesting of [[60_Items/Materials/Magical Materials\|magical monster parts]] and [[60_Items/Materials/Crystalized Mana\|Crystalized Mana]], pushing many magical species to the brink of local extinction and driving hunters deeper into the lethal [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]].

The tension between traditionalist, purist mages (who view technology as a crude crutch) and progressive mechanists is incredibly high.
