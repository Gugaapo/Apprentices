# Interspecies Relations

Humanity is not the only sapient species in the world, and the rapid expansion of human magitech and the [[20_World/Society/Spread of Magical Training\|Spread of Magical Training]] have created complex, often fraught relationships with other intelligent beings.



## [[70_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Shifters\|Shifters]]
Shifters (humans with inherited animalistic transformation traits) face significant prejudice in mainstream society. Often viewed as cursed, dangerous, or primitive, they are frequently marginalized. Many shifters find work as mercenaries, trackers, or in the dangerous lower tiers of the [[20_World/Society/The Dungeon Delving Industry\|Dungeon delving industry]], where their physical enhancements are valued over their social standing.


## Legal Status
In most human nations, non-human sapient species are not recognized as citizens and afford few, if any, legal rights. The [[Magistracy\|Magistracy]] rarely investigates crimes against non-humans unless it threatens human settlements.
