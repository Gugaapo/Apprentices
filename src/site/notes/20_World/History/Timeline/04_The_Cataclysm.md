# 04 - The Cataclysm

Less than a century after the Silence of the Gods, a supernatural disaster of apocalyptic proportions severed the back of the reeling [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]]. 

## The Desertification of Xlotic
With unnatural, horrifying speed, the great plains of central [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]]—the heartland of human civilization—began to dry out. Within a span of 20 years, an incredibly fertile region capable of sustaining tens of millions of people was reduced to a lifeless, sprawling wasteland known today as the [[30_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] Desert. 

## Global Migration
The sudden, absolute destruction of their agricultural base triggered mass starvation on a genocidal scale. The shifting desert sands buried ancient cities and forced the largest known migration in human history. Millions of Ikosian refugees fled north, crossing the [[30_Locations/Regions/Shivan Archipelago\|Shivan Archipelago]] to conquer the "uncivilized" natives of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] and rapidly establishing modern human kingdoms.

The event fundamentally shifted the political and magical center of the world from [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] to [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]]. The glorious Ikosian Empire was dead, buried forever beneath the sands.
