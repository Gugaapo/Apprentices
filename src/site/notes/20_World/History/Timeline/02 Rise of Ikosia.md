# Rise of Ikosia

> - **When:** 2–900 STC (~900 years of supremacy)
> - **What happened:** [[40_People/NPCs/Others/Shutur-Tarana\|Shutur-Tarana]] conquered rival city-states, founded the [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]], and created the [[20_World/01 Time Loop and The Sovereign Gate\|Sovereign Gate]].
> - **Why it matters:** Every modern institution (Houses, academies, the Church, the Mage Guild) descends from Ikosian blueprints.

## The Conquest

Over 1500 years before the [[20_World/History/Timeline/04 The Cataclysm\|Cataclysm]], human civilization flourished along the Umani-Re river valley in northern [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]]. The city-state of Ikos, under [[40_People/NPCs/Others/Shutur-Tarana\|Shutur-Tarana Ihilkush]], swiftly conquered neighboring rivals. Facing an alliance of 13 remaining city-states, Shutur-Tarana decimated their combined forces at **Quatari** through superior combat magic and strategy, crowning himself Emperor.

He famously possessed the [[20_World/01 Time Loop and The Sovereign Gate\|Sovereign Gate]], a powerful time/domain artifact.

## The House System

The Ikosians realized that entirely subjugating powerful mage clans was too costly and destroyed their unique family magics. Shutur-Tarana instituted the **House System** — granting clans massive local autonomy and privileges in exchange for service and loyalty. This allowed rapid pacification without obliterating arcane knowledge.

## The State Mage Guild

Unlike other civilizations where priesthoods hoarded magic, the Ikosians sharply divided religious authority from magical practice. The state-run **Mage Guild** aggressively assimilated magics from conquered peoples, shedding dogma for pragmatism. The Guild ensured spells were widely shared while policing mages for rebellious intent.

## 900 Years of Supremacy

For nine centuries, the [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]] was unstoppable. It expanded rapidly across northern [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]], colonized the [[30_Locations/Regions/Shivan Archipelago\|Shivan Archipelago]], and dominated trade. Attempts to conquer [[30_Locations/Regions/Koth\|Koth]] and [[30_Locations/Continents/Blantyrre\|Blantyrre]] failed, but Ikosia remained the undisputed superpower — rivaled only distantly by the Grand Dynasty of [[30_Locations/Continents/Hsan\|Hsan]].
