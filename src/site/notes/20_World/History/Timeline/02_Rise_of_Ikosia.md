# 02 - Rise of Ikosia 

Over 1500 years before the [[20_World/History/Timeline/04_The_Cataclysm\|Cataclysm]], human civilization flourished along the Umani-Re river valley in northern [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]]. The city-state of Ikos, under the rule of the fiercely ambitious king [[40_People/NPCs/Others/Shutur-Tarana\|Shutur-Tarana Ihilkush]], swiftly conquered neighboring rivals. Facing a great alliance of 13 remaining city-states, Shutur-Tarana decimated their combined forces at Quatari through superior combat magic and strategy, crowning himself Emperor. He famously possessed the [[60_Items/Key Items/06 Sovereign Gate\|06 Sovereign Gate]], a powerful time/domain artifact.

## The House System
The Ikosians realized that entirely subjugating powerful, localized mage clans was too costly and resulted in the loss of their unique, deeply-held family magics. Emperor Shutur-Tarana instituted the **House System**, granting these powerful clans massive local autonomy and privileges in exchange for their service and loyalty. This allowed the Empire to rapidly pacify conquered territories without obliterating their arcane knowledge.

## The State Mage Guild
Unlike other civilizations where priesthoods hoarded magic, the Ikosians sharply divided religious authority from magical practice. The state-run Mage Guild aggressively assimilated magics from conquered peoples, shedding dogma for pragmatism. The Guild ensured spells were widely shared across the empire while simultaneously policing mages for rebellious intent. 

## 900 Years of Supremacy
For nine centuries, the [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]] was an unstoppable behemoth. It expanded rapidly, subjugating northern [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]], colonizing the [[30_Locations/Regions/Shivan Archipelago\|Shivan Archipelago]], and dominating early trade. Attempts to conquer the dense jungles of [[30_Locations/Regions/Koth\|Koth]] or the monster-infested continent of [[30_Locations/Continents/Blantyrre\|Blantyrre]] failed, but they remained the undisputed superpower of the human world, rivaled only distantly by the Grand Dynasty of [[30_Locations/Continents/Hsan\|Hsan]].
