# Mutr
**Translation:** Flesh / Mutation
**School/Menu:** Soul Magic (Level 3)

A characteristic glyph (similar to Alteration's Flei) but used specifically to force grotesque, cancerous, or unnatural biological changes.

## Example Formations
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Reis\|Reis]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Mutr\|Mutr]]**: Violate Flesh (Cause flesh to rot or mutate uncontrollably)
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Bind\|Bind]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Mutr\|Mutr]]**: Graft Flesh (Fuse two living creatures together)
