# Raum
**Translation:** Space / Area Size
**School/Menu:** Dimensionalism (Level 2), Divination (Level 3)

The characteristic glyph representing physical dimensions, volume, and the fabric of space itself.

## Example Formations
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Raum\|Raum]]**: Alter Space (Expand or compress the interior of a room)
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Sehn\|Sehn]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Raum\|Raum]]**: Perceive Space (Sense the layout of a dungeon)
