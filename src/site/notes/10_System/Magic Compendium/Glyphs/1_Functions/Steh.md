# Steh
**Translation:** Stop
**School/Menu:** Animation (Level 3)

A function/modifier glyph that immediately halts an animated construct or autonomous spell.

## Example Formations
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Steh\|Steh]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Baut\|Baut]]**: Stop Statue (Freeze a rampaging golem)
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Steh\|Steh]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Leic\|Leic]]**: Stop Corpse (Halt an undead minion in its tracks)
