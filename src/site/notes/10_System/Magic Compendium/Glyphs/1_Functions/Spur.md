# Spur
**Translation:** Track
**School/Menu:** Divination (Level 3)

A function glyph used to isolate and follow a specific physical or magical trail over time and distance.

## Example Formations
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Spur\|Spur]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Blut\|Blut]]**: Track Blood (Follow a wounded target)
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Spur\|Spur]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Aura\|Aura]]**: Track Aura (Follow a mage's residual mana)
