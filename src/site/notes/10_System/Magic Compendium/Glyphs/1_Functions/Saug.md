# Saug
**Translation:** Drain / Siphon
**School/Menu:** Blood Magic (Alteration Specialization), Soul Magic (Level 4)

A function glyph used to forcibly extract energy, vitality, or matter from a target into the caster.

## Example Formations
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Saug\|Saug]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Blut\|Blut]]**: Drain Blood (Siphon a target's vitality)
- **[[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Saug\|Saug]] [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Seel\|Seel]]**: Drain Soul (Extract spiritual energy)
