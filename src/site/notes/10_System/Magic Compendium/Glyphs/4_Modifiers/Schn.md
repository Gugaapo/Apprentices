# Schn
**Translation:** Fast / Projectile (Also Haste)
**School/Menu:** Combat Magic (Projection Specialization), Chronomancy (Divination Specialization)

A modifier glyph that accelerates the speed of a spell or alters the flow of time to increase swiftness.
