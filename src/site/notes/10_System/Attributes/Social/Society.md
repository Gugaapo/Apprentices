**Category:** Social

Society (formerly Etiquette) represents your knowledge of high society, noble politics, and complex bureaucracies. It dictates your ability to navigate the intricate social webs of the Altazian noble houses, the political maneuvering of the Cyorian Academy, and the formal protocols required when dealing with royalty or high-ranking officials.

### Common Uses
- **Protocol:** Knowing exactly how to address a noble, dress for a gala, or navigate a formal dinner without causing offense.
- **Bureaucracy:** Navigating Academy paperwork, legal loopholes, or government red tape.
- **Gossip:** Gathering rumors and political secrets from the upper crust of society.

### Specializations
- Altazian Nobility, Academy Politics, Bureaucracy, High Society Gossip, Protocol.


## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Novice | Outsider. Commits faux pas constantly and knows nothing of noble etiquette. |
| **2** | Competent | Citizen. Knows the basic laws, who the major players are, and how to behave at a formal dinner. |
| **3** | Skilled | Courtier. Can navigate Academy bureaucracy, mingle with Altazian nobles, and understand political subtext. |
| **4** | Expert | Aristocrat. Can manipulate House politics, orchestrate scandals, and gain audiences with national leaders. |
| **5** | Master | Kingmaker. Understands the entire web of global alliances and can shape national policy from the shadows. |
