**Category:** Social

Connections represent a character's network, reputation, and ability to navigate various societal structures—from the high courts of Noble Houses to the seedy criminal underbelly. A character with high Connections knows who to talk to in order to acquire illegal goods, how to secure a meeting with a Professor, or the etiquette required to avoid offending an Archmage's patron.

### Common Uses
- **Networking:** Navigating high society or Academy bureaucracy efficiently.
- **Streetwise Interactions:** Finding a black market, fencing stolen goods, or making contact with a clandestine organization (like an Aranea web or a gang).
- **Rumor-Mongering:** Sifting through local gossip to find the kernel of truth, or planting a rumor of your own.

### Specializations
- Black Market, Noble Protocol, Academy Bureaucracy, Underworld Syndicates.

## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0** | Untrained | Ignorant. Socially awkward outcast; constantly offends people out of sheer ignorance. |
| **1** | Novice | Familiar. Knows the basic rules of etiquette or gang turf lines. |
| **2** | Competent | Networker. Can get a meeting with a minor noble or find a fence for stolen goods within a day. |
| **3** | Skilled | Connected. Can navigate high society effortlessly or act as a respected middle-man for a criminal syndicate. |
| **4** | Expert | Broker. Can arrange an audience with the Mayor or effectively mobilize a street gang with just a few rumors. |
| **5** | Master | Puppeteer. Knows the devastating secrets of top-tier Archmages, controls vast black markets, or can leverage a King's ear. |
