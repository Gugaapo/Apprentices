# Medicine

**Category:** Mental

Medicine represents your knowledge of mundane anatomy, biology, and first aid. Even in a world of magic, Medical Magic is notoriously difficult and rare. Knowing how to set a bone, staunch bleeding, or identify poisons using mundane means is an invaluable and highly respected skill. It is also a critical foundation for those attempting to learn the Medical Magic or Blood Magic disciplines.

### Common Uses
- **First Aid:** Stabilizing a dying comrade or treating wounds to prevent infection.
- **Diagnosis:** Identifying diseases, poisons, or the specific cause of a physical ailment.
- **Anatomy:** Knowing exactly where to strike a specific creature for maximum biological damage.

### Specializations
- First Aid, Toxicology/Poisons, Anatomy, Surgery, Disease.


## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **2** | Competent | First Responder. Can stop bleeding, set broken bones, and treat common illnesses. |

| **4** | Expert | Surgeon. Can perform complex operations, cure magical diseases, and understand intricate anatomy. |
| **5** | Master | Master Physician. Can revive the clinically dead, perform organ transplants, and manipulate biology to perfection. |
