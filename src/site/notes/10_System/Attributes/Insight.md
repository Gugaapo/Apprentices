# Insight

**Category:** Social

Insight (formerly Empathy) is the ability to read people, detect lies, and sense hidden motives or emotions. It is the defensive counterpart to Deception and Charm. A character with high Insight can tell when a smile is forced, when a merchant is overcharging, or when an ally is secretly harboring dark intentions.

### Common Uses
- **Lie Detection:** Sensing the subtle physical tells that indicate someone is lying.
- **Reading the Room:** Quickly assessing the mood and power dynamics of a social situation.
- **Interrogation (Passive):** Knowing exactly which psychological buttons to push to get someone to open up.

### Specializations
- Lie Detection, Body Language, Reading the Room, Psychological Profiling.


## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Novice | Oblivious. Takes everything at face value and misses social cues. |
| **2** | Competent | Empathetic. Can tell when a friend is upset or lying about something obvious. |
| **3** | Skilled | Perceptive. Can read body language, spot hidden motives, and catch skilled liars in a contradiction. |
| **4** | Expert | Profiler. Can deduce a person's entire emotional state, predict their next move, and see through magical glamours of emotion. |
| **5** | Master | Soul Reader. Can instantly understand a person's deepest fears and desires just from how they stand and breathe. |
