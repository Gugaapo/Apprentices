# Willpower

Your **Willpower** attribute represents your raw force of intent, mental fortitude, and ability to forcibly push [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] through your channels. It is the core attribute for defending against mental intrusions and dictates your raw magical output and brute-force casting capabilities when finesse isn't enough.

## Mechanical Uses

Mechanically, Willpower sets your passive **Mind Defense**, combines with Calculation to determine your **Mana Capacity**, and combines with Memory for your **Sanity Resistance** pool.

### Associated Magic Moves
- **[[10_System/00 Moves/02 Magic Moves#Cast a Spell Formula\|Cast a Spell Formula]]:** Used when rolling destructive or raw energy spells.
- **[[10_System/00 Moves/02 Magic Moves#Channel Unstructured Magic\|Channel Unstructured Magic]]:** Used when you abandon formulas and blast raw, unshaped mana through sheer force of will.

### Common Uses
- **Mind Defense:** Resisting telepathic intrusions, compulsions, and illusions through sheer mental resilience.
- **Sanity & Trauma:** Anchoring your mind against atrocities and psychic damage.
- **Raw Output:** Forcing massive amounts of [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] into a spell to increase its destructive power or defensive strength.
- **Pain Tolerance:** Pushing through severe physical pain or magical exhaustion to complete a casting.

## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Novice | Average resolve. Susceptible to basic coercion and intimidation. |
| **2** | Competent | Solid resolve. Can push through minor pain and resist mild psychic nudges or illusions. |
| **3** | Skilled | Strong resolve. Capable of sustained high [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] output; can resist direct psychic interrogation with effort. |
| **4** | Expert | Iron will. Can generate massive, brute-force [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] surges; highly resistant to deep psychological manipulation. |
| **5** | Master | Indomitable. Can ignore severe physical trauma to complete a spell and block powerful master telepaths from breaching their mind. |
