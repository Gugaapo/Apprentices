# Streetwise

**Category:** Social

Streetwise (formerly Leadership) represents your ability to navigate the criminal underworld, find black markets, and deal with gangs, smugglers, or corrupt officials. It is the gritty counterpart to Society. If you need to fence stolen magical artifacts, hire an assassin, or find an illegal Blood Magic tutor in the slums of Cyoria, you need Streetwise.

### Common Uses
- **The Black Market:** Finding illegal goods, poisons, or fenced items.
- **Underworld Contacts:** Locating and negotiating with criminals, smugglers, or the local thieves' guild.
- **Urban Survival:** Knowing which alleys to avoid and how to blend in with the dregs of society.

### Specializations
- Black Markets, Smuggling Routes, Gang Politics, Fencing Stolen Goods, Bribery.


## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Novice | Sheltered. Easily scammed and stands out in dangerous neighborhoods. |
| **2** | Competent | Local. Knows which alleys to avoid, where to fence stolen goods, and the names of local gangs. |
| **3** | Skilled | Fixer. Can navigate the black market, find illegal tutors (like Blood Magic), and gather rumors effectively. |
| **4** | Expert | Underboss. Has contacts in every major criminal syndicate, can smuggle anything, and organize large-scale heists. |
| **5** | Master | Shadow Broker. Controls the flow of information in the underworld; nothing illegal happens in the city without them knowing. |
