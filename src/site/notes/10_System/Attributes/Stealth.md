# Stealth

**Category:** Physical

Stealth dictates your ability to move silently, hide in shadows, and avoid detection. Whether you are sneaking past the Academy guards after curfew, trailing a suspicious Cultist through the streets of Cyoria, or avoiding the gaze of a monstrous Drake in the Dungeon, Stealth is vital for remaining unseen.

### Common Uses
- **Sneaking:** Moving without making a sound over various types of terrain.
- **Hiding:** Finding cover and remaining perfectly still to avoid visual detection.
- **Tailing:** Following a target without them realizing they are being watched.

### Specializations
- Urban Stealth, Wilderness Stealth, Tailing, Hiding in Shadows.


## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Novice | Noisy. Steps on twigs and breathes heavily. |
| **2** | Competent | Quiet. Can sneak past distracted guards or sleep through an area unnoticed. |
| **3** | Skilled | Prowler. Can move silently in the dark, follow targets undetected, and avoid basic patrols. |
| **4** | Expert | Shadow. Can blend into crowds, hide in plain sight, and bypass sophisticated security completely unnoticed. |
| **5** | Master | Ghost. Leaves no footprints, makes zero sound, and can evade even magical detection senses or apex predators. |
