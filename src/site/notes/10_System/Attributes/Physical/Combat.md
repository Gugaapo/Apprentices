**Category:** Physical

Combat reflects your physical reflexes, spatial awareness, and training in mundane armed or unarmed conflict. While a mage relies on their spellcasting for the bulk of their power, a character with high Combat proficiency knows how to deflect a sword swing, fire a rifle, throw an alchemical grenade with precision, or defend themselves with a staff when their mana runs dry.

### Common Uses
- **Physical Attacks (Ranged):** Firing crossbows, mundane firearms, or precisely pitching explosive vials.
- **Physical Attacks (Melee):** Fighting effectively with a rapier, quarterstaff, or bare fists.
- **Spatial Awareness in the Fray:** Keeping track of multiple combatants and finding tactical openings without relying entirely on magic.

### Specializations
- Marksmanship, Quarterstaff Defense, Unarmed Strikes, Trench Tactics.

## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0** | Untrained | Pacifist. Panic swings wildly, cannot hit the broad side of a barn with a gun. |
| **1** | Novice | Basic Training. Knows which end of the sword is pointy; can generally hit a stationary target. |
| **2** | Competent | Fighter. Handled themselves in a scrap; can effectively wield a simple weapon in a skirmish. |
| **3** | Skilled | Veteran. Highly trained physical combatant. Knows how to flow between melee defense and ranged attacks. |
| **4** | Expert | Weapon Master. A blur in combat; fires with deadly precision and disarms opponents effortlessly. |
| **5** | Master | Apex. Can cut down arrows mid-flight or shoot the wings off a fly; physically lethal even to high-tier combat mages. |
