**Category:** Physical

Dexterity is the combination of fine motor control, hand-eye coordination, and the ability to move unseen. It governs feats of delicate physical manipulation—such as picking a lock, safely handling volatile alchemical reagents, or stealing a set of keys—as well as the ability to move silently, stick to the shadows, and bypass physical security measures without raising an alarm. 

### Common Uses
- **Stealth and Infiltration:** Moving silently past guards, monsters, or Warders.
- **Sleight of Hand:** Picking pockets, slipping restraints, or palming a small object unnoticed.
- **Delicate Work:** Assisting in engineering or alchemy when physical precision is required.

### Specializations
- Lockpicking, Pickpocketing, Shadow Walk, Escapology.

## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0** | Untrained | Clumsy. Drops things frequently, trips over their own feet in the dark. |
| **1** | Novice | Careful. Can move quietly if they focus completely, or perform basic sleight of hand. |
| **2** | Competent | Sneak. Can confidently ghost past an inattentive guard or pick a standard padlock. |
| **3** | Skilled | Thief. Can vanish into thin shadows, palm items while being observed, and bypass complex tumbler locks. |
| **4** | Expert | Ghost. Can move silently over broken glass, pick pockets from highly alert targets, and disable active traps. |
| **5** | Master | Phantom. Can essentially turn invisible simply by standing perfectly still in a shadow, and can crack a combination vault by feel. |
