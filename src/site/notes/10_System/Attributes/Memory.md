# Memory

**Category:** Magical

Memory represents your character's capability to recall complex formulas across resets and their overall conceptual retention. In a time-loop scenario, this attribute is paramount for retaining knowledge, spells, and experiences from previous loops, and it serves directly as your Sanity Resistance. 

Mechanically, Memory dictates the size of your "mental hard drive" for internalized magic. You can hold a number of **Mastered Spells** equal to your **Memory × 2**. Mastered spells can be cast silently, without gestures, cost 1 less Mana (minimum 1), and can be used as instant reactions.

### Common Uses
- **Mastered Spells:** Internalizing your signature spells for instant, silent, and cheaper casting.
- **Loop Retention:** Keeping detailed notes and formulas perfectly intact in your mind across resets.
- **Sanity Resistance:** Anchoring your mind against the psychological trauma of repeated deaths and the immense pressure of the time loop.
- **Formula Recall:** Memorizing massive, multi-page matrices for complex spellcasting without relying on a physical grimoire.

## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Novice | Average recall. Can memorize simple facts. **Limit:** 2 Mastered Spells. |
| **2** | Competent | Good memory. Can study for exams efficiently. **Limit:** 4 Mastered Spells. |
| **3** | Skilled | Excellent recall. Can memorize complex spell formulas. **Limit:** 6 Mastered Spells. |
| **4** | Expert | Exceptional memory. Near-perfect recall of distant events. **Limit:** 8 Mastered Spells. |
| **5** | Master | Eidetic-level recall. Can perfectly reconstruct massive visual scenes. **Limit:** 10 Mastered Spells. |
