**Category:** Mental

Survival (formerly Nature) represents your knowledge of the natural world and your ability to stay alive in hostile environments. This includes navigating the deep wilderness, tracking prey or enemies, and understanding the behaviors and weaknesses of magical beasts. It is essential for anyone venturing into the Koth Wilds or descending into the Dungeon beneath Cyoria.

### Common Uses
- **Tracking:** Following footprints, broken twigs, or magical residue through the wilderness.
- **Foraging:** Finding safe food, water, and shelter in harsh environments.
- **Monster Lore:** Recalling the habits, territories, and vulnerabilities of creatures like Drakes or Aranea.

### Specializations
- Tracking, The Dungeon, Koth Wilds, Beast Lore, Foraging.
