# Deception

**Category:** Social

Deception (formerly Manipulation) is the art of lying, fast-talking, scamming, and hiding your true intentions. It is the attribute of con artists, spies, and anyone who needs to operate under false pretenses. Whether you are forging a document, wearing a magical glamour, or simply lying to the City Watch, Deception is your shield against scrutiny.

### Common Uses
- **Lying:** Convincing someone of a falsehood or covering up your true motives.
- **Disguise:** Passing yourself off as someone else (often paired with Glamour magic).
- **Fast-Talking:** Confusing or overwhelming someone with rapid, plausible-sounding nonsense to escape a sticky situation.

### Specializations
- Fast-Talking, Forgery, Acting/Disguise, Scams, Bluffing.


## Attribute Tiers
| Tier | Title | Example Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Novice | Honest. Terrible liar; stutters and avoids eye contact. |
| **2** | Competent | Fibber. Can tell believable white lies, bluff in games, and keep a straight face. |
| **3** | Skilled | Con Artist. Can forge documents, maintain false identities, and scam merchants. |
| **4** | Expert | Manipulator. Can gaslight interrogators, weave complex webs of lies, and play factions against each other. |
| **5** | Master | Mastermind. Can convince people of entirely false realities, beat truth spells, and live a double life perfectly. |
