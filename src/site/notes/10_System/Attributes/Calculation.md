# Calculation

**Category:** Magical

Calculation measures your mental processing speed, spatial reasoning, and ability to handle multi-step magical matrices on the fly. It is the engine behind your ability to multitask and manage complex spellwork in high-stress situations without losing focus.

Mechanically, Calculation acts as your mental RAM. It dictates your **Ongoing Spell Limit** (the maximum number of active, sustained spells you can maintain at once) and your **Simultaneous Casting** limit (you can cast multiple spells in a single action as long as their combined Tiers do not exceed your Calculation).

### Common Uses
- **Multitasking:** Maintaining an active ward while simultaneously casting an offensive spell.
- **Simultaneous Casting:** Weaving multiple spell matrices at the exact same moment to overwhelm an opponent's defenses.
- **Vector Math:** Instantly calculating the trajectory of a kinetic blast or a dimensional teleportation jump.
- **Matrix Weaving:** Processing the complex, overlapping geometric shapes required for advanced spell formulas in real time.
