# Syndicate Scion

*You grew up in the Cyorian underworld. The local gangs, smugglers, and black-market alchemists know your name, for better or worse.*

## Overview

Cyoria is a city of glittering magical towers, but its shadows are deep and dangerous. You were raised in those shadows. Whether your parents ran a gambling den, smuggled Kothian artifacts, or acted as brokers for illegal Blood Magic tutors, you learned the rules of the street long before you learned the laws of Eldemar.

You understand the economy of favors, the reality of violence, and the fact that the city guard can always be bought. While the Academy professors try to teach you the "proper" way to cast a ward, you already know how to bypass one using a crowbar and a stolen disruption crystal. 

---

## Mechanical Benefits

- **Starting Bonus:** Gain +1 free dot in **[[10_System/Attributes/Social/Connections\|Connections]]** and +1 free dot in **[[10_System/Attributes/Physical/Dexterity\|Dexterity]]**.
- **Connection — The Black Market:** You know exactly who to talk to when you need something illegal. Once per arc, you can use your underworld ties to procure highly restricted magical materials, hire a professional thief, or completely launder your identity to hide from the authorities.

---

## Roleplay Hooks
*   **Transactional Relationships:** You view every interaction as a trade. If you do someone a favor, you expect one in return, and you keep a mental ledger of debts.
*   **Distrust of Authority:** You actively avoid talking to the city guard, Academy security, or high-ranking officials unless absolutely necessary.
*   **Street Slang:** You frequently use underworld jargon, thieves' cant, or crude idioms that shock your more sheltered classmates.
