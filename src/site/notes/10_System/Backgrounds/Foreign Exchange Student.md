# Foreign Exchange Student

*You hail from Koth, Sulamnon, or another distant nation. You are unfamiliar with Cyoria but bring unique perspectives and exotic methodologies to the Academy.*

## Overview

Eldemar is not the center of the world, though its citizens often act like it is. You traveled across continents, oceans, or heavily guarded borders to attend the prestigious Cyorian Academy. Your homeland has completely different cultural norms, political structures, and approaches to magical theory.

While you might occasionally struggle with Altazian idioms or the sheer arrogance of Eldemarian purebloods, your outsider perspective allows you to see social dynamics and magical applications that the locals are completely blind to. You are an exotic novelty to some, and an unwelcomed foreigner to others, but you possess a worldly wisdom that cannot be taught in a classroom.

---

## Mechanical Benefits

- **Starting Bonus:** Gain +1 free dot in **[[10_System/Attributes/Insight\|Insight]]** and +1 free dot in **[[90_AI_Context/SOCIETY\|Society]]**.
- **Connection — The Embassy:** You maintain ties to your homeland's diplomatic mission or expatriate community in Cyoria. Once per arc, you can use this connection to secure foreign goods, seek diplomatic sanctuary, or obtain information that Eldemarian censors have tried to suppress.

---

## Roleplay Hooks
*   **Cultural Misunderstanding:** You frequently misinterpret Eldemarian social cues, or intentionally play the "ignorant foreigner" card to get out of trouble.
*   **Exotic Traditions:** Your spellcasting incorporates strange vocalizations, gestures, or focuses not found in standard Cyorian textbooks.
*   **Homesick:** You fiercely defend the reputation of your homeland and actively seek out food, music, or news from across the border.
