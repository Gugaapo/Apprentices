# Noble Scion

**Category:** Background

## Description
You are the younger child of a noble house of Eldemar. Your family name opens doors — and creates expectations you may never fulfil. You grew up with tutors, servants, and the constant pressure to uphold your family's reputation. At the Academy, your name precedes you — some students admire you, others resent you, and everyone watches you.

## Starting Bonus
Begin with **3 extra Attribute Points** to represent your privileged upbringing (tutors, nutrition, training). These follow normal maximums.

## Connection
**House Name.** Once per session, you may invoke your family's reputation and political influence to gain an audience, bypass bureaucracy, or secure a favour from [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]'s elite. The GM determines the scope.

## Roleplay Hooks
- Your family expects regular letters reporting your progress — and your professors' political leanings.
- Another student from a rival house is in your year. The rivalry is centuries old.
- A family servant has been stationed in [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]] to 'look after you.' They report everything back to your parents.

> *"My family didn't send me here to learn. They sent me here to win."*
