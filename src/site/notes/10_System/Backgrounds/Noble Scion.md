# Noble Scion

**Category:** Background

## Description
You are the younger child of a powerful noble house. Your family name opens doors — and creates expectations you may never fulfil. You grew up with tutors, servants, and the constant pressure to uphold your family's reputation. At the Academy, your name precedes you — some students admire you, others resent you, and everyone watches you.

## Fictional Permission
You can demand basic accommodations and entry to places normally off-limits to commoners.

## Mechanical Benefits

**Background Move — Commanding Presence:** When you leverage your family name to command obedience from lower-class citizens or academy staff, roll `+Social` with **Advantage**.

## Connection (Anchor)
**House Name.** once per quest, you may invoke your family's reputation and political influence to gain an audience, bypass bureaucracy, or secure a favour from the nation's elite. The GM determines the scope.

## Roleplay Hooks
- Your family expects regular letters reporting your progress — and your professors' political leanings.
- Another student from a rival house is in your year. The rivalry is centuries old.
- A family servant has been stationed in the city to 'look after you.' They report everything back to your parents.

> *"My family didn't send me here to learn. They sent me here to win."*
