**Physical Defense** (often referred to as Evasion) is a passive derived stat that represents a character's ability to instinctively dodge, deflect, or simply not be in the path of incoming physical harm.

### Calculation

Your **Physical Defense** is equal to the formula: **`6 + Mobility + Highest of (Awareness or Dexterity)`**.

### How It Works

Instead of forcing defenders to roll the dice every time they are attacked, Physical Defense acts as a static Target Number (TN) for the attacker. 
* If the attacker's Casting Roll (or mundane combat attack roll) is **less than or equal to** your Physical Defense, the attack misses or is harmlessly deflected.
* If the attacker's roll **exceeds** your Physical Defense, the attack hits and deals its listed damage normally.

*(Note: Physical Defense represents your passive, mundane evasion. A defender may still choose to spend a Reaction to cast an instant magical shield—like the `Scht Wand` Mastered spell—which generates a new magical threshold that blocks the attack instead).*

### Examples

**Example 1: The Agile Scout**
Lyra has Mobility 3, Awareness 2, and Dexterity 4.
* Her highest value between Awareness and Dexterity is Dexterity (4).
* Her Physical Defense is `6 + 3 + 4 = 13`.
* An attacker attempting to hit her with a combat spell must roll a 14 or higher.

**Example 2: The Oblivious Scholar**
A young academy student has Mobility 1, Awareness 1, and Dexterity 0.
* His highest value between Awareness and Dexterity is Awareness (1).
* His Physical Defense is `6 + 1 + 1 = 8`.
* He is extremely easy to hit in open combat.
