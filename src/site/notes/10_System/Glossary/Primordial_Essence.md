# Primordial Essence

Primordial Essence is a mysterious and highly sought-after substance within this setting, central to the existence of **Shifters**.

## Nature
It is believed to be a fragment of the chaotic vitality of the primordial dragons. Unlike standard [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]], it is a permanent part of a being's soul or physical makeup.

## Ritual Use
The essence is extracted (often through dark or secretive means) from shifter children. It is a necessary component for the initiation rituals of shifter tribes, allowing an "outsider" or a child born without the innate bond to be bonded with a spirit and gain the ability to transform.

## Ethical Concerns
Because extraction is often traumatic or destructive to the source, the trade and use of Primordial Essence is a highly controversial subject, often hidden from the public and the [[The_Triumvirate_Church\|Triumvirate Church]].
