# Mana Burn (The Exhaustion Cascade)

**Mana Burn** is the severe physical backlash that occurs when a mage pushes magic through their spiritual canals beyond their body's safe Mana Capacity (their [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Capacity\|Mana Capacity]]).

There is no "mana bar" that simply empties. If a student tries to pull an all-nighter fighting monsters and casts spells that push their total accumulated Mana past their Capacity (`4 + Core Resonance`), they enter the **Exhaustion Cascade**:

> **IF** your Spent Mana > Mana Capacity:
> - **THEN IF (+1 to 5 over):** Take 1 Physical Wound immediately per spell. (*Fatigued*)
> - **THEN IF (+6 to 8 over):** Your spell automatically fails on any roll ≤ TN, AND take 1 Wound. (*Exhausted*)
> - **THEN IF (+9+ over):** You fall unconscious or die from massive organ failure. (*Depleted*)
