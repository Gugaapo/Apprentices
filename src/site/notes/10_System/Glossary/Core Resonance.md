**Core Resonance** represents a mage's intrinsic attunement to channeling magical energy. It serves as the primary multiplier for a character's [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Capacity\|Mana Capacity]], reflecting their soul's highest point of magical development.

### Definition

Your **Core Resonance** is a derived value equal to the **highest dot rating** among three of your Magical Attributes: **[[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]]**, **[[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Finesse\|Finesse]]**, or **[[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Willpower\|Willpower]]**. 

*(Note: [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Memory\|Memory]] is explicitly excluded from this equation as it governs mental storage limit and mastered spells, not the physical processing of raw mana.)*

### How It Works

Because your safe [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Capacity\|Mana Capacity]] is calculated via the formula **`4 + Core Resonance`**, improving your highest eligible Magical attribute directly expands the amount of raw magic your body can withstand before tearing your spiritual canals and suffering [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Burn\|Mana Burn]]. 

This system ensures that any powerful mage, regardless of whether they specialize in raw force (Willpower), delicate weaves (Finesse), or complex structure (Calculation), has the spiritual depth necessary to fuel high-tier spells.

### Examples

**Example 1: The Focused Artificer**
Corin has Calculation 5, Finesse 2, and Willpower 2. 
* His highest eligible attribute is Calculation (5).
* His Core Resonance is **5**.
* His total Mana Capacity is `4 + 5 = 9`.

**Example 2: The Generalist Mage**
A mid-tier academy student has Calculation 3, Finesse 3, and Willpower 3.
* Her highest eligible attribute is a tie among all three (3).
* Her Core Resonance is **3**.
* Her total Mana Capacity is `4 + 3 = 7`.

**Example 3: The Scholar**
A brilliant historian has Memory 4, Calculation 1, Finesse 1, and Willpower 1.
* Although Memory is his highest Magical attribute, it does *not* contribute to Core Resonance.
* His highest eligible attribute is 1.
* His Core Resonance is **1**.
* His total Mana Capacity is `4 + 1 = 5`.

> [!NOTE] 
> Because Core Resonance only looks at your *highest* relevant attribute, becoming a "jack-of-all-trades" in magic does not inherently increase your Mana Capacity. You must push at least one core shaping attribute to its absolute limits to safely channel apocalyptic levels of magic.
