# Mana Well

A **Mana Well** is a natural phenomenon where the ambient, unstructured magical energy ([[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]]) from deep within the earth (often originating from the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] or the Heart of the World) naturally vents or pools on the surface. These upwellings of pure, atmospheric mana act as inexhaustible batteries for mages and magical infrastructure.

## Mechanics and Importance

1. **Ambient Regeneration:**
   A mage's personal [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] regenerates over time by passively siphoning ambient mana from the environment. Being near a Mana Well dramatically speeds up this process, limited only by the mage's biological and spiritual capacity to safely process the raw energy without suffering mana poisoning.

2. **Rank System:**
   Mana Wells are categorized by a universally accepted **Rank** system, which measures both the volume and the density of the mana being emitted.
   - **Rank 1 - 2:** Minor bleeding points. Often found in untamed wilderness or beneath small-to-medium settlements. They can support local industries, magical workshops, and minor magical infrastructure (like [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Knyazov Dveri\|Knyazov Dveri]], built over a Rank 2 well).
   - **Rank 3 - 5:** Major industrial hubs. A strong enough well can safely power city-wide ward networks, mass transit, and large-scale enchanting operations. 
   - **Rank 6 - 8:** Continent-shaping phenomena. Such wells are extremely rare and highly contested, often forming the epicenters of powerful empires or highly advanced academies.
   - **Rank 9:** The highest known tier. Only one Rank 9 Mana Well is definitively known to exist in the world: **The Hole** in [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]]. The sheer magnitude of energy pouring from a Rank 9 well can spontaneously crystallize, warp local space-time, bind primordial entities, and power magics that would elsewhere be completely impossible.

3. **Crystallization:**
   Near powerful Mana Wells or deep within the Dungeon where mana is thickest, atmospheric mana can precipitate into solid form, creating **Crystalized Mana**. This resource is the equivalent of magical coal or oil, driving the modern industrial and economic engines of the world.

4. **Personal Mana Wells vs. Natural:**
   While the term strictly refers to geographical upwellings, incredibly powerful magical artifacts (such as the [[60_Items/Key Items/01 Imperial Crown\|01 Imperial Crown]]) are sometimes described as containing "Internal" or "Personal" Mana Wells, functioning as boundless pockets of pre-processed mana that bypass the user's natural limits.

## Setting Context

Because of their immense strategic and economic value, human civilization clusters heavily around known Mana Wells. However, because these wells also attract deadly magical beasts from the Dungeon and the wilderness, living near a high-ranking well is extremely dangerous without massive, organized protection—hence the creation of fortified magical capitals like Cyoria. Their energy is both the lifeblood of magical society and a beacon for catastrophic threats.
