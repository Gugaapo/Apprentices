# Spell Formula (and Formulas)

A **Formula** is the rigid mathematical syntax of a spell written down in a student's Grimoire (composed of exactly one Verb, one Noun, and optional Modifiers). When a Formula is cast, it manifests into reality as a **Spell**.

*See also: [[10_System/Glossary/Artifice\|Artifice]] for the practice of permanently carving Formulas into physical objects.*
