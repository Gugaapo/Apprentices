# Hollow

A **Hollow** is a term used to describe a mage whose mind has completely broken.

Mechanically, this occurs when a player character's Sanity track reaches 0. The character goes completely crazy, losing their sense of self and grip on reality. At this point, they become an unplayable shell of their former self.
