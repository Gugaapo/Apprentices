Traits are permanent markers on a character's mind and soul. Some are prerequisites for specialised [[10_System/Glossary/Shaping\|Shaping]] Skills, while others provide unique benefits or drawbacks that carry throughout their career.

---

## Positive Traits (Merits)

| Merit                                            | Points | Description                                                                                                                 |
| :----------------------------------------------- | :----- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Mana Sensitivity\|Mana Sensitivity]]**       | 1      | +1 to your 2d6 roll for [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] or [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] when detecting magic.                      |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Natural Shaper\|Natural Shaper]]**           | 2      | Choose one School; +1 to all Casting Rolls for that specific School.                                                        |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Eidetic Memory\|Eidetic Memory]]**           | 2      | +2 to [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Memory\|Memory]] checks to resist Sanity Erosion.                                                                           |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Inner Peace\|Inner Peace]]**                 | 2      | Start each adventure with +2 bonus Sanity or [[10_System/Glossary/Soul Integrity\|Soul Integrity]].                                                            |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Social Chameleon\|Social Chameleon]]**       | 1      | Ignore the -1 penalty from "Detachment" (Sanity 7).                                                                         |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Efficiency Matrix\|Efficiency Matrix]]**     | 2      | You can endure 1 additional point of Mana over your Capacity before entering the Exhaustion Cascade.                        |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Fast Caster\|Fast Caster]]**                 | 2      | Ignore the +2 Difficulty penalty for Unstructured (Tier 0) Snap casting once per combat.                                    |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Soul Anchor\|Soul Anchor]]**                 | 3      | Once per month, ignore 1 point of Soul Damage.                                                                              |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Academic Prodigy\|Academic Prodigy]]**       | 1      | +2 to [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] rolls for theoretical tasks.                                                                          |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Versatile Mind\|Versatile Mind]]**           | 3      | Once per adventure, swap one dot between two [[10_System/Glossary/Shaping\|Shaping]] Skills for a day.                                                  |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Mana Battery\|Mana Battery]]**               | 3      | +2 to your maximum **Mana Capacity** limit.                                                                                 |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Iron Will\|Iron Will]]**                     | 3      | +2 to passive [[10_System/Glossary/Mind Defense\|Mind Defense]] Target Number.                                                                               |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Danger Sense\|Danger Sense]]**               | 3      | Cannot be surprised in combat; act in the first round of an ambush.                                                         |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Wealthy Patron\|Wealthy Patron]]**           | 2      | Substantial cash stipend at the start of every month.                                                                       |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Beast Speaker\|Beast Speaker]]**             | 1      | +2 to [[10_System/Attributes/Mental/Survival\|Survival]] or [[10_System/Attributes/Social/Charm\|Charm]] rolls when dealing with mundane animals.                                                    |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Soul Sight\|Soul Sight]]**                   | 4      | Prerequisite for [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]]/[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Spirit Calling\|Spirit Calling]]. Can see ghosts and the souls of the living.                             |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Empath\|Empath]]**                           | 2      | Prerequisite for [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Mind Magic\|Mind Magic]]. Sensitive to psychic noise (-1 to Calculation rolls in high-trauma areas).                 |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Forbidden Knowledge\|Forbidden Knowledge]]** | 3      | Prerequisite for [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Necromancy\|Necromancy]]/[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Carving\|Soul Carving]]. Costs 1 permanent Sanity. Use risks "True Death" deletion by authorities. |

| :----------------------------------------------------------------- | :----- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Soul Bleed\|Soul Bleed]]**               | -3     | When you take Soul Damage, take +1 extra point.                                             |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Fragile Mind\|Fragile Mind]]**           | -2     | -2 to all 2d6 rolls to resist Sanity Erosion.                                               |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Mana Blind\|Mana Blind]]**               | -1     | Cannot detect magic without a [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] spell.                             |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Social Outcast\|Social Outcast]]**       | -1     | Permanent -1 penalty to social rolls (stacks with Sanity).                                  |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Academic Struggle\|Academic Struggle]]** | -1     | -2 to [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] rolls for theoretical/academic tests.                                 |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Magical Static\|Magical Static]]**       | -1     | Your magic is noisy; +1 Difficulty (TN) required for Silent casting.                        |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Arcane Echoes\|Arcane Echoes]]**         | -2     | Distracted by magical debris from previous castings; occasional -1 penalty.                 |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Weak Will\|Weak Will]]**                 | -2     | -2 to passive [[10_System/Glossary/Mind Defense\|Mind Defense]] Target Number.                                               |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Physical Frailty\|Physical Frailty]]**   | -2     | Start with 5 Physical Health boxes instead of 7 (skipping Bruised/Hurt).                    |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Nightmares\|Nightmares]]**               | -1     | Start the first day of an adventure with -1 to all mental rolls.                            |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Soul Tether\|Soul Tether]]**             | -2     | If another Student dies, you take 1 point of Sanity Erosion.                                |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Mana Wasting\|Mana Wasting]]**           | -3     | All spells cost +1 Mana to cast (cancels out the discount from Mastered Spells).            |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Open Book\|Open Book]]**                 | -2     | -2 to Mind Defense against telepathic reading or emotional sensing.                         |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Bad Mana Channels\|Bad Mana Channels]]** | -2     | Maximum Modifiers limit (`Calculation - 1`) is permanently reduced by 1.              |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Notorious Family\|Notorious Family]]**   | -1     | -1 to [[10_System/Attributes/Social/Charm\|Charm]] and [[10_System/Attributes/Social/Society\|10_System/Attributes/Social/Society]] rolls when dealing with law-abiding citizens/Academy staff. |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Magical Signature Anomaly\|Magical Signature Anomaly]]** | -1     | Your personal mana signature is highly distinct and lingers for days; Warders/Diviners gain +2 to track you. |
| **[[50_Magic/Traits/Flaws/Severe Phobia\|Severe Phobia]]**         | -1 / -2| -2 to all 2d6 rolls when confronted with a specific phobia (-1 for uncommon, -2 for common).|
