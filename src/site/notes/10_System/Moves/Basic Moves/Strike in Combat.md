# Strike in Combat

*A Basic Move. Available to all Students.*

When you **engage an enemy in non-magical, physical combat**, roll `+Physical`.
- **10+:** You strike true. Deal your damage, and choose one:
  - You evade their reprisal.
  - You put them in a bad spot.
  - You deal +1 damage.
- **7-9:** You strike true and deal your damage, but the enemy hits you back or inflicts a condition.
