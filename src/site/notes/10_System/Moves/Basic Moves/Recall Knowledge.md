# Recall Knowledge

*A Basic Move. Available to all Students.*

When you **consult your academic knowledge regarding history, science, or beasts**, roll `+Mental`.
- **10+:** The GM tells you something interesting, useful, and directly applicable.
- **7-9:** The GM tells you something interesting, but it's incomplete or comes with a catch.
