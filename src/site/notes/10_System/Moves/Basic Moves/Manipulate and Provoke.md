# Manipulate and Provoke

*A Basic Move. Available to all Students.*

When you **have leverage and use it to charm, bribe, or threaten an NPC**, roll `+Social`.
- **10+:** They do what you ask, provided you follow through with your end of the bargain.
- **7-9:** They will do it, but they need a concrete assurance or a significant concession first. 
- **6-:** They are offended, report you, or demand the impossible.
