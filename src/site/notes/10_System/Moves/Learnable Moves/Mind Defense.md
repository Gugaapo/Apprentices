# Mind Defense

*A modular Playbook Move. Can be learned to actively resist mental assaults.*

When you **actively brace your mind against an invasive telepathic, illusory, or psychological assault**, roll `+Willpower`.
- **10+:** Your mental fortress holds perfectly. The intrusion is repelled, and you take +1 forward against the attacker or gain momentary insight into their intent.
- **7-9:** You repel the worst of it, but the friction causes a headache. Choose one:
  - You take 1 temporary Sanity damage (heals next downtime).
  - You suffer a temporary condition (like Paranoia or Disorientation) until the scene ends.
  - You let a small, harmless piece of information slip.
- **6-:** Your defenses shatter. The attacker gets what they want (extracts information, implants an illusion), and the GM makes a Hard Move concerning your mental state.
