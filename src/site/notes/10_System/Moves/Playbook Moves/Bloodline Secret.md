# Bloodline Secret

*A Playbook Move for [[00_Meta/Playbooks/The Heir\|The Heir]]*

You begin the game with 1 Mastered Spell from a highly restricted or extinct School (like Soul Magic or an advanced specialization). It does not count toward your Memory limit. When you cast it, you may take 1 Tick Strain (Wound) to treat a `7-9` as a `10+`.
