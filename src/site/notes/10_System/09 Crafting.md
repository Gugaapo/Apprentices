# Magical Crafting: Alchemy & Spell Formula

Crafting physical magical assets requires time, the correct Skill, and raw materials (abstracted as a "**Material Cache**" of a Tier equal to the desired item). Both [[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|Alchemy]] and [[50_Magic/Glossary/Spell Formula\|Spell Formula]] utilize these core mechanics.

Crafting is resolved using a **Progress Track**: to successfully create an item, you must accumulate **Successes equal to the item's Tier**. 

## 1. Alchemy (The Recipe)
Alchemy creates consumable or physical items without requiring a Shaping roll to use. 

* **Time Cost:** 1 Time Slot per crafting attempt. Consumes 1 Alchemical Material Cache.
* **Roll:** `Academics + Nature`
* **Progress:** 
  * *Success:* Gain 1 Progress point. 
  * *Critical Success (10):* Gain 2 Progress points.
  * *(Simple Tier 1-2 potions usually take 1—2 Time Slots. Complex Tier 3+ formulas may take 3—5 dedicated Time Slots).*
* **Failure:** 0 Progress; the Time Slot is spent. A Critical Failure ruins the batch, wasting the Material Cache entirely.
* **Pushing the Limits:** If a crafter misses a required success right before a crucial deadline, they can intentionally suffer [[50_Magic/Glossary/Mana Burn\|Mana Burn]] (taking 1 Physical Health damage and applying Wound Penalties) to force 1 "fake" success, representing desperate and dangerous late-night cramming.

## 2. Spell Formula (The Schematic)
Formulas anchor spells into physical mediums for instant casting at double the spell's normal `mana` cost.

* **Time Cost:** 1 Full Day (3 Time Slots) per crafting attempt. Consumes 1 Anchor Material Cache. 
* **Roll:** `Calculation` (or `Finesse`) + `Academics`. The crafter (or a willing ally) must also cast the Base Spell to anchor it.
* **Progress:** 
  * *Success:* Gain 1 Progress point. 
  * *(Simple Tier 1-2 formulas usually require 1—2 full days. Complex Tier 3+ formulas require intense dedication over multiple days or weeks to accumulate successes).*
* **Failure:** The day is spent with no progress. A Critical Failure destabilizes the matrix; the creator suffers 1 Physical Damage (Wound Penalty) from mana feedback, and the physical item shatters.

> [!TIP] Collaborative Crafting
> Allies can help! An ally can spend a matching Time Slot to Assist the crafting roll (+1 die), or supply the necessary magic (e.g., casting the Base Spell into the formula matrix if the crafter hasn't mastered it). 
> 
> Furthermore, **Tier 3+ Material Caches cannot simply be bought**—they often require the entire party to dedicate a Night slot to raid a dungeon, harvest from a dangerous beast, or steal from an Academy vault.
