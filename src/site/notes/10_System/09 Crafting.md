# Magical Crafting: Alchemy & Artifice

Crafting physical magical assets requires time, the correct School, and raw materials (abstracted as a "**Material Cache**" of a Tier/Complexity equal to the desired item). Both [[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|Alchemy]] and [[10_System/Glossary/Artifice\|Artifice]] utilize these core mechanics.

Crafting is resolved using a **Progress Track**: to successfully create an item, you must accumulate **Progress points equal to the item's Complexity value** (e.g., 2 points for a *Hard* item). 

## 1. Alchemy (The Recipe)
Alchemy creates consumable or physical items without requiring a 2d6 Shaping roll to use in combat. 

* **Time Cost:** 1 Time Slot per crafting attempt. Consumes 1 Alchemical Material Cache.
* **Roll:** `2d6 + Crafting + Alchemy` vs TN 6 (or higher for complex recipes).
* **Progress:** 
  * *Success:* Gain 1 Progress point. 
  * *Critical Success (Natural 12):* Gain 2 Progress points.
* **Failure:** 0 Progress; the Time Slot is spent. A Critical Failure (Natural 2) ruins the batch, wasting the Material Cache entirely.
* **Pushing the Limits:** If a crafter misses a required success right before a crucial deadline, they can intentionally suffer **Mana Burn** (taking 1 Physical Health damage and applying Wound Penalties) to force 1 "fake" Progress point, representing desperate and dangerous late-night cramming.

## 2. Artifice (The Engraving)
Artifice anchors Root Words into physical mediums for instant casting. The crafter physically carves the glyph roots into the item.

* **Time Cost:** 1 Full Day (3 Time Slots) per crafting attempt. Consumes 1 Anchor Material Cache. 
* **Roll:** `2d6 + Crafting (or Calculation/Finesse) + Artifice` vs TN 6 (or higher for complex items). The crafter (or a willing ally) must also possess the necessary Root Words in their Grimoire to engrave them.
* **Progress:** 
  * *Success:* Gain 1 Progress point. 
* **Failure:** The day is spent with no progress. A Critical Failure destabilizes the matrix; the creator suffers 1 Physical Damage (Wound Penalty) from mana feedback, and the physical item shatters.
* **Usage:** Once created, firing an Artifice item ignores the caster's Calculation limit, activates instantly, and requires **no 2d6 casting roll**. However, it causes **Double the normal Mana cost** for those spell roots due to the inefficiency of pushing raw mana passively through a physical object.

> [!TIP] Collaborative Crafting
> Allies can help! An ally can spend a matching Time Slot to Assist the crafting roll (+1 to the roll), or supply the necessary magic (e.g., providing the Formula if the crafter hasn't learned it). 
> 
> Furthermore, **Complex Material Caches cannot simply be bought**—they often require the entire party to dedicate a Night slot to raid a dungeon, harvest from a dangerous beast, or steal from an Academy vault.
