# Blood Magic

*Blood Magic deals with the manipulation of a person's life force, usually using the target's (or caster's) own blood as a proxy. Ritual sacrifice, powering spells through one's own health, improved enhancement rituals, and bloodline theft all fall under this discipline.*
**Classification:** Specialization **Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]]

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Blood Magic` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

No specific boundaries defined.

## The Core Matrix
Unlocking this knowledge grants you structural mastery of the following base Glyphs to use during *Shape the Matrix*:
**Vocabulary:** **Blood Magic**: (Forbidden application of Medical Magic - Uses [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Blut\|Blut]] and [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Flei\|Flei]])

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Causes a minor nosebleed in a target. (`Andr Blut Klen`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** Boils the target's blood slightly, causing intense pain. (`Andr Blut Pein`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** Draws blood out of a wound and shapes it into a rigid blade. (`Andr Blut Form`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** Violently rips the blood from a target's body to heal the caster. (`Andr Flei Blut Saug`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** Steals a bloodline trait from a victim and permanently alters the caster's DNA. (`Andr Blut Atom Bind`)
