# Transformation

**Classification:** Specialization
**Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]]

Transformation magic changes a target's form, in whole or in part, through shape shifting and augmentation spells that apply a **transformation shell** over the target's soul. Crucially, **both the target and the caster must have souls** for transformation to work. It cannot affect the truly soulless, such as constructs or minor undead.

> [!WARNING] Dangerous Specialty
> Transformation is not taught to beginning students in most academies. A mage who transforms into a non-humanoid form will find it extremely difficult to cast spells from that form, as their mana channels, physical gestures, and vocal cords are completely altered. Most specialists combine this expertise with [[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|Alchemy]], creating transformation potions instead of casting directly.

## Specialization Rules
This is an advanced Specialization. To learn **Spells (Powers)** in this field, a mage must use their dot-purchase selections from the **[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]]** skill track. Furthermore, specific spells within Transformation will explicitly require a secondary prerequisite (such as 1 or 2 dots in collateral Schools) to simulate the mixed-matrix required to cast them.

## Progression
Acquiring dots and Powers here requires deep dedication. The spells are extremely potent but narrowly focused.

## Example Powers

| Tier | Example Effect | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | *Minor Shift* | Change a part of the body temporarily — a hand into a paw, eyes into cat-eyes for low-light vision. |
| **Tier 2** | *Augmentation Shell* | Temporarily grant the target the physical traits of a simple animal (claws, hide, gills). |
| **Tier 3** | *Partial Transformation* | Transform the target's body from the waist down into that of a different creature species. |
| **Tier 4** | *Full Transformation* | Complete change into a non-humanoid form. The caster retains their mind but spell-casting becomes extremely difficult. |
| **Tier 5** | *Permanent Shell* | A full transformation that becomes permanent on the target without ongoing [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] cost — requires a masterwork formula. |

## Mechanical Focus
All Transformation spells target the soul-body interface, requiring [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] for anatomical precision and [[10_System/Attributes/Finesse\|Finesse]] for a clean transformation without side effects. A botched roll may cause partial, painful, or unstable shifts.

## Lore Deep Dive

Transformation is the ultimate application of [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] on biological matter, but it differs fundamentally from standard flesh-shaping or [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Medical Magic\|Medical Magic]]. Instead of manually knitting bone and tissue, the mage constructs a metaphysical **Transformation Shell** around the target's soul, which forces the physical body to adapt to the new spiritual blueprint. 

Because it relies on this soul-body interface, the target must possess a soul. You cannot transform a stone into a dog, nor can you transform an undead zombie into a bird. 

**The Spellcasting Problem:** One of the greatest hurdles of Transformation is that spellcasting requires highly specific gestures, vocalizations, and mana channel alignments. When a human transforms into an eagle, they lose the vocal cords to chant and the hands to form somatic seals. Their mana channels warp to fit the new body. Therefore, casting magic while fully transformed is nearly impossible without decades of specialized practice or completely custom spell formulas designed for that specific animal form.
