# Alteration

**Classification:** Core School

Alteration (formerly "Transmutation") is the art of restructuring matter, changing physical properties, composition, density, and form of objects and living organisms. It is heavily utilized for both shaping the environment (such as earth manipulation) and modifying biology.

## [[50_Magic/Glossary/Specializations\|Specializations]]
As mages progress their dots in this School, they may elect to unlock highly advanced **[[50_Magic/Glossary/Specializations\|Specializations]]** by meeting secondary prerequisites. The specializations for this school are:
- **[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Medical Magic\|Medical Magic]]**
- **[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Transformation\|Transformation]]**
- **[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Blood Magic\|Blood Magic]]**

## Progression
Under Academy rules, acquiring a dot in a [[10_System/06 The Magic System#The Casting System\|School Proficiency]] does not instantly grant knowledge of every spell in that tier. Instead, purchasing a dot in Alteration grants the mage **exactly one Spell (Power)** from this School (at that dot level or lower).

## Example Powers

| Tier | Example Effect | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | *Flexibility* | Make a small wooden item as flexible as rubber or as brittle as glass for 5 minutes. |
| **Tier 2** | *Earth Spike* | Cause an arc of spear-like stone spikes to erupt from the ground in front of you. |
| **Tier 3** | *Flesh Binding* | Magically bind and fuse the flesh or bone of a limb so it cannot thrash or move. |
| **Tier 4** | *Deep Burial* | Rapidly restructure the soil beneath a target to swallow them deep into the earth. |
| **Tier 5** | *Form Dissolution* | Completely break down a non-magical object at the molecular level, or reshape a willing body permanently. |

## Mechanical Focus
Alteration provides durability bonuses, utility for environmental obstacles, and material crafting. It relies on [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] for structural integrity and [[10_System/Attributes/Finesse\|Finesse]] to avoid collateral changes.

## Lore Deep Dive

Alteration is the art of **restructuring what already exists**. Where [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] creates energy from [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]], Alteration uses mana as a catalyst to rearrange the molecular or atomic bonds of existing matter. The mage does not add or remove material — they change its *properties*: making stone as flexible as rubber, iron as brittle as glass, causing the earth to rapidly shift into spikes, or forcing flesh to fuse and bind.

At the Academy, Alteration is considered the most intellectually demanding of the "safe" schools. A Projection mage can brute-force their way through problems, but an Alteration mage must understand *what they are changing and how*. You cannot restructure a material you don't understand. This is why Alteration students are required to take parallel courses in [[10_System/Attributes/Survival\|Survival]] and [[10_System/Attributes/Academics\|Academics]] — the mundane sciences provide the theoretical framework that mana then executes.

**What it feels like to cast:** The caster typically needs physical contact with the target, though environmental alteration (like earth shaping) can be done at a short distance. They feel their [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] seeping into the target material like water into a sponge, then they mentally "grip" the molecular structure and twist. Observers see the material's surface ripple, shimmer, or warp as its properties change. Higher-tier effects produce audible groaning or crackling sounds as major structural changes take hold.

**The fundamental limitation:** Alteration cannot create matter from nothing, nor can it destroy matter entirely. It can only *change* what is already there. Turning lead into gold is technically possible at high tiers — but the lead must exist first.

## Design Boundaries

When creating Alteration spells, respect these rules:

**Alteration CAN:**
- Change the physical properties of existing matter (hardness, flexibility, density, conductivity)
- Manipulate the environment by restructuring it (e.g., causing earth to erupt into spikes, turning stone to mud)
- Alter biological tissue (healing, hardening skin, reshaping bone, binding flesh to restrict movement)
- Transform one material into another of similar mass at higher tiers
- Create structural changes that persist after the mana fades (Tier 5 only)

**Alteration CANNOT:**
- Create matter or energy from nothing (that's [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] or [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Conjuration\|Conjuration]])
- Move objects through space without physically acting on them (that's [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]])
- Provide information or sensory data (that's [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]])
- Animate objects with autonomous behaviour (that's [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]])

**The key verb is RESTRUCTURE.** If the spell's primary action is changing the properties of something that already exists, it belongs in Alteration.

## Combining with Specializations

- **[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Medical Magic\|Medical Magic]]:** Restructuring applied to living tissue. Requires deep knowledge of anatomy ([[10_System/Attributes/Medicine\|Medicine]]). Healing spells are Alteration effects targeted at biological systems — knitting flesh, purging disease, regrowing organs.
- **[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Transformation\|Transformation]]:** Extreme bodily restructuring — shapeshifting, limb modification, species-level changes. Often requires [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] as a secondary to "map" the target biology before altering it.
- **[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Blood Magic\|Blood Magic]]:** Forbidden. Restructuring blood and circulatory systems to weaponize the target's own biology. Requires [[50_Magic/Traits/Merits/Forbidden Knowledge\|Forbidden Knowledge]].
