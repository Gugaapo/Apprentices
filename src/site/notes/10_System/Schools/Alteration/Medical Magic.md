# Medical Magic

**Classification:** Specialization
**Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]]

# Lore
Medical Magic is a multi-disciplinary field combining [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] (to diagnose illness) and [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] (to heal wounds and cure disease).
> **Casting Roll:** 2d6 + Willpower + [Alteration]

# Lore deep dive

> [!IMPORTANT] Difficulty
> Medical magic is considered **hands-down the hardest field of magic to pursue.** It requires a massive amount of theoretical knowledge and impeccable shaping skills. Its relative youth makes it hard to find qualified teachers, and training in it requires living targets. It is almost unheard of for medical mages to practice any form of magic except ones that support their chosen specialization.

**History and The Weeping:** Medical magic is a very young magical discipline. It was founded only after the gods fell silent and stopped granting their priests healing spells. Even then, it took many years for the field to gather sufficient reputation to be taken seriously and attract world-class experts. It recently received a massive boost when a plague known as "The Weeping" swept across Altazia, causing many wealthy organizations and individuals to invest heavily into magical medical research.

**Relationship with Alchemy:** Even today, medical magic is primarily used to *supplement* traditional alchemical treatments rather than serving as a cure for absolutely everything on its own.

**The Economic Reality:** Because the field demands absolutely everything from the practitioner—massive study, living practice targets, and extreme precision—it is incredibly difficult to enter. However, medical mages are extremely well paid, meaning there is never any shortage of candidates clamoring to become one.

## Mechanical Focus
Medical Magic uses [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] (for anatomical precision) and [[Medicine\|Medicine]] (mundane medical knowledge) together. A high [[Medicine\|Medicine]] attribute significantly boosts the effectiveness of Medical Magic spells. Without mundane anatomical knowledge, even a high-tier medical mage will cause more harm than good.
