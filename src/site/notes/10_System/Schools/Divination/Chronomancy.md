# Chronomancy

*Chronomancy is an intensely theoretical and resource-heavy discipline. While true time travel is considered impossible, practitioners can manipulate localized time dilution, temporal stasis, and attempt to reconstruct the past or predict the future.*
**Classification:** Specialization **Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]]

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Chronomancy` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

**Chronomancy CAN:**
- Accelerate, decelerate, or pause localized time for specific targets (`Schn`, `Lang`, `Wart`).
- Reconstruct past events based on the physical clues left in an area.
- Predict immediate, short-term futures (like a sword swing a split-second before it happens).

**Chronomancy CANNOT:**
- Alter the grand timeline or resurrect the dead by "rewinding" them years.
- Provide flawless, 100% accurate visions of the distant future. The universe is too chaotic, and the spell will only ever provide a "most likely" guess.

## The Core Matrix
Unlocking this knowledge grants you structural mastery of the following base Glyphs to use during *Shape the Matrix*:
**Vocabulary:** **Chronomancy**: [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Schn\|Schn]] (Fast), [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Lang\|Lang]] (Slow), [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Wart\|Wart]] (Pause)

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Senses exactly what time of day it is. (`Sehn Zeit Klen`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** Slightly accelerates the caster's perception of time. (`Sehn Schn`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** Slows down perception to see a flying arrow clearly. (`Sehn Lang`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** Briefly pauses the caster's flow of time to avoid an attack. (`Sehn Wart`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** Perfectly calculates and reacts to all timelines in a fight. (`Sehn Zeit Suta Alle`)
