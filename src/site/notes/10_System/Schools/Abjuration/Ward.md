# Ward (Prepared Boundaries)

*Ward magic transforms the temporary defenses of Abjuration into persistent, area-anchored spells. Wards envelop an entire area and enforce a particular effect on everything inside that domain. While usually protective in nature, some wards react violently when triggered. Magic is now cast by weaving geometric Glyphs into spell formulas.*
**Classification:** Specialization **Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]]

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Ward` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

No specific boundaries defined.

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Sets a tiny ward that chimes if crossed. (`Scht Wart Klen`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** Creates a basic magical boundary around a campsite. (`Scht Zone`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** A pre-prepared dome that springs up over an area when an intruder enters. (`Scht Kupp Zone Wart`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** A massive, triggered wall of force that blocks an entire corridor. (`Scht Wand Wart Gros`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** A permanent city ward that absorbs and nullifies all hostile magic cast within it. (`Scht Kres Zone Fang`)
