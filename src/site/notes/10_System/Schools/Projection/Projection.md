# Projection

**Classification:** Core School

Projection (formerly "[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Evocation\|Evocation]]") is the direct manifestation and manipulation of raw energy (light, sound, heat, physical force, electricity) into the physical world. It is a staple of the modern mage due to its extreme utility, ease of use, and role as the foundation for many other magical disciplines.

## [[50_Magic/Glossary/Specializations\|Specializations]]
As mages progress their dots in this School, they may elect to unlock highly advanced **[[50_Magic/Glossary/Specializations\|Specializations]]** by meeting secondary prerequisites. The specializations for this school are:
- **[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Evocation\|Evocation]]**
- **[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Combat Magic\|Combat Magic]]**
- **[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Negation\|Negation]]**

## Progression
Under Academy rules, acquiring a dot in a [[10_System/06 The Magic System#The Casting System\|School Proficiency]] does not instantly grant knowledge of every spell in that tier. Instead, purchasing a dot in Projection grants the mage **exactly one Spell (Power)** from this School (at that dot level or lower).

| Tier | Example Effect | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | *Luminescence* | Create a steady glow from your hand, light a candle, or deliver a static shock. |
| **Tier 2** | *Force Bolt* | A concentrated beam of force, fire, or lightning (Tier 2 combat damage). |

## Mechanical Focus
Projection is the most direct combat school. It uses [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] for raw damage output and [[10_System/Attributes/Finesse\|Finesse]] for precision targeting.

## Lore Deep Dive

Projection is the oldest and most instinctive branch of magic. When a panicked, untrained mage accidentally releases energy, it is almost always a Projection effect — a flash of light, a burst of heat, a shockwave of force. The school works by taking raw [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] from the caster's internal pool and **converting it directly into physical energy**. The caster does not manipulate existing energy — they *generate* it. 

**The Foundation of Magic:** Mastery of Projection is necessary for advancement in many other magical disciplines. The production and manipulation of energies is the foundation upon which many other spells are based. 

**The Economic Reality:** Because Projection is so foundational, quite a few mages specialize in it, and even more have a solid grasp of it despite specializing elsewhere. This makes it incredibly easy to find qualified instructors and spellbooks, but conversely, it makes it much harder to earn money purely in this field—supply simply outstrips demand.

**What it feels like to cast:** The caster feels their internal [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] draining rapidly through their hands (or a focal point), accompanied by a building pressure behind the sternum. Observers see a brief shimmer or glow at the point of emission before the energy manifests. Higher-tier effects produce visible mana distortion — the air warps like heat haze.

## Design Boundaries

When creating Projection spells, respect these rules:

**Projection CAN:**
- Generate energy from nothing: force, heat, cold, light, sound, electricity
- Create kinetic barriers (walls of compressed force)
- Accelerate or launch physical projectiles using mana-generated force
- Produce environmental effects (heat storms, pressure zones) at high tiers

**Projection CANNOT:**
- Alter the properties of existing matter (that's [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]])
- Create persistent autonomous effects (that's [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]])
- Move objects through teleportation (that's [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]])
- Create illusions or false perceptions (that's [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]])

**The key verb is GENERATE.** If the spell's primary action is creating energy that didn't exist before, it belongs in Projection.

## Combining with Specializations

- **[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Evocation\|Evocation]]:** Pure energy refinement. Evocation spells are Projection effects with extreme efficiency.
- **[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Combat Magic\|Combat Magic]]:** Speed and brutality. Combat Magic Projection spells trade efficiency for casting speed and battlefield versatility.
- **[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Negation\|Negation]]:** Counter-magic. Negation uses Projection's energy generation in reverse — creating disruptive mana fields that shred, suppress, or unravel other spells.
