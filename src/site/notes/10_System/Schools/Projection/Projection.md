# Projection (Syllabus of Generation)

**Classification:** Core School

# Lore
Projection is the brute force of the academic world. To project is to reach into the void and draw forth violent, raw energy from pure mana. It is highly volatile, favoring military tacticians and elemental researchers. Magic is now cast by weaving geometric Glyphs into spell formulas.
> **Casting Roll:** 2d6 + Willpower + [Projection]

# Lore deep dive

Projection is the oldest and most instinctive branch of magic. When a panicked, untrained mage accidentally releases energy, it is almost always a Projection effect — a flash of light, a burst of heat, a shockwave of force. The school works by taking raw mana from the caster's internal Capacity and **converting it directly into physical energy**. The caster does not manipulate existing energy — they *generate* it. 

**The Foundation of Magic:** Mastery of Projection is necessary for advancement in many other magical disciplines. The production and manipulation of energies is the foundation upon which many other spells are based. 

**What it feels like to cast:** The caster feels their internal Capacity draining rapidly through their hands (or a focal point), accompanied by a building pressure behind the sternum. Pushing past one's limit results in severe Mana Burn. Observers see a brief shimmer or glow at the point of emission before the energy manifests. Higher-tier effects produce visible mana distortion — the air warps like heat haze.

## Mechanical Focus
Projection is the most direct combat school. All casting rolls use **`2d6 + Willpower + Projection`**. 

## Design Boundaries

When creating Projection spells, respect these rules:

**Projection CAN:**
- Generate energy from nothing: force, heat, cold, light, sound, electricity
- Create kinetic barriers (walls of compressed force)
- Accelerate or launch physical projectiles using mana-generated force
- Produce environmental effects (heat storms, pressure zones) at high complexities

**Projection CANNOT:**
- Alter the properties of existing matter (that's [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]])
- Create persistent autonomous effects (that's [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]])
- Move objects through teleportation (that's [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]])
- Create illusions or false perceptions (that's [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]])

**The key verb is GENERATE (`Schf`).** If the spell's primary action is creating energy that didn't exist before, it belongs in Projection.
