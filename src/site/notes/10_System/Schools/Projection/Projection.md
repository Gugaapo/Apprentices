# Projection (Syllabus of Generation)

*Projection is the brute force of the academic world. To project is to reach into the void and draw forth violent, raw energy from pure mana. It is highly volatile, favoring military tacticians and elemental researchers. Magic is now cast by weaving geometric Glyphs into spell formulas.*
**Classification:** Core School

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Projection` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

When creating Projection spells, respect these rules:

**Projection CAN:**
- Generate energy from nothing: force, heat, cold, light, sound, electricity
- Create kinetic barriers (walls of compressed force)
- Accelerate or launch physical projectiles using mana-generated force
- Produce environmental effects (heat storms, pressure zones) at high complexities

**Projection CANNOT:**
- Alter the properties of existing matter (that's [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]])
- Create persistent autonomous effects (that's [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]])
- Move objects through teleportation (that's [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]])
- Create illusions or false perceptions (that's [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]])

**The key verb is GENERATE (`Schf`).** If the spell's primary action is creating energy that didn't exist before, it belongs in Projection.

## The Core Matrix
Unlocking this knowledge grants you structural mastery of the following base Glyphs to use during *Shape the Matrix*:
**Vocabulary:** [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Schf\|Schf]] (Create/Invoke), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Feur\|Feur]] (Fire), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Lict\|Lict]] (Light), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Erde\|Erde]] (Rock), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Kraf\|Kraf]] (Kinetic Force), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Luft\|Luft]] (Air), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Damp\|Damp]] (Steam)

> [!NOTE] Rumored Wild Glyphs
> Extreme manifestations of Projection—such as [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Nah\|Nah]] (Lightning), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Donn\|Donn]] (Thunder), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Plaz\|Plaz]] (Plasma), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Rein\|Rein]] (Pure Mana) or [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Angs\|Angs]] (Beam)—cannot be learned academically. They are Wild Glyphs that must be discovered in the world.

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Creates a harmless spark of floating light. (`Schf Lict`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** A searing blast of fire; Inflicts a Wound. (`Schf Feur`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** A lightning projectile; Inflicts a Wound. Requires Combat Magic. (`Schf Nah Schn`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** A massive fireball destroying a room; Imposes a severe Condition (Obliterated). Requires Evocation. (`Schf Feur Gros Knal`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** Summons a roaring storm of plasma wiping out a battlefield; Inflicts lethal Mass Destruction. (`Schf Plaz Wolk`)
