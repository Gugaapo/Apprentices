# Evocation (Area Control)

*Evocation is the aggressive, destructive application of the Projection discipline. It is the backbone of what is commonly referred to as "Combat Magic," focusing on the rapid generation of heat, kinetic force, and elemental energy over wide areas.*
**Classification:** Specialization **Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]]

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Evocation` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

**Evocation CAN:**
- Deal massive, instantaneous elemental or kinetic damage over wide areas (`Wolk`, `Knal`).
- Be cast incredibly quickly, making it ideal for chaotic firefights.
- Overwhelm physical and magical shielding through sheer brute force.

**Evocation CANNOT:**
- Create permanent matter (that requires Alteration or Conjuration).
- Be sustained efficiently. Evocation burns through mana reserves faster than almost any other discipline.
- Differentiate between friend and foe. An explosion hits everything in its radius.

## The Core Matrix
Unlocking this knowledge grants you structural mastery of the following base Glyphs to use during *Shape the Matrix*:
**Vocabulary:** **Evocation** : [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Wolk\|Wolk]] (Cloud/Storm), [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Knal\|Knal]] (Explosion)

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Creates a harmless, tiny glowing cloud. (`Schf Lict Wolk`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** A small concussive pop that pushes targets back. (`Schf Luft Knal`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** A fiery explosion hitting a small area. (`Schf Feur Knal`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** A massive fireball destroying a room. (`Schf Feur Gros Knal`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** Summons a roaring storm of plasma wiping out a battlefield. (`Schf Plaz Wolk`)
