# Soul Carving (Modification)

*Soul Carving is the most invasive, agonizing, and universally reviled branch of Soul Magic. It focuses on directly modifying, damaging, or enforcing permanent magical effects upon a target's soul.*
**Classification:** Specialization **Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]]

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Soul Carving` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

**Soul Carving CAN:**
- Permanently link two souls together (`Bind`).
- Inflict permanent curses, compulsions (Geas), or metaphysical damage that bypasses physical healing (`Mahl`).
- Cripple a target's ability to generate or shape mana.

**Soul Carving CANNOT:**
- Truly destroy a soul. It can only be twisted, fragmented, or scarred.
- Be healed by [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Medical Magic\|Medical Magic]]. A severed arm can be regrown; a severed soul fragment is gone forever unless reattached by another Soul Mage.
- Be practiced without Soul Sight.

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Leaves a temporary, harmless magical mark on a soul. (`Reis Mahl Klen`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** Brands a target so their mana slowly leaks away. (`Reis Mahl Rinn`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** Grafts a minor magical affinity onto the caster's soul. (`Reis Seel Bind`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** Brands an agonizing slaver's mark directly onto an enemy's soul. (`Reis Seel Mahl`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** Tears another mage's soul apart and permanently grafts their magical power onto the caster. (`Reis Seel Bind Fetz`)
