# Divine Magic

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Divine Magic` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

**Divine Magic CAN:**
- (Historically) Heal wounds flawlessly without anatomical knowledge.
- (Historically) Damage souls, banish demons, and destroy undead with extreme prejudice.

**Divine Magic CANNOT:**
- Be learned through standard Academy study. 
- Be relied upon in the modern era. Any player character utilizing "Divine Magic" is either an ancient being, using an Imperial Artifact, or secretly just a highly skilled Medical/Soul mage pretending to be a conduit of the gods.
