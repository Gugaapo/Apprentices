# Teleportation (Self)

*Teleportation is the complex art of folding space for instantaneous travel. It requires immense calculation, spatial awareness, and deeply embedded anchors to execute safely.*
**Classification:** Specialization **Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]]

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Teleportation` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

**Teleportation CAN:**
- Move the caster (and explicitly held objects) instantaneously from point A to point B (`Ich`, `Flhh`).
- Bypass physical barriers, provided the destination is known or visible.

**Teleportation CANNOT:**
- Teleport an unwilling hostile target against their will (without overwhelming their magical defense/warding).
- Be cast cheaply. It is one of the most mana-intensive disciplines in existence.
- Penetrate active Anti-Teleportation Wards, which lock down the spatial fabric of an area.

## The Core Matrix
Unlocking this knowledge grants you structural mastery of the following base Glyphs to use during *Shape the Matrix*:
**Vocabulary:** **Teleportation**: [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Flhh\|Flhh]] (Flee/Blink), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Ich\|Ich]] (Self)

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Shifts the caster's balance or footing instantly. (`Bewg Ich Klen`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** Teleports the caster a few feet straight up. (`Bewg Ich Hoch`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** Blinks the caster 30 feet away instantly. (`Bewg Ich Flhh`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** Teleports the caster across the city. (`Bewg Ich Fern`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** Teleports the caster anywhere on the continent instantly. (`Bewg Ich Alle`)
