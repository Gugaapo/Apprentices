# Mind Magic (Restricted)

*Mind Magic is the direct manipulation of a living mind — thoughts, emotions, memories, and motor control. It is the most heavily restricted and surveilled branch of magic in the Academy.*
**Classification:** Specialization **Parent School:** [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]]

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Mind Magic` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

No specific boundaries defined.

## The Core Matrix
Unlocking this knowledge grants you structural mastery of the following base Glyphs to use during *Shape the Matrix*:
**Vocabulary:** **Mind Magic**: [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Ernn\|Ernn]] (Memory), [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Werk\|Werk]] (Command/Obey)

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Plants a tiny, fleeting distraction in a target's mind. (`Tusc Geis Klen`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** Makes a target hear a whispered voice in their head. (`Tusc Geis Klan`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** Forces a target to obey a single, simple command. (`Tusc Geis Werk`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** Replaces the last 10 minutes of a guard's memory. (`Tusc Geis Ernn`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** Permanently rewrites a target's entire personality and loyalty. (`Tusc Geis Werk Gros`)
