# Illusionism (Syllabus of Deception)

*Hijacking the sensory input of reality. Illusionism doesn't change physical matter—it changes the human mind perceiving the matter, making it invaluable for espionage and psychological warfare. Magic is now cast by weaving geometric roots (Max 4 letters) into compound spell names.*
**Classification:** Core School

## Fictional Permission
When you hold `Illusionism` in your Grimoire, you have specific fictional positioning.

**Illusionism CAN:**
- Create false visual, auditory, olfactory, and tactile sensations
- Disguise the appearance of people, objects, or locations
- Generate distractions (phantom sounds, movement in peripheral vision)
- Be combined with Conjuration to create tangible holograms
- Be combined with Mind Magic to create entirely mental hallucinations

**Illusionism CANNOT:**
- Create real physical effects on its own (illusory fire does not burn objects)
- Provide real information about the world (that's [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]])
- Animate autonomous constructs (that's [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]])

**The key verb is DECEIVE (`Tusc`).** If the spell's primary action is making someone perceive something that isn't what it appears to be, it belongs in Illusionism.

## The Core Matrix
Unlocking this knowledge grants you structural mastery of the following base Glyphs to use during *Shape the Matrix*:
**Vocabulary:** [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/1_Functions/Tusc\|Tusc]] (Deceive/Phantom), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Bild\|Bild]] (Visual), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Klan\|Klan]] (Auditory), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Ruch\|Ruch]] (Smell), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Tast\|Tast]] (Touch/Texture), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Kost\|Kost]] (Taste)

> [!NOTE] Rumored Wild Glyphs
> Extreme manifestations of Illusionism—such as [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Trau\|Trau]] (Dream/Subconscious), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Pein\|Pein]] (Mental Pain), [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Dunk\|Dunk]] (Darkness), and [[10_System/Magic Compendium/Glyphs/2_Objects/Geis\|Geis]] (True Belief)—cannot be learned academically. They are Wild Glyphs that must be discovered in the world.

## Geometric Scaling (Mana Track Box Costs)
When determining the cost of a spell, the GM uses this scale:
- **Trivial (0 Ticks):** Creates the faint sound of footsteps in an empty hall. (`Tusc Klan`)
- **Regular (1 Tick):** Bends light around someone to turn them invisible; requires Glamour. (`Tusc Bild Birg`)
- **Hard (2 Ticks):** Creates phantom sensations of severe burning on a target's skin; Inflicts a Sanity Wound. (`Tusc Tast Pein`)
- **Complex (2 Ticks):** Replaces the last 10 minutes of a guard's memory; requires Mind Magic. (`Tusc Geis Ernn`)
- **Miracle (4+ Ticks):** Traps a target's consciousness entirely inside a fabricated, inescapable lucid nightmare. (`Tusc Trau Gros`)
