# Corrosive Fire Pistol

**Description:** An experimental alchemical focus that channels raw mana through a core of concentrated salamander blood. It creates a burst of fire that clings to targets like acid.

## Attributes
- **Category:** Magic Weapon
- **Tags:** *Close, Fire, Messy*

## Unique Move: Melt the Ward
When you **fire this weapon at a stationary magical barrier**, roll `2d6 + Willpower`. 
- **10+**: The barrier's matrix is dissolved; it collapses instantly.
- **7-9**: The barrier is weakened, but the pistol "overheats." You must spend **2 Mana Ticks** and the weapon is unusable for the rest of the scene.
