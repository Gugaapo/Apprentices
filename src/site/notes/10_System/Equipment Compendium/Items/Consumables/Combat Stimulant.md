# Combat Stimulant

**Effect:** Using this weapon triggers the fictional positioning to inflict an immediate Combat Wound.

A potent shot of adrenaline and alchemical pain-killers. It does not suppress penalties from [[Mana Burn\|Mana Burn]] or magical damage.
