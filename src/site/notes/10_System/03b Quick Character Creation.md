## Step 2: Assign Attributes
You have 4 categories of Attributes: Magical, Mental, Physical, and Social.
* **Base:** All 22 attributes start at **1 dot**.
* **Maximum:** No attribute can start higher than **3 dots**.
* **Points to Spend:** (1 point = +1 dot)
    * **Magical (4 Points):** Memory, Calculation, Finesse, Willpower.
    * **Mental, Physical, Social (Array):** Use the array **[9, 7, 5]**. Choose one category to receive 9 points, one to receive 7 points, and one to receive 5 points.
        * *Mental:* Academics, Investigation, Awareness, Survival, Crafting, Medicine.
        * *Physical:* Might, Mobility, Sleight, Stealth, Melee Combat, Ranged Combat.
        * *Social:* Charm, Deception, Intimidation, Insight, Society, Streetwise.

---

## Step 3: Magic & Spells
* **Base:** All 8 Core Schools start at **0 dots**.
* **Points to Spend:** **2 Points** (Max 3 dots per school. Cannot buy Specializations yet).
* **Spells Known:** Choose exactly **1 Spell per dot spent**. The spell's Tier cannot exceed your dots in that school. (Total starting spells = 2).

---

## Step 4: Merits & Flaws
* Start with **0 Points**.
* Take up to **4 Points of Flaws** to gain an equal number of points to spend on **Merits**.
* *(Note: You can use these to buy prerequisites for restricted magic like Soul Magic).*

---

## Step 5: Academy Logistics
* Pick **1 Mentor Professor** (for afternoon time slots).
* Pick **2 Majors** (initial classes).

---

## Step 6: Calculate Derived Stats
* **Mana Capacity:** `(Willpower + Calculation) × 5`
* **Physical Health:** 7 Boxes (Bruised 0 to Incapacitated)
* **Mind Defense:** `Willpower + Highest of (Divination or Abjuration)`
* **Soul Integrity:** 10 Boxes
* **Sanity:** 10 Boxes
* **Ongoing Spell Limit:** Equal to your `Calculation` rating
* **Mastered Spells:** Choose a number of your known spells up to `Memory × 2` as Mastered. (They cost 1 less Mana, are cast silently/instantly, and cost 0 SP to master at creation).
