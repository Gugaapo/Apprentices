## Step 2: Assign Attributes
You have 4 categories of Attributes: Magical, Mental, Physical, and Social. These directly add to your 2d6 rolls.
* **Base:** Magical attributes start at **1 dot**. Mental, Social, and Physical attributes start at **0 dots**.
* **Maximum:** No attribute can start higher than **3 dots**.
* **Points to Spend:** (1 point = +1 dot)
    * **Magical (4 Points):** Memory, Calculation, Finesse, Willpower.
    * **Mental, Physical, Social (Array):** Use the array **[5, 4, 3]**. Choose one category to receive 5 points, one to receive 4 points, and one to receive 3 points.
        * *Mental:* Academics, Awareness, Survival, Crafting.
        * *Physical:* Might, Mobility, Dexterity, Combat.
        * *Social:* Charm, Deception, Insight, Connections.

---

## Step 3: Magic & Spells
* **Base:** All 8 Core Schools start at **0 dots**.
* **Points to Spend:** **2 Points** (Max 3 dots per school. Cannot buy Specializations yet).
* **Glyph Syllabuses:** Learning a school grants you access to its specific Function, Object, and Characteristic Glyphs (e.g., *Tvor*, *Vatr*).
* **Spells Known:** Choose exactly **1 Spell Formula** per dot spent. The spell's Complexity cannot exceed your dots in that school. (Total starting spells = 2).

---

## Step 4: Merits & Flaws
* Start with **0 Points**.
* Take up to **4 Points of Flaws** to gain an equal number of points to spend on **Merits**.
* *(Note: You can use these to buy prerequisites for restricted magic like Soul Magic).*

---

## Step 5: Academy Logistics
* Pick **1 Mentor Professor** (for afternoon time slots).
* Pick **2 Majors** (initial classes).

---

## Step 6: Calculate Derived Stats
* **Mana Capacity:** `4 + [[Core Resonance]]`. (Going over this limit causes Mana Burn/Exhaustion Cascade).
* **Physical Health:** 7 Boxes (Bruised 0 to Incapacitated).
* **Mind Defense (Passive TN):** `6 + Willpower + Highest of (Divination or Abjuration)`.
* **[[10_System/Glossary/Physical Defense\|Physical Defense]] (Evasion):** `6 + Mobility + Highest of (Awareness or Dexterity)`.
* **Soul Integrity:** 10 Boxes.
* **Sanity:** 10 Boxes.
* **Max Modifiers:** `Calculation - 1`.
* **Mastered Spells:** You may designate a number of known formulas up to your `Memory` rating as Mastered. They cost 0 SP at creation, permanently lower their Complexity Category by 1 step for you (reducing Mana cost), and skip calculation rolls.
