## Step 2: Assign Attributes
You have 4 categories of Attributes: Magical, Mental, Physical, and Social. These directly add to your 2d6 rolls.
* **Base:** Magical attributes start at **1 dot**. Mental, Social, and Physical attributes start at **0 dots**.
* **Maximum:** No attribute can start higher than **3 dots**.
* **Points to Spend:** (1 point = +1 dot)
    * **Magical:** You have **2 Points** to distribute among [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Memory\|Memory]], [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]], [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Finesse\|Finesse]], and [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Willpower\|Willpower]].
    * **Mundane:** You have **2 Points** to distribute among [[10_System/Attributes/Mundane/Mental\|Mental]], [[10_System/Attributes/Mundane/Social\|Social]], and [[10_System/Attributes/Mundane/Physical\|Physical]].

---

## Step 3: Magic & Spells
* **Base:** All 8 Core Schools start at **0 dots**.
* **Points to Spend:** **2 Points** (Max 3 dots per school. Cannot buy Specializations yet).
* **Glyph Syllabuses:** Learning a school grants you access to its specific Function, Object, and Characteristic Glyphs.
* **Spells Known:** Choose exactly **1 Spell Formula** per dot spent to add to your Grimoire. The spell's Complexity cannot exceed your dots in that school.

---

## Step 4: Merits & Flaws
* Start with **0 Points**.
* Take up to **4 Points of Flaws** to gain an equal number of points to spend on **Merits**.
* *(Note: You can use these to buy prerequisites for restricted magic like Soul Magic).*

---

## Step 5: Academy Logistics
* Pick **1 Mentor Professor** (for afternoon time slots).
* Pick **2 Majors** (initial classes).

---

## Step 6: Calculate Derived Stats
* **Max Health:** 7 Boxes (Bruised through Incapacitated).
* **Mind Defense (Passive TN):** `6 + Willpower + Highest of (Divination or Abjuration)`.
* **Soul Integrity:** 10 Boxes.
* **Sanity:** 10 Boxes.
* **Max Modifiers:** `Calculation`.
* **Mastered Spells:** You may designate a number of known formulas up to your `Memory` rating as "Mastered." You can have many spells annotated in your Grimoire, but Mastered spells represent the subset you hold perfectly in your mind. You can swap which spells are Mastered during any period of rest outside of combat or tense roleplay scenes. While Mastered, formulas lower their Complexity Category by 1 step for you (reducing Mana cost), and skip calculation rolls.
