# Raw Intuition

*A Playbook Move for [[00_Meta/Playbooks/The Prodigy\|The Prodigy]]*

When you cast Unstructured Magic on the fly, roll `+Finesse` instead of Willpower. 
- **10+:** You completely bypass the mandatory +1 Tick Tax and do not take a Wound. 
- **7-9:** You take the tax, but the spell is wildly devastating.
- **6-:** The GM makes a Hard Move.
