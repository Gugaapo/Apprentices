# Perceive & Investigate

*A Basic Move. Available to all Students.*

When you **closely study a person, place, or situation**, roll `+Mental`.
- **10+:** You gain a clear insight. Ask the GM 2 questions from the list below. You take +1 forward when acting on the answers.
- **7-9:** Ask the GM 1 question from the list.
- **6-:** You deduce incorrectly, or your investigation draws unwanted attention.

*Questions:*
- What here is not what it appears to be?
- What happened here recently?
- What is the greatest danger or opportunity?
- Who is truly in control here?
