# Defend

*A Basic Move. Available to all Students.*

When you **put yourself in danger to protect yourself or an ally from an incoming attack**, roll `+Physical` or `+Mental` (if tactical).
- **10+:** You successfully mitigate the danger. Halve the incoming damage or completely avoid a condition.
- **7-9:** You mitigate the danger, but choose one:
  - You take the brunt of the attack instead of your ally.
  - You lose your footing or positioning.
  - You drop an item.
