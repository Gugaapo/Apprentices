# Force Your Way

*A Basic Move. Available to all Students.*

When you **use raw physical power to smash, lift, grapple, or overcome an obstacle**, roll `+Physical`.
- **10+:** You do it, and you're in a dominant position or break it cleanly.
- **7-9:** You do it, but choose one:
  - You break something else in the process (collateral damage).
  - You take a physical Wound from the strain or counterattack.
  - You leave yourself exposed.
