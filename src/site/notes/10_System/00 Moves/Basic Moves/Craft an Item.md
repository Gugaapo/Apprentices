# Craft an Item

*A Basic Move. Available to all Students.*

When you **spend 1 Time Slot and basic materials to forge, build, or tinker with a mundane item**, state what you are trying to make and roll.
*(Roll `2d6 + Mental`)*

- **10+ (Strong Hit):** You work efficiently. Gain **2 Progress points** toward the project's Complexity.
- **7-9 (Mixed Success):** The work is hard going. Gain **1 Progress point**, but the GM will offer you a complication (e.g., you consume extra materials, the project is fragile, or it draws unwanted attention).
- **6- (Miss):** Fails to advance. The Time Slot is spent, the materials may be wasted, and the GM makes a Hard Move.
