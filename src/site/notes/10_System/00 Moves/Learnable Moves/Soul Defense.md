# Soul Defense

*A modular Playbook Move. Can be learned to actively resist spiritual damage.*

When your **Soul Cohesion is directly targeted by [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] or terrifying unearthly entities**, roll `+Willpower` or `+Finesse`.
- **10+:** Your soul remains completely intact. The attack glances off your spiritual boundary, and you immediately identify the exact nature or source of the attack.
- **7-9:** The attack is deflected, but your internal channels are bruised from the friction. Choose one:
  - You lose 1 Tick Capacity until you take a full rest.
  - You take 1 Physical Wound from the internal strain.
  - You drop an active spell or ward immediately.
- **6-:** The soul-scarring takes root. Mark 1 box of Soul Integrity and the GM makes a Hard Move.
