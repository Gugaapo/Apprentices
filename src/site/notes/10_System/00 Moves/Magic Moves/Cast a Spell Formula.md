# Cast a Spell Formula

*A Magic Move. Available to all Students.*

When you **channel mana through a structured magical formula you have learned or Mastered**, calculate your **Mana Spent** and roll.
*(Roll `2d6 + Finesse` for precision/defensive spells, or `2d6 + Willpower` for destructive/raw energy).*

> **Mana Spent Calculation Formula:**
> Complexity Level Cost + [[10_System/05 Magic System#APPENDIX Quick Casting Card\|System Taxes]]
> *If holding sustained Wards, those dynamically lock away Max Capacity instead of spending it.*
{ #tni75z}


- **10+ (Strong Hit):** The spell manifests perfectly as intended. Deal damage or apply the effect. You may choose **two** Overcharge options:
  - You maximize the effect or deal +1 damage.
  - You perfectly contain the spell; reduce the Mana Spent by 1 (minimum 1).
  - You duplicate the effect to a secondary target.
  - You apply a lingering status effect (e.g., Burning, Blinded).
- **7-9 (Mixed Success):** The spell succeeds, but the structure was erratic. Choose one complication:
  - **Mana Friction:** The spell costs 1 additional Mana to stabilize.
  - **Collateral:** The spell affects the environment or allies in an unintended way.
  - **Opening:** You leave yourself vulnerable to an enemy's immediate retaliation.
- **6- (Miss):** The spell collapses or backfires wildly. You spend the Mana, the spell fails, and the GM makes a Hard Move.
