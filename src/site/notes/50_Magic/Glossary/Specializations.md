# Specializations

**Specializations** represent a mage's deep, almost obsessive focus within a specific Core School of magic. 

While any competent mage can cast basic [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Evocation\|Evocation]] spells, a mage who takes the [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Evocation\|Evocation]] Specialization has fundamentally altered their spiritual matrix to accommodate that specific type of [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] shaping. 

**Mechanics:**
- Specializations cannot be purchased during character creation (unless strictly permitted by the GM).
- A character must reach **3 dots** in a Core School to unlock the ability to purchase a child Specialization.
- Having a Specialization grants a permanent **+1 die** to all spellcasting rolls made using that specific sub-school.
