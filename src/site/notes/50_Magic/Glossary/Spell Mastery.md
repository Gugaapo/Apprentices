# Spell Mastery (Muscle Memory)

**Spell Mastery** represents a level of fluency where a mage has cast a specific spell so many times that they no longer need to consciously think about calculating its mana canals. 

In lore, it is exactly like how an adult human doesn't need to consciously think about the mechanics of placing one foot in front of the other to walk. A mage who has mastered a spell shapes the complex magical architecture purely through muscle memory and instinct.

Mechanically, a character can master a maximum number of spells equal to their **Memory / 2**. When casting a Mastered Spell, the mage gains a **+1 die** bonus to their casting pool, reflecting this effortless execution and bypassing the normal calculation limits.
