# The Breach (Mind Magic)

**The Breach** refers to the terrifying event when an Illusionist or Mind Mage successfully overcomes a target's passive Mind Defense. 

When a Breach occurs, the attacker gains access to the target's psyche. The main mechanical and narrative consequences are severe:
*   **Memory Alteration:** The invading mage can change, erase, or implant bits of memory.
*   **Personality Rewrite:** If not stopped soon enough, the attacker can fundamentally alter the victim's personality, effectively turning them into another person entirely.
*   **Mind-Rape / Hollowing:** A malicious attacker can violently assault the mind, instantly shattering the victim's Sanity and turning them into a **[[50_Magic/Glossary/Hollow\|Hollow]]**—a completely broken, crazy shell of their former self.
