# Soul Sigil

The **Soul Sigil** (also known as the Spiritual Matrix) is a metaphysical representation of a person's soul and their capacity for magic. It dictates their [[50_Magic/Glossary/Mana Capacity\|Mana Capacity]], their [[50_Magic/Glossary/Sanity\|Sanity]], and their [[50_Magic/Glossary/Soul Integrity\|Soul Integrity]].
