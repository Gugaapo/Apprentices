**Mana Capacity** represents the maximum amount of magical energy a character's soul can safely hold and utilize without resting or drawing on external batteries like [[60_Items/Materials/Crystalized Mana\|Crystalized Mana]].

**[[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]]:** `(Willpower + Calculation) x 5`

This pool is vital for spellcasting, as powerful spells can drain these reserves rapidly.
