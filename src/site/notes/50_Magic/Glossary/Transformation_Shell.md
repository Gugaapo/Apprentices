# Transformation Shell

A Transformation Shell (sometimes called "soul clothes") is a sophisticated mana structure required for advanced [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Transformation\|Transformation Magic]].

## Function
Instead of simply changing the physical body (which is dangerous and prone to failure), a mage constructs a "shell" around their soul. This shell acts as a template or a set of "clothes" that filters the mage's magical output through a new biological or spiritual pattern.

## Safety and Persistence
The shell prevents the mage's soul from being permanently warped by the transformation. It also allows for more stable and lingering transformations, such as the master-level **[[True Shapeshifting\|True Shapeshifting]]** used by high-tier mages and certain magical creatures.

## Mastery
Creating a stable, resilient Transformation Shell requires extreme precision in [[50_Magic/Glossary/Shaping\|Shaping]] and a deep understanding of biological templates.
