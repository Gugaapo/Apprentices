# Archmage

An **Archmage** is an unofficial title reserved for mages who have achieved mastery in **multiple different fields of magic** to such a degree that they can rival or surpass specialists in each of those fields individually.

## What Sets an Archmage Apart

Most mages dedicate their entire careers to mastering a single school of magic, and even then, true mastery takes decades. An Archmage is someone who has done this *several times over*. They don't just dabble in multiple schools; they command each one with the depth and precision of a lifelong specialist.

This level of versatility is extraordinarily rare. It requires not only immense natural talent and an unusually large [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] pool, but also an innate ability to sense even minuscule amounts of ambient mana and detect subtle changes in their surroundings. This heightened [[50_Magic/Glossary/Shaping\|mana sense]] is considered one of the defining traits of a potential Archmage, as it provides the intuitive foundation needed to excel across fundamentally different magical disciplines.

## Reputation

The title of Archmage carries enormous weight. In a world where even a competent specialist mage is considered dangerous, an Archmage represents a threat on a completely different scale. They can adapt to virtually any combat situation, counter almost any spell thrown at them, and exploit weaknesses across multiple magical domains simultaneously.

There is no formal certification or institution that grants the title. It is earned purely through reputation: when other mages, even accomplished ones, acknowledge that someone has transcended the boundaries of specialization, that person is called an Archmage.
