# Mana Sensitivity

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 1 points

## Description
Your soul is acutely attuned to the ambient mana in the environment. You can instinctively feel the ebb and flow of magical energies around you, making it easier to parse the nature of spells and enchantments.

**Mechanic:** You gain **+1 die** to [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Divination Class\|Divination Class]] or [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] rolls when specifically attempting to detect or analyze active magic, magical traps, or lingering arcane signatures.
