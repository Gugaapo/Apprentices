# Fast Caster

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 2 points

## Description
You have drilled the basic forms and mathematical calculations of spellcasting into your muscle memory. When seconds count, you can bypass the standard structural requirements of a spell formula without sacrificing the spell's stability.

**Mechanic:** Once per combat encounter, you may ignore the standard **+2 success penalty** typically applied when attempting to "Snap cast" (casting a spell instantly without proper preparation time/gestures).
