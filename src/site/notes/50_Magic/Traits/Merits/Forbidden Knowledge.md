**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 3 points

## Description
You have delved into the "Vile Arts," studying texts and concepts strictly outlawed by the Crown and the [[40_People/Factions/Triumvirate Church\|Triumvirate Church]]. You understand the fundamental architecture of souls, blood, and death.

**Requirement:** This trait is mandatory for learning [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] or [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Blood Magic\|Blood Magic]].

**Cost:** Acquiring this trait fundamentally stains your worldview; it permanently costs **1 point of Sanity** upon acquisition.

**Risk:** Utilizing high-tier [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Necromancy\|Necromancy]] or Soul Magic marks you. If discovered by the authorities, you are targeted for "True Death"—execution and soul-destruction
