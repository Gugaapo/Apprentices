# Eidetic Memory

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 2 points

## Description
Your mind is a steel trap, capable of recalling visual, auditory, and textual information with perfect clarity. In a time loop where knowledge is the only currency that persists, your mind is your greatest asset. It also helps ground your sense of reality against the fraying effects of the loop.

**Mechanic:** You gain **+2 dice** to [[10_System/Attributes/Memory\|Memory]] checks, specifically including rolls made to resist Sanity Erosion at the end of a loop or after experiencing trauma.
