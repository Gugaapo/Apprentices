**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 2 points

## Description
Your mind is a steel trap, capable of recalling visual, auditory, and textual information with perfect clarity. 

**Mechanic:** You gain **+2 dice** to [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Memory\|Memory]] checks, specifically including rolls made to resist Sanity Erosion or after experiencing trauma.
