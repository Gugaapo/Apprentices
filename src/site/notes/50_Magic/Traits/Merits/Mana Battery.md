# Mana Battery

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 3 points

## Description
Your internal mana reserves are unnaturally vast (similar to Zach Noveda). While other mages carefully manage their energy to avoid exhaustion, you possess a deep, roaring ocean of magical power.

**Mechanic:** You gain **+10** to your maximum Mana Capacity. This allows you to cast significantly more spells before risking Mana Burn or exhaustion.
