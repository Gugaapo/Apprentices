# Soul Anchor

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 3 points

## Description
Your soul is unusually dense or well-tethered to your physical vessel. Attacks that would normally shear away your very essence meet a wall of metaphysical resistance.

**Mechanic:** Once per adventure, you may completely ignore **1 point of Soul Damage** that you would have otherwise taken from forbidden magic, eldritch entities, or severe magical backlash.
