# Iron Will

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 3 points

## Description
Your mind is a fortress. Whether through natural stubbornness, intense meditation, or childhood trauma, you possess an innate resistance to mental intrusion, telepathy, and emotional manipulation.

**Mechanic:** You gain a permanent **+2 bonus** to your passive Mind Defense. This applies against all attempts to read your surface thoughts, alter your memories, or magically influence your emotions.
