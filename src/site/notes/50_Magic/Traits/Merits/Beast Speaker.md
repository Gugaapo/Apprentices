# Beast Speaker

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 1 points

## Description
You have a natural, almost empathic connection with mundane animals and minor magical beasts. You understand their body language perfectly and project an aura of calm that puts them at ease.

**Mechanic:** You gain **+2 dice** to [[10_System/Attributes/Mental/Survival\|Survival]] or [[10_System/Attributes/Social/Charm\|Charm]] rolls when dealing with, calming, taming, or interpreting the behavior of non-sapient beasts.
