# Soul Sight

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 4 points
**Note:** Mandatory Prerequisite for Soul Magic

## Description
You have undergone the agonizing, highly illegal, and often maddening ritual required to open your "third eye" and perceive the spiritual plane. You can see the invisible architecture of life and death.

**Mechanic:** You can passively see ghosts, spirits, and the souls of living beings as glowing, metaphysical constructs. This merit is the **mandatory prerequisite** for learning the *Soul Magic*, *Spirit Calling*, *Necromancy*, or *Soul Carving* disciplines. Without this merit, those magical paths are completely locked to you.
