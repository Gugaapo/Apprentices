**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 2 points

## Description
You have an innate talent for manipulating mana into structured forms without the steep learning curve most students face. A specific field of magic comes as naturally to you as breathing.

**Mechanic:** Choose one core Magic School (e.g., [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Projection Class\|Projection Class]], [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Alteration Class\|Alteration Class]]). When casting Base Effects within that chosen School, the required number of successes is reduced by 1 (minimum of 1 success required).
