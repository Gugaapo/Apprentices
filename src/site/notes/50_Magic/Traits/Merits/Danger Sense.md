# Danger Sense

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 3 points

## Description
Your magical instincts scream when violence is imminent. You possess an uncanny, almost precognitive ability to sense incoming attacks, hidden traps, or malicious intent directed at you.

**Mechanic:** You cannot be surprised in combat. Even if completely ambushed, you always get to act in the first round of combat. Additionally, you gain +1 die to [[10_System/Attributes/Awareness\|Awareness]] rolls specifically made to detect traps or hidden enemies.
