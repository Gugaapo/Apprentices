# Wealthy Patron

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 2 points

## Description
Someone rich is funding your education. Whether it's a doting noble parent, a secretive guild, or a mysterious benefactor, you do not have to worry about the day-to-day costs of living in Cyoria.

**Mechanic:** You receive a substantial cash stipend at the start of every month (or at the beginning of every Time Loop Reset). This removes the need to track minor expenses and gives you a pool of funds to purchase common alchemical ingredients, standard equipment, or bribes without rolling.
