# Efficiency Matrix

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 2 points

## Description
Your internal mana pathways are exquisitely optimized. When you push your body past its limits to force a spell into existence, the physical toll is significantly mitigated by your incredibly efficient mana circulation.

**Mechanic:** When utilizing [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Burn\|Mana Burn]] to force a spell to cast, you spend **1 less Physical Health** for the required successes (to a minimum of 0 point of health spent). For instance, generating 1 fake success usually costs 1 HP; for you, the first fake success costs 0 HP.
