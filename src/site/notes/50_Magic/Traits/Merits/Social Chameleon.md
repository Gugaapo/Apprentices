**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 1 points

## Description
You possess a frighteningly adaptable personality, able to seamlessly blend into different social strata and feign normalcy even when your mind is buckling under the weight of accumulated trauma. 

**Mechanic:** You do not suffer the standard **-1 penalty** to social rolls usually incurred when you reach the "Detachment" stage of mental degradation (Sanity 7).
