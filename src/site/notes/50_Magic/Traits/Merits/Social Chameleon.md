# Social Chameleon

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 1 points

## Description
You possess a frighteningly adaptable personality, able to seamlessly blend into different social strata and feign normalcy even when your mind is buckling under the weight of accumulated trauma. The loop's isolating detachment doesn't cripple your ability to relate to 'ephemerals' (non-loopers).

**Mechanic:** You do not suffer the standard **-1 penalty** to social rolls usually incurred when you reach the "Detachment" stage of mental degradation (Sanity 7).
