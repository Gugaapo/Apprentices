**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 2 points

## Description
Through rigorous meditation, profound philosophical understanding, or sheer unbothered temperament, your soul and mind are exceptionally stable.

**Mechanic:** At the start of each adventure, you begin with **+2 bonus points** to either your Sanity or your [[10_System/Glossary/Soul Integrity\|Soul Integrity]]. You must choose which pool receives this bonus at the start of the adventure.
