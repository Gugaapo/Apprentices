# Inner Peace

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 2 points

## Description
Through rigorous meditation, profound philosophical understanding, or sheer unbothered temperament, your soul and mind are exceptionally stable. The existential dread of the time loop and the horrors of the arcane world slide off you more easily than others.

**Mechanic:** At the start of each adventure (each loop or scenario), you begin with **+2 bonus points** to either your Sanity or your [[50_Magic/Glossary/Soul Integrity\|Soul Integrity]]. You must choose which pool receives this bonus at the start of the adventure.
