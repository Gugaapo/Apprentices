# Academic Prodigy

**Category:** Merits
**Cost/Value:** 1 points

## Description
You are a natural scholar. Complex spell formulas, historical dates, and alchemical ratios make intuitive sense to you. You thrive in the classroom and the library.

**Mechanic:** You gain **+2 dice** to [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] rolls whenever performing theoretical tasks, researching arcane theory, or solving mathematical problems, as opposed to practical field-casting.
