# Physical Frailty

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -2 points

## Description
Whether due to chronic illness, a sedentary lifestyle, or genetic misfortune, your physical body is remarkably weak and prone to injury. You cannot take nearly as much punishment as a healthy young adult.

**Mechanic:** Your maximum Physical Health track starts at **5 boxes** instead of the standard 7. You do not have the top two boxes ("Bruised [0]" and "Hurt [-1]"). You begin taking severe Wound Penalties much faster than a healthy adult.
