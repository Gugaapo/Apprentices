# Social Outcast

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -1 points

## Description
Whether due to a terrible reputation, off-putting mannerisms, or a fundamental inability to read social cues, you are treated as a pariah by your peers and authority figures alike. People instinctively distrust or dislike you.

**Mechanic:** You suffer a permanent **-1 penalty** to all social rolls (e.g., [[10_System/Attributes/Social/Charm\|Charm]], [[10_System/Attributes/Social/Deception\|Deception]], [[90_AI_Context/SOCIETY\|90_AI_Context/SOCIETY]]). This penalty stacks with any penalties incurred from low Sanity.
