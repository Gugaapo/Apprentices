# Academic Struggle

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -1 points

## Description
The rigid, highly theoretical, and intensely mathematical curriculum of the Academy is your worst nightmare. While you might be a capable practical caster, the rote memorization of complex formulas eludes you.

**Mechanic:** You suffer a **-2 dice penalty** to [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] or [[10_System/Attributes/Academics\|Academics]] for theoretical exams, academic tests, or abstract magical theory.
