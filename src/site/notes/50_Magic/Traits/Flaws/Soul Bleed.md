# Soul Bleed

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -3 points

## Description
Your soul is weakly bound to your physical form, or perhaps inherently brittle. When your spiritual essence is attacked, the damage spirals rapidly out of control, causing cascading structural failure to your soul.

**Mechanic:** Whenever you take Soul Damage from any source, you take **+1 extra point** of Soul Damage.
