# Severe Phobia

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -1 or -2 points

## Description
You have a deep, irrational, and paralyzing terror of a specific object, creature, or situation. This isn't just a dislike; it is a full-blown panic response. Common phobias in Cyoria might include spiders (Aranea), enclosed Dungeon spaces, heights, or the undead.

**Mechanic:** You suffer a **-2 dice penalty** to all actions when directly confronted with the object of your phobia. 
*   **-1 Point Value:** The phobia is relatively uncommon and easily avoided (e.g., deep water, specific rare monsters).
*   **-2 Point Value:** The phobia is common and hard to avoid in a typical campaign (e.g., spiders, dark enclosed spaces, blood).
