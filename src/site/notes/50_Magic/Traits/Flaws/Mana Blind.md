# Mana Blind

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -1 points

## Description
Most mages can feel a faint "buzz" or pressure when in the presence of strong magic or active spells. You lack this basic sensory organ entirely. Magic is invisible and intangible to you until its effects are physically manifested.

**Mechanic:** You cannot detect the presence of magic, active spells, or wards using passive perception. You must cast a specific [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Divination Class\|Divination Class]] spell to perceive magical auras.
