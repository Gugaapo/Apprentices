# Weak Will

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -2 points

## Description
Your sense of self and mental boundaries are porous. You find it difficult to stand your ground against psychic intrusion, domination, or forceful personalities attempting to override your commands.

**Mechanic:** You suffer a **-2 penalty** to your passive [[50_Magic/Glossary/Mind Defense\|Mind Defense]], making you highly vulnerable to [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Mind Magic\|Mind Magic]] and psychic attacks.
