# Open Book

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -2 points

## Description
Your mind is a chaotic, noisy place. You have absolutely no natural mental shielding, and your surface thoughts and intense emotions broadcast outward like a beacon to anyone with basic telepathic sensitivity.

**Mechanic:** You suffer a **-2 penalty** to your Mind Defense specifically against attempts to read your surface thoughts, sense your emotions, or pinpoint your location via Mind Sense. (This does not penalize your defense against deep mental domination or memory editing).
