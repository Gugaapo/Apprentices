# Magical Static

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -1 points

## Description
Your mana signature is inherently "loud" and chaotic. When you cast a spell, it creates a disruptive, noticeable ripple in the ambient mana field, making it incredibly difficult for you to cast magic discreetly.

**Mechanic:** When attempting to cast a spell Silently or discreetly, the required number of successes increases by **+1**.
