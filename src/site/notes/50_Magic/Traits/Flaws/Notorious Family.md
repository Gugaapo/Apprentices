# Notorious Family

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -1 points

## Description
Your family name carries a heavy burden. Whether your parents were convicted of practicing forbidden magic, your House committed treason during the Splinter Wars, or your family simply owes money to the wrong people, your surname opens the wrong doors.

**Mechanic:** You suffer a **-1 die penalty** to [[10_System/Attributes/Charm\|Charm]] and [[90_AI_Context/SOCIETY\|Society]] rolls when dealing with law-abiding citizens, pureblood nobles, or Academy staff who recognize your name.
