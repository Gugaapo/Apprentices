# Arcane Echoes

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -2 points

## Description
Your mind retains "sticky" debris from spells you have recently cast. The complex mathematical matrices of previous spells continue to echo in your head, distracting you and making it difficult to formulate new spells quickly.

**Mechanic:** Under pressure or during prolonged casting sequences (like a long combat encounter), the GM may impose an occasional **-1 penalty** to Shaping rolls due to distracting magical echoes.
