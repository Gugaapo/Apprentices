# Magical Signature Anomaly

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -1 points

## Description
Every mage leaves behind a faint "fingerprint" of mana when they cast a spell, but yours is incredibly loud, distinct, and sticky. It smells like ozone, glows a specific color, or just feels uniquely *wrong* to other mages.

**Mechanic:** Your magical signature lingers in the environment for days instead of hours. Warders, Diviners, and investigators gain **+2 dice** to any rolls made to track you, identify your spells, or link a magical crime back to you.
