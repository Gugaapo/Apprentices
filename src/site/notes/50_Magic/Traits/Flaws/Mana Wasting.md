# Mana Wasting

**Category:** Flaws
**Cost/Value:** -3 points

## Description
You have terrible mana control. Your internal shaping is sloppy, and your spells bleed excess energy into the environment as light, heat, or noise. While your spells still work, they are incredibly inefficient.

**Mechanic:** All spells you cast cost **+1 Mana** (this completely cancels out the -1 Mana discount you would normally receive from Mastered Spells). Additionally, your spells are much louder and brighter than normal, making stealth-casting nearly impossible.
