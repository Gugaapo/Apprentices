### Astral Projection
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 4)*
**Mana Cost:** 4

*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 4)*
