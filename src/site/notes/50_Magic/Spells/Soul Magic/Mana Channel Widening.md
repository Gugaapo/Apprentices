### Mana Channel Widening
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Carving\|Soul Carving]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 4

The mage carefully widens a mana pathway in the target's soul, improving mana flow.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] vs difficulty 4.
**On success:** Carefully widens a mana pathway in the target's soul, improving mana flow.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
