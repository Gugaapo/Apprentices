### Soul Shield
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

The mage wraps their soul in a protective barrier, reducing vulnerability to soul-damaging effects.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 2 successes.
**On success:** Wraps their soul in a protective barrier, reducing vulnerability to soul-damaging effects.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or Potency (+1 defense against soul attacks).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
