### Custom Soul Ward
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 3)*

*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Carving\|Soul Carving]] (Tier 3)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] 3*

The mage carves a defensive pattern directly into a target's soul, protecting against specific magical attacks.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] vs difficulty 6.
