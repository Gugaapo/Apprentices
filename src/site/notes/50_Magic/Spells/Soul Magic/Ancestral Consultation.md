### Ancestral Consultation
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Spirit Calling\|Spirit Calling]] (Tier 2)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] 2*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage contacts the spirit of a deceased ancestor and asks for guidance.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] vs difficulty 5.
**On success:** Contacts the spirit of a deceased ancestor and asks for guidance.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
