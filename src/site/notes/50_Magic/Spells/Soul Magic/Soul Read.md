### Soul Read
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Carving\|Soul Carving]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage reads the basic spiritual configuration of a target's soul, learning their innate affinities.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Reads the basic spiritual configuration of a target's soul, learning their innate affinities.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or Potency (+1 piece of information).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
