### Manifest Ghost
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] 3*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage provides enough raw [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] to a summoned spirit that it gains physical density. The resulting specter is horrifying to behold, capable of phasing through walls and physically attacking enemies with freezing, necrotic touches.
