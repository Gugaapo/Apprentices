### Call of the Dead
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 5)*
**Mana Cost:** 5

*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Spirit Calling\|Spirit Calling]] (Tier 5)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] 5*
**Difficulty:** 8

The mage summons every spirit in a vast area, creating a host of the dead that follows their command.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] vs difficulty 8.
**On success:** Summons every spirit in a vast area, creating a host of the dead that follows their command.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
