### Soul Mark
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

The mage inscribes a minor mark on a target's soul, allowing them to be identified and tracked.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] vs difficulty 4.
**On success:** Inscribes a minor mark on a target's soul, allowing them to be identified and tracked.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
