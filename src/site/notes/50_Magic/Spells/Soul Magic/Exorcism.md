### Exorcism
*[[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

**Difficulty:** 6

The mage forcibly ejects a possessing entity from a host body.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] vs difficulty 6.
**On success:** Forcibly ejects a possessing entity from a host body.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
