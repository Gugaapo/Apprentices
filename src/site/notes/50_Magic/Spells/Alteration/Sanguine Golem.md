### Sanguine Golem
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Alteration Class\|Alteration Class]] (Tier 4)*
**Mana Cost:** 4

The mage shapes a massive quantity of blood into a semi-autonomous construct that fights on their behalf.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 4 successes.
**On success:** Shapes a massive quantity of blood into a semi-autonomous construct that fights on their behalf.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or Potency (+1 to construct stats).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
