### Elongated Reach
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Alteration Class\|Alteration Class]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

The mage temporarily extends the length of their arms by a few centimetres, increasing their reach.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Temporarily extends the length of their arms by a few centimetres, increasing their reach.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration) or Potency (+1 to reach).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
