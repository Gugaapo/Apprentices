### Minor Cosmetic Shift
*[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Transformation\|Transformation]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage subtly alters their own facial features — changing eye colour, adjusting bone structure, or modifying hair texture.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Subtly alters their own facial features — changing eye colour, adjusting bone structure, or modifying hair text...
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration) or Potency (+1 effect/disguise).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
