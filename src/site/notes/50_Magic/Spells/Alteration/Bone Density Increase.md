### Bone Density Increase
*[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

*[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Transformation\|Transformation]] (Tier 2)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] 2*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage increases the density of their skeletal structure, making their bones nearly unbreakable.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 2 successes.
**On success:** Increases the density of their skeletal structure, making their bones nearly unbreakable.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration) or Potency (+1 defense).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
