### Chameleon Skin
*[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage superficially alters the pigmentation of their skin, hair, and clothing to perfectly match the colors and textures of the surface behind them. They blend seamlessly into stone walls, wooden paneling, or leafy underbrush.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Superficially alters the pigmentation of their skin, hair, and clothing to perfectly match the colors and textures of the surface behind them. They blend seamlessly...
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or Potency (+1 to stealth).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
