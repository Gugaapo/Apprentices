### Blood Bond
*[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] (Tier 4)*
**Mana Cost:** 4

*[[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Blood Magic\|Blood Magic]] (Tier 4)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] 4*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage creates a deep sympathetic link between two creatures. Damage dealt to one is shared by the other.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Alteration\|Alteration]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 4 successes.
**On success:** Creates a deep sympathetic link between two creatures. Damage dealt to one is shared by the other.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration) or Potency (+1 effect).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
