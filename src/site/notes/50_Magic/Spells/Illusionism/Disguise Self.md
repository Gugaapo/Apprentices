### Disguise Self
*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Glamour\|Glamour]] (Tier 2)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] 2*

The mage generates a complete, hollow optical illusion over their own body, projecting the exact visual appearance, voice, and smell of another humanoid. Only physical touch will reveal that the disguise is a hard-light hologram.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 2 successes.
**On success:** Generates a complete, hollow optical illusion over their own body, projecting the exact visual appearance, voice, and smell of another humanoid. Only physical touch...
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
