### Phantom Footsteps
*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

**Difficulty:** 6

The mage creates the sound of approaching footsteps from a specific direction.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Creates the sound of approaching footsteps from a specific direction.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
