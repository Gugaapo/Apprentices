### Complete Mind Wipe
*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] (Tier 5)*
**Mana Cost:** 5

*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Mind Magic\|Mind Magic]] (Tier 5)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] 5*

The mage erases all of a target's memories, reducing them to a blank slate.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 5 successes.
**On success:** Erases all of a target's memories, reducing them to a blank slate.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
