### Attractive Aura
*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Glamour\|Glamour]] (Tier 2)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] 2*

The mage radiates a subtle enchantment of physical attractiveness that makes them striking to observers.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 2 successes.
**On success:** Radiates a subtle enchantment of physical attractiveness that makes them striking to observers.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
