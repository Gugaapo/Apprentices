### Emotional Mask
*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

*[[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Glamour\|Glamour]] (Tier 3)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] 3*

The mage projects a false emotional state, appearing calm when terrified, confident when doubtful, or friendly when hostile.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Illusionism\|Illusionism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 3 successes.
**On success:** Projects a false emotional state, appearing calm when terrified, confident when doubtful, or friendly when host...
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
