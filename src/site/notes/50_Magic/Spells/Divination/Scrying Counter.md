### Scrying Counter
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Divination Class\|Divination Class]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

The mage detects an active scrying sensor and traces it back to its origin, revealing the scryer's location.

**System:** Roll [[50_Magic/Spells/Divination/Slow Perception\|Slow Perception]] + [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 3 successes.
**On success:** Detects an active scrying sensor and traces it back to its origin, revealing the scryer's location.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (disorientation).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
