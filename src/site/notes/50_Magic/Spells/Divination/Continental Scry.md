### Continental Scry
*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] (Tier 4)*
**Mana Cost:** 4

*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Scrying\|Scrying]] (Tier 4)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] 4*

The mage can remotely view any known location on the same continent with perfect clarity.

**System:** Roll [[Perception\|Perception]] + [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 4 successes.
**On success:** Can remotely view any known location on the same continent with perfect clarity.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (disorientation).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
