### Person Scry
*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Scrying\|Scrying]] (Tier 3)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] 3*

The mage scries directly on a specific person rather than a location, following them wherever they go.

**System:** Roll [[Perception\|Perception]] + [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 3 successes.
**On success:** Scries directly on a specific person rather than a location, following them wherever they go.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (disorientation).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
