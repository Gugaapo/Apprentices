### Anti-Scrying Fortress
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Divination Class\|Divination Class]] (Tier 4)*
**Mana Cost:** 4

The mage creates a zone that is invisible to all forms of scrying and remote observation.

**System:** Roll [[50_Magic/Spells/Divination/Slow Perception\|Slow Perception]] + [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 4 successes.
**On success:** Creates a zone that is invisible to all forms of scrying and remote observation.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (disorientation).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
