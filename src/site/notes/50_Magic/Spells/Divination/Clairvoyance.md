### Clairvoyance
*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Scrying\|Scrying]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] 1*

The mage detaches their sensory perception, creating a localized invisible sensor up to 100 meters away. They can look around a corner, peek through a keyhole, or watch a conversation from the ceiling perfectly unnoticed.
