### Map Render
*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Scrying\|Scrying]] (Tier 2)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] 2*

The mage uses scrying to create an aerial view of their surroundings, rendering a mental map.

**System:** Roll [[Perception\|Perception]] + [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 2 successes.
**On success:** Uses scrying to create an aerial view of their surroundings, rendering a mental map.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (disorientation).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
