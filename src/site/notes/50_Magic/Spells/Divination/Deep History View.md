### Deep History View
*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] (Tier 5)*
**Mana Cost:** 5

*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Scrying\|Scrying]] (Tier 5)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] 5*

The mage reads the mana imprint of a location going back centuries, witnessing ancient events.

**System:** Roll [[Perception\|Perception]] + [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 5 successes.
**On success:** Reads the mana imprint of a location going back centuries, witnessing ancient events.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (disorientation).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
