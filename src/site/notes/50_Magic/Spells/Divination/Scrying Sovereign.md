### Scrying Sovereign
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Divination Class\|Divination Class]] (Tier 5)*
**Mana Cost:** 5

The mage achieves perfect surveillance mastery — undetectable, untraceable, and capable of scrying through any non-divin...

**System:** Roll [[50_Magic/Spells/Divination/Slow Perception\|Slow Perception]] + [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 5 successes.
**On success:** Achieves perfect surveillance mastery — undetectable, untraceable, and capable of scrying through any non-divin...
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (disorientation).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
