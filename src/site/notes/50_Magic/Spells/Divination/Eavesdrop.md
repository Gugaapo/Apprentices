### Eavesdrop
*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Divination/Scrying\|Scrying]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] 1*

The mage extends their hearing to a nearby location, listening to conversations through walls.

**System:** Roll [[Perception\|Perception]] + [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Extends their hearing to a nearby location, listening to conversations through walls.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (disorientation).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
