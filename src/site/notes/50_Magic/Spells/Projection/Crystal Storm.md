### Crystal Storm
*[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

*[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Evocation\|Evocation]] (Tier 3)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] 3*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage rapidly crystallizes mana into dozens of razor-sharp shards and launches them in a horizontal spread like magical shrapnel.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 3 successes.
**On success:** Rapidly crystallizes mana into dozens of razor-sharp shards and launches them in a horizontal spread like magic...
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale) or Potency (+1 damage).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
