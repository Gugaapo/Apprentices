### Mana Knuckles
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Projection Class\|Projection Class]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

The mage wraps their fists in a thin layer of kinetic energy, turning every punch into a magically enhanced strike.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Wraps their fists in a thin layer of kinetic energy, turning every punch into a magically enhanced strike.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration) or Potency (+1 damage).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
