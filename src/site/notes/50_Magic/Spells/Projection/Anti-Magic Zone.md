### Anti-Magic Zone
*[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] (Tier 4)*
**Mana Cost:** 4

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] 4, [[01_Rules/Schools/Ward/Ward\|Ward]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage plants their staff or foot into the ground, radiating a massive dome of absolute null-magic. Within this grey, oppressive sphere, no magical spark can ignite. Flaming swords extinguish, golems collapse like marionettes with cut strings, and spells die on the lips of casers.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 4 successes.
**On success:** Plants their staff or foot into the ground, radiating a massive dome of absolute null-magic. Within this grey, oppressive sphere, no magical spark can ignite....
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or Potency (+1 defense or intensity).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
