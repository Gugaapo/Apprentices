### Combat Roll
*[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] 2*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage propels themselves with a burst of kinetic force, covering ground at supernatural speed while evading attacks.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 2 successes.
**On success:** Propels themselves with a burst of kinetic force, covering ground at supernatural speed while evading attacks.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale/Distance) or Potency (+1 defense during movement).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
