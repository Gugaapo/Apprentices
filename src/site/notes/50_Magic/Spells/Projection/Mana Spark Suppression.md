### Mana Spark Suppression
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Projection Class\|Projection Class]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

The mage extends their hand and snuffs out a minor magical effect — extinguishing a mage light, silencing a magical alar...

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Extends their hand and snuffs out a minor magical effect — extinguishing a mage light, silencing a magical alar...
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale) or Potency (+1 intensity).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
