### Absolute Null
*[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] (Tier 5)*
**Mana Cost:** 5

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] 5*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage creates a permanent zone of absolute magical suppression. Nothing magical functions within — ever.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 5 successes.
**On success:** Creates a permanent zone of absolute magical suppression. Nothing magical functions within — ever.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale) or Potency (+1 intensity).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
