### Combat Flare
*[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 6

A sharp flash of light designed specifically for combat. Unlike decorative light spells, this one is optimized for disorientation.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** A sharp flash of light designed specifically for combat. Unlike decorative light spells, this one is optimized for disorientation.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale) or to apply the *Hindered* Status Effect (blinding).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
