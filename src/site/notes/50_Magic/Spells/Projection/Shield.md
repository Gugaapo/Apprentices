### Shield
*[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage projects a small, momentary shield of force just large enough to deflect one incoming attack.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Projects a small, momentary shield of force just large enough to deflect one incoming attack.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale) or Potency (+1 defense).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
