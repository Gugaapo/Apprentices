### Shield Crack
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Projection Class\|Projection Class]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

The mage identifies the weakest point in an active magical barrier and sends a precise pulse to fracture it.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Identifies the weakest point in an active magical barrier and sends a precise pulse to fracture it.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale) or Potency (+1 intensity).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
