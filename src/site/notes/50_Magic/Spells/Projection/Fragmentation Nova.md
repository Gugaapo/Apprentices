### Fragmentation Nova
*[[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] 3*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage detonates a sphere of crystallized mana that bursts into razor-sharp shrapnel in all directions.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Willpower\|Willpower]] + [[10_System/Schools/Projection/Projection\|Projection]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 3 successes.
**On success:** Detonates a sphere of crystallized mana that bursts into razor-sharp shrapnel in all directions.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale) or Potency (+1 damage).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
