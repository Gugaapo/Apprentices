### Mass Shield
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 4)*
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Ward\|Ward]] (Tier 4)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] 4*
**Mana Cost:** 4

The mage creates a large protective dome that shields all allies inside.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] vs difficulty 7.
**On success:** Creates a large protective dome that shields all allies inside.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
