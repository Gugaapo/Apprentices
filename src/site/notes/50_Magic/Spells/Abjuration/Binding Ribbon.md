### Binding Ribbon
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Sealing\|Sealing]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 4

The mage creates a thin band of sealing energy that ties shut a container, book, or mouth.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] vs difficulty 4.
**On success:** Creates a thin band of sealing energy that ties shut a container, book, or mouth.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
