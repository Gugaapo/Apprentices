### Blood Seal
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Sealing\|Sealing]] (Tier 3)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] 3*
**Difficulty:** 7

The mage creates a seal that can only be opened with a specific person's blood.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] vs difficulty 6.
**On success:** Creates a seal that can only be opened with a specific person's blood.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
