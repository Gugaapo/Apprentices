### Absolute Seal
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 5)*
**Mana Cost:** 5

*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Sealing\|Sealing]] (Tier 5)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] 5*
**Difficulty:** 9

The mage creates a seal of such power that it cannot be broken by any means short of divine intervention.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] vs difficulty 8.
**On success:** Creates a seal of such power that it cannot be broken by any means short of divine intervention.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
