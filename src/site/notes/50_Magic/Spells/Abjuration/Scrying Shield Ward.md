### Scrying Shield Ward
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 2)*
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Ward\|Ward]] (Tier 2)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] 2*
**Mana Cost:** 2

The mage creates a ward that blocks scrying and remote observation.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] vs difficulty 5.
**On success:** Creates a ward that blocks scrying and remote observation.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
