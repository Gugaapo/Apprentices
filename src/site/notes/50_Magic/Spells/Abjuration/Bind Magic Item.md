### Bind Magic Item
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Sealing\|Sealing]] (Tier 2)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] 2*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage alters the spiritual attunement of a dedicated magical artifact (like a wand, a ring, or enchanted armor). They seal it to the exact soul-signature of a specific person. If anyone else attempts to use or wear it, the item responds with a violent electrical shock.
