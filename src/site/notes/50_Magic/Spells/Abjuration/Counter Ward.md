### Counter Ward
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

**Difficulty:** 5

The mage creates a ward that automatically counters one incoming spell.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] vs difficulty 5.
**On success:** Creates a ward that automatically counters one incoming spell.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
