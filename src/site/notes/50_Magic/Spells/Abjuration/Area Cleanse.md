### Area Cleanse
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

**Difficulty:** 6

The mage purges all magical effects from a room-sized area.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] vs difficulty 6.
**On success:** Purges all magical effects from a room-sized area.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
