### Leak Seal
*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Sealing\|Sealing]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 3

The mage seals cracks, leaks, and small openings in a surface.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Abjuration\|Abjuration]] vs difficulty 4.
**On success:** Seals cracks, leaks, and small openings in a surface.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
