### Blink Assault
*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Teleportation\|Teleportation]] (Tier 3)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] 3*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage makes three rapid teleports in sequence, attacking a single target from three different angles.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 3 successes.
**On success:** Makes three rapid teleports in sequence, attacking a single target from three different angles.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale) or Potency (+1 damage).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
