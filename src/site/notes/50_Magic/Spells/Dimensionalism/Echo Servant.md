### Echo Servant
*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Summoning\|Summoning]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage creates a momentary dimensional echo of themselves that performs a single simple task.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Creates a momentary dimensional echo of themselves that performs a single simple task.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration).
**On failure:** mana is lost.

***
**Construct Stats:**
- **Health:** 1
- **Combat Pool:** None (cannot fight)
- **Special:** Exact replica of the caster for one task.
