### Phase Walk
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Dimensionalism Class\|Dimensionalism Class]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

The mage partially enters a dimensional interstice, allowing them to walk through solid matter.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] vs difficulty 6.
**On success:** Partially enters a dimensional interstice, allowing them to walk through solid matter.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
