### Bait Lure
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Dimensionalism Class\|Dimensionalism Class]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

The mage sends a mana signal through dimensions that attracts small spirits or creatures to a specific location.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Sends a mana signal through dimensions that attracts small spirits or creatures to a specific location.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
