### Iron Rain
*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] (Tier 2)*
**Mana Cost:** 2

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] 2*
**Difficulty:** 5

The mage opens dozens of tiny portals above an area and drops a hail of metal scraps through them.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] vs difficulty 5.
**On success:** Opens dozens of tiny portals above an area and drops a hail of metal scraps through them.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
