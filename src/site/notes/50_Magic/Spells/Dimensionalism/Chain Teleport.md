### Chain Teleport
*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] (Tier 4)*
**Mana Cost:** 4

*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Teleportation\|Teleportation]] (Tier 4)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] 4*
**Difficulty:** 8

The mage makes a sequence of long-range teleportations, each jumping from the last landing point.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] vs difficulty 7.
**On success:** Makes a sequence of long-range teleportations, each jumping from the last landing point.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
