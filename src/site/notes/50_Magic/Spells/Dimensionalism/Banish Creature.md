### Banish Creature
*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Summoning\|Summoning]] (Tier 3)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] 3*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage forcibly returns a summoned creature to its origin point, severing the summoner's connection.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 3 successes.
**On success:** Forcibly returns a summoned creature to its origin point, severing the summoner's connection.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Scale).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
