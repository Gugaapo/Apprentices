### Quick Draw
*[[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 3

The mage stores a small object in a dimensional micro-pocket and can summon it to their hand instantly.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Dimensionalism/Dimensionalism\|Dimensionalism]] vs difficulty 4.
**On success:** Stores a small object in a dimensional micro-pocket and can summon it to their hand instantly.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
