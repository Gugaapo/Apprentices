### Clay Figurine
*[[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Animation/Golemancy\|Golemancy]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 6

The mage shapes a tiny clay figure and animates it. The figurine can walk, climb, and carry objects up to its own weight.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Magical/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Shapes a tiny clay figure and animates it. The figurine can walk, climb, and carry objects up to its own weight...
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or Potency (+1 to minion stats).
**On failure:** mana is lost.

***
**Construct Stats:**
- **Health:** 1
- **Combat Pool:** None (cannot fight)
- **Special:** Tiny, can carry up to 5 lbs.
