### Scrap Automaton
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Animation Class\|Animation Class]] (Tier 3)*
**Mana Cost:** 3

Binds nearby metal scraps into a temporary worker drone.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 3 successes.
**On success:** Binds nearby metal scraps into a temporary worker drone.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale) or Potency (+1 to minion stats).
**On failure:** mana is lost.

***
**Construct Stats:**
- **Health:** 3
- **Combat Pool:** 3 dice
- **Special:** Sharp edges (deals +1 damage on hit).
