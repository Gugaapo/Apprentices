### Death Sense
*[[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Animation/Necromancy\|Necromancy]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] 1*
**Difficulty:** 3

The mage detects the presence of dead bodies and undead creatures nearby.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] vs difficulty 4.
**On success:** Detects the presence of dead bodies and undead creatures nearby.
Each additional success adds duration or intensity.
**On failure:** mana is lost.
