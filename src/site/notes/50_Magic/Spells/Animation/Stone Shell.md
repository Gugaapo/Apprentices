### Stone Shell
*[[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

*[[10_System/Schools/Animation/Golemancy\|Golemancy]] (Tier 1)*
*Requires: [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] 1*
