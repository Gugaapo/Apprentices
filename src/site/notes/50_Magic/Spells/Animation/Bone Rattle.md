### Bone Rattle
*[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Animation Class\|Animation Class]] (Tier 1)*
**Mana Cost:** 1

The mage animates a handful of bones to create a rattling distraction or crude alarm system.

**System:** Roll [[10_System/Attributes/Calculation\|Calculation]] + [[10_System/Schools/Animation/Animation\|Animation]] vs difficulty 6. Requires 1 success.
**On success:** Animates a handful of bones to create a rattling distraction or crude alarm system.
**Overcharge:** Can be spent on standard enhancements (Duration, Scale).
**On failure:** mana is lost.
