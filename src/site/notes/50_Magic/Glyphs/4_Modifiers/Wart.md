# Wart
**Translation:** Pause Time / Trigger / Wait
**School/Menu:** Chronomancy (Divination Specialization), Ward (Abjuration Specialization)

A complex modifier glyph that halts a process, suspends a spell matrix, or stops time locally until a condition is met.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Zeit\|Zeit]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Wart\|Wart]]**: Alter Time Pause (Briefly freeze time around an object)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Schf\|Schf]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Feur\|Feur]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Wart\|Wart]]**: Create Fire Wait (A delayed fireball or fire trap)
