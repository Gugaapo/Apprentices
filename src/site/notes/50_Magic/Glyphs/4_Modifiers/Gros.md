# Gros
**Translation:** Greater
**School/Menu:** Projection (Level 4), Alteration (Level 4), Dimensionalism (Level 4), Divination (Level 4), Illusionism (Level 4), Animation (Level 4), Abjuration (Level 4), Soul Magic (Level 4)

A modifier glyph used to massively amplify the scale, area, or intensity of a spell matrix.
