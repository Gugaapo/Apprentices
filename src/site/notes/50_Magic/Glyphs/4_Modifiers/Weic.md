# Weic
**Translation:** Soft / Liquid
**School/Menu:** Alteration (Level 3)

A modifier glyph that reduces structural integrity, making things pliable, soft, or fluid.

- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Form\|Form]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Weic\|Weic]]**: Alter Shape Soft (Make a stone wall moldable like clay)
