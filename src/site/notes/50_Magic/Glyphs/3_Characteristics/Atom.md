# Atom
**Translation:** Molecular Structure
**School/Menu:** Alteration (Level 5)

The ultimate characteristic glyph of Alteration, allowing transmutation of base elements.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Atom\|Atom]]**: Alter Molecular Structure (Turn lead into gold, or stone into glass)
