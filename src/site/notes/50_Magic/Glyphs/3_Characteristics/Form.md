# Form
**Translation:** Shape
**School/Menu:** Alteration (Level 2)

The characteristic glyph that dictates the physical form and geometry of an object.

- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Form\|Form]]**: Alter Shape (Mold clay, bend iron bars)
