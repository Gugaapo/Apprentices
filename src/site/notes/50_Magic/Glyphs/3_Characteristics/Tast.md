# Tast
**Translation:** Touch / Texture
**School/Menu:** Illusionism (Level 2)

The characteristic glyph representing tactile feedback, texture, and surface sensation.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Tusc\|Tusc]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Tast\|Tast]]**: Deceive Touch (Make a wooden sword feel like sharp steel)
