# Trau
**Translation:** Dream / Subconscious
**School/Menu:** Illusionism (Level 5)

The ultimate characteristic glyph of Illusionism, representing the deep subconscious, sleep, and dreamscapes.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Tusc\|Tusc]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Trau\|Trau]]**: Deceive Dream (Trap a target in a waking nightmare or force them to sleep)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Sehn\|Sehn]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Trau\|Trau]]**: Perceive Dream (Enter and observe someone's dreams)
