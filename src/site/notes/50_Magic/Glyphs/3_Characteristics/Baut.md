# Baut
**Translation:** Construct / Statue
**School/Menu:** Golemancy (Animation Specialization)

The characteristic glyph representing an artificially crafted body designed specifically to house an animation matrix.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Lebe\|Lebe]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Baut\|Baut]]**: Animate Statue (Bring a stone or clay golem to life)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Baut\|Baut]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Hart\|Hart]]**: Alter Statue Hard (Reinforce a golem's armor)
