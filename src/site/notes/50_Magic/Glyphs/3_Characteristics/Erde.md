# Erde
**Translation:** Earth / Rock
**School/Menu:** Projection (Level 1)

The characteristic glyph representing terrestrial matter, soil, and stone.

- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Schf\|Schf]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Erde\|Erde]]**: Create Earth (Summon a boulder or dirt)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Bewg\|Bewg]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Erde\|Erde]]**: Move Earth (Earth manipulation)
