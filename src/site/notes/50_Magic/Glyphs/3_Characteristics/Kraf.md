# Kraf
**Translation:** Kinetic Force
**School/Menu:** Projection (Level 2)

The characteristic glyph representing pure physical impact, momentum, and blunt force.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Schf\|Schf]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Kraf\|Kraf]]**: Create Force (A kinetic push or invisible punch)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Wand\|Wand]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Kraf\|Kraf]]**: Force Wall (A barrier of pure kinetic energy)
