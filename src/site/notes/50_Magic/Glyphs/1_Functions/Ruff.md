# Ruff
**Translation:** Summon
**School/Menu:** Summoning (Dimensionalism Specialization)

The core function glyph for forcibly tearing a specific entity or object from its current location to the caster.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Ruff\|Ruff]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Wese\|Wese]]**: Summon Creature (Pull a beast or spirit to you)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Ruff\|Ruff]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Waff\|Waff]]**: Summon Weapon (Call your bonded sword to your hand)
