# Reis
**Translation:** Violate / Tear
**School/Menu:** Soul Magic (Level 1)

The fundamental, highly illegal function glyph for forcefully breaching the spiritual integrity of a living being.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Reis\|Reis]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Seel\|Seel]]**: Violate Soul (A basic soul-strike that causes immense spiritual damage)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Reis\|Reis]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Geis\|Geis]]**: Violate Mind (Tear through natural mental defenses)
