# Ball
**Translation:** Shoot / Blast
**School/Menu:** Projection (Level 2)

A function glyph used to violently expel or project energy outward at high velocity.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Ball\|Ball]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Feur\|Feur]]**: Blast Fire (A continuous stream of flame)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Ball\|Ball]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Kraf\|Kraf]]**: Shoot Force (A rapid kinetic projectile)
