# Lebe
**Translation:** Animate / Move
**School/Menu:** Animation (Level 1)

The fundamental function glyph for granting artificial movement or simulated life to inanimate matter.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Lebe\|Lebe]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Ding\|Ding]]**: Animate Object (Make a broom sweep on its own)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Lebe\|Lebe]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Knoc\|Knoc]]**: Animate Bone (Raise a skeleton)
