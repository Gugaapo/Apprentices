# Horr
**Translation:** Hear / Listen
**School/Menu:** Divination (Level 2)

A function glyph specifically tailored for auditory perception over distances or through barriers.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Horr\|Horr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Raum\|Raum]]**: Hear Space (Eavesdrop on a room through a wall)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Horr\|Horr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/4_Modifiers/Fern\|Fern]]**: Hear Far (Listen to a distant conversation)
