# Andr
**Translation:** Alter
**School/Menu:** Alteration (Level 1)

The fundamental function glyph for changing the properties, state, or nature of existing matter or energy.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Farb\|Farb]]**: Alter Color (Change an object's hue)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/3_Characteristics/Temp\|Temp]]**: Alter Temperature (Heat up or cool down an object)
