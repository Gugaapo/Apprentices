# Herz
**Translation:** Heart
**School/Menu:** Blood Magic (Alteration Specialization)

The characteristic glyph representing the core organ of life and the center of vitality.

## Example Formations
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Andr\|Andr]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Herz\|Herz]]**: Alter Heart (Induce cardiac arrest or restart a stopped heart)
- **[[50_Magic/Glyphs/1_Functions/Reis\|Reis]] [[50_Magic/Glyphs/2_Objects/Herz\|Herz]]**: Violate Heart (A forbidden spell to crush the organ)
