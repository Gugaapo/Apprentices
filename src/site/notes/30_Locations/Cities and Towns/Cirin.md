# Cirin

> - **What it is:** A small, quiet town in [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] — the hometown of the Kazinski family and the "normal life" the players left behind.

## Atmosphere

Cirin is everything [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]] is not. Small, unhurried, and unremarkable. The streets are narrow and cobblestoned, the houses are traditional timber-and-stone, and the biggest event each year is the harvest festival. There are no magical universities here, no mana wells, no dungeon entrances — just a minor noble family's modest estate, a handful of shops, and the quiet rhythms of provincial life.

For the Kazinski children — Daimen, Fortov, Zorian, and little Kirielle — Cirin is the home they were eager to escape. For the players, it represents the "before" — the normal, peaceful life that exists outside the time loop's violent cycle.
