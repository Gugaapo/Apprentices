# Korsa

Korsa is the third-largest city in [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] and serves as a major trade and industrial hub.

## Economy and Industry
The city is known for its bustling textile industry, specializing in spider-silk fabrics sourced from Aranean colonies (like the Sword Divers Web) and Cyoria. It is a home for many rising merchant families who have built wealth through modern commerce rather than noble inheritance.

## Facilities
Korsa features a major train station on the continental line, numerous accessible alchemical workshops, and a regional police department.

## Dungeon Access
Like many major Eldemarian settlements, Korsa possesses a gate to [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|the Dungeon]], which fuels its economy through the mining of **[[60_Items/Materials/Crystalized Mana\|Crystalized Mana]]**.
