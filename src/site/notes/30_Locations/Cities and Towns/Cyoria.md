## Description

Cyoria is a massive magical metropolis in [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]], built over a deep chasm known as "The Hole." It is one of the most developed and largest cities in the world. Despite being close to monster-infested wilderness and lacking a favorable trade location, it rose to prominence thanks to **the Hole** — a massive circular chasm on the west side of the city that is both the most obvious Dungeon entrance ever and the only known Rank 9 [[50_Magic/Glossary/Mana Well\|mana well]].

[[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]]

The enormous quantities of [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] gushing from the underworld make Cyoria an irresistible magnet for mages. Their presence has given the city a unique culture and architecture, with magically-aided constructions that would be impractical anywhere else. Due to the proximity of the bound primordial **Panaxeth**, the city experiences periodic mana surges, making it a highly significant site for mana-intensive research.

---

## The Royal Academy of Magical Arts
Cyoria serves as the kingdom's intellectual heart. The Academy is an elite institution for the third and fourth years of a mage's education, offering a rigorous four-year program with tiered classes. It houses ~600 elite students at any time.
