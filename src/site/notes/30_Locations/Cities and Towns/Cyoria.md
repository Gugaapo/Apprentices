# Cyoria

> - **What it is:** The largest magical metropolis on [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]], built over the only known Rank 9 [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]].

## Atmosphere

Cyoria is a city that hums. Even non-mages can feel it — a low, resonant thrum in the bones, strongest near the western chasm. The streets are wider than most cities, built to accommodate magical traffic: floating cargo platforms drifting between warehouses, spell-light lanterns that never go out, architectural marvels held together by shaping rather than mortar. The air tastes faintly metallic near [[30_Locations/Other/The Hole\|the Hole]], where raw [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] bleeds upward from the underworld in visible shimmers.

Despite its proximity to monster-infested wilderness and its lack of a favorable trade location, Cyoria rose to prominence for one reason: **the Hole**. The enormous quantities of [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] gushing from this massive chasm on the city's west side make it an irresistible magnet for mages, researchers, and industrialists. Their presence has given the city a unique culture — magical architecture that would be impractical anywhere else, a booming [[20_World/Society/The Dungeon Delving Industry\|dungeon delving industry]], and an academy system that draws the brightest minds on the continent.

## Key Locations

### The Royal Academy of Magical Arts
The kingdom's intellectual heart. An elite institution for third and fourth-year students, offering a rigorous program with tiered classes, mentor assignments, and certification exams. Houses ~600 elite students at any time. See: [[20_World/Society/The Academy System\|The Academy System]].

### [[30_Locations/Other/The Hole\|The Hole]]
A massive circular chasm on the city's west side — the most obvious [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] entrance ever and the only known Rank 9 [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]]. The sheer magnitude of magical energy bleeding from its depths powers the city's entire infrastructure. See dedicated entry: [[30_Locations/Other/The Hole\|The Hole]].

### The Dungeon (Beneath Cyoria)
The vast subterranean world extends far below the city. The upper levels are exploited by delvers for [[60_Items/Materials/Crystalized Mana\|Crystalized Mana]] and monster parts. The middle levels are home to the [[70_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Aranea\|Aranea]] webs. The deepest levels contain ancient planar seals — and the [[20_World/01 Time Loop and The Sovereign Gate\|Sovereign Gate]]. See: [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]].

### Noble Districts
The city houses the headquarters of several powerful [[20_World/Society/The Noble Houses\|Noble Houses]]:
- **House Noveda** — Ancient, prestigious. Estate now controlled by the corrupt caretaker [[Tesen\|Tesen]].
- **House Aope** — Progressive, politically savvy. Led by the heir **[[40_People/NPCs/Students/Tinami Aope\|Tinami Aope]]**.

### The Train Station
Cyoria is a major hub on the continental rail network — the primary way students and travelers arrive from across [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]. The players ride the train into Cyoria at the start of every loop.

### Imaya's Boarding House
A private student boarding house run by **Imaya Kuroshka**, where many academy students (including the players) reside. A warm, safe hub that serves as the players' home base each iteration.
