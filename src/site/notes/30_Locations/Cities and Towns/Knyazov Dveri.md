# Knyazov Dveri

> - **What it is:** A small northern town in [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]], known as "Prince's Gates" — a quiet frontier settlement with a dark secret beneath its surface.

## Atmosphere

Knyazov Dveri feels like the edge of civilization. The town sits in the foothills leading toward [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]'s monster-infested northern frontier, surrounded by dense forests and low hills. It is quiet, orderly, and unremarkable — the kind of town where everyone knows everyone, where the mayor's word carries weight, and where outsiders are politely observed.

The town's Rank 2 [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]] feeds a modest local economy of mana-crystal extraction and minor alchemy. A gateway to the upper [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] draws a steady trickle of delvers and mercenaries. Near the edge of town, a small temple serves the faithful — and provides a training ground for a certain battle priest.

## Key Features

- **[[10_System/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]]:** A Rank 2 well provides steady energy for local industries and mages.
- **Dungeon Access:** A secure gateway to the upper [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] levels.
- **[[Alanic\|Alanic]]'s Temple:** The battle priest **[[Alanic\|Alanic]]** maintains a temple on the town's outskirts — a training site for combat magic and warding.
