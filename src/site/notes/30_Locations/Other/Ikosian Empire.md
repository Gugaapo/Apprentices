# The Ikosian Empire

> - **What it is:** The dominant human superpower that once unified much of the known world — now fallen, but its language, laws, and magic system remain the foundation of modern civilization.

The Ikosian Empire was the greatest human civilization in recorded history. At its peak, it spanned most of [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] and [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]], unified under a magocratic government that established the [[20_World/Society/The Noble Houses\|House system]], the state-wide Mage Guild, and the standardized magical tradition that all modern mages still practice.

## Peak and Conquest

Founded by **Shutur-Tanara** on the coast of [[30_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] (northern [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]]), the Empire was built on the power of its mage-aristocrats. It colonized much of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]], conquering the native populations — the [[100_sources/Magic Users of Pre-Ikosian Altazia\|Witches, Morlocks, and Shifters]] — and imposing its language, culture, and [[20_World/Society/The Noble Houses\|House system]].

## The Cataclysm and Decline

The Empire was shattered by the **[[20_World/History/Timeline/04 The Cataclysm\|Cataclysm]]** — a rapid, magical desertification of its heartland in [[30_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]]. This triggered a "Great Migration" to [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] and a long period of stagnation and decline. The successor states eventually splintered into the [[20_World/History/Timeline/06 The Splinter Wars\|Splinter Wars]], producing the modern patchwork of nations.

## Legacy

Every modern nation on [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] is a cultural descendant of the Ikosian Empire:
- **Language:** Ikosian remains the common tongue, with regional variations.
- **Magic:** The Empire's standardized shaping techniques and structured spells are the basis of all modern magical education.
- **The [[20_World/02 The Imperial Artifacts\|Five Treasures]]:** Forged by the gods and given to the first Emperor. Now scattered across the world.
- **The [[20_World/Society/The Noble Houses\|House System]]:** Aristocratic lineages tracing their authority back to Ikosian grants of power.
