# Ikosian Empire

The Ikosian Empire was the dominant human superpower that unified much of the known world before its eventual collapse.

## Peak and Conquest
Founded by **Shutur-Tanara** on the continent of [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]], the empire was a magocratic state that established the [[40_People/Factions/Houses/Noble_Houses_Overview\|House system]] and the state-wide Mage Guild. It colonized much of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]], conquering the native populations and imposing its language and culture.

## The Cataclysm and Decline
The empire was shattered by the **[[20_World/History/Timeline/04_The_Cataclysm\|Cataclysm]]**, a rapid desertification of its heartland in [[30_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] (Miasina). This triggered a "Great Migration" and a period of stagnation and decline, eventually leading to the [[20_World/History/Timeline/06_The_Splinter_Wars\|Splinter Wars]].

## Legacy
The modern nations of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]], including [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]], [[30_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]], and [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinea]], are all cultural successors of the Ikosian Empire. Their laws, traditions of magic, and social structures remain deeply rooted in Ikosian precedents.
