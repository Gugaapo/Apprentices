# The Hole

## Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Massive Chasm / Dungeon Entrance |
| **Location** | West side of [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]], [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] |
| **Classification** | Rank 9 [[50_Magic/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]] |

**The Hole** is a massive, plunging circular chasm located on the west side of the magical metropolis of [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]]. It is the most prominent and obvious entrance to the subterranean world known as **[[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|The Dungeon]]** and serves as the only known Rank 9 [[50_Magic/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]] in the world. 

The sheer magnitude of raw magical energy ([[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]]) bleeding from this unfathomable abyss powers Cyoria's extensive magical infrastructure. This ambient magic makes the city an absolute paradise for magical research, allowing for spells, rituals, and massive magical architecture that would be impossible to sustain anywhere else. 

---

## Key Features

### The Rank 9 Mana Well
As the highest known tier of a Mana Well, the energy pouring from The Hole is so intense that it can spontaneously crystallize, warp local space-time, and bind primordial entities. The well causes periodic "mana surges" in Cyoria, where the air itself hums with raw power. However, this overwhelming magical "noise" can also be used to mask massive build-ups of dark mana.

:::hidden
### The Sovereign Gate
Hidden deep within the uncharted, highly dangerous depths of The Hole lies the **Sovereign Gate**, the ancient, divine artifact mechanism that controls the [[20_World/History/01 Time Loop\|Time Loop]]. 

### The Prison of Panaxeth
The deepest levels of The Hole contain ancient, incredibly powerful planar seals. These seals are responsible for keeping the Primordial **[[40_People/NPCs/Enemies/Panaxeth\|Panaxeth]]** imprisoned beneath the city.

## Campaign Significance

### The Ultimate Target
The Hole is the primary objective of the [[80_Campaign/Invasion of Cyoria\|Invasion of Cyoria]]. At the end of every time loop (during the Summer Festival), forces from [[30_Locations/Other/Ulquaan Ibasa\|Ulquaan Ibasa]] and the [[40_People/Factions/Esoteric Order of the Celestial Dragon\|Esoteric Order of the Celestial Dragon]], led by [[40_People/NPCs/Enemies/Quatach-Ichl\|Quatach-Ichl]], launch a massive assault to seize The Hole. Their goal is to shatter the planar seals within its depths and use the immense mana of the Rank 9 well to summon [[40_People/NPCs/Enemies/Panaxeth\|Panaxeth]] into the material plane.

### Dungeon Delving & Jobs
The upper, relatively "safer" tiers of The Hole are frequently used by [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Cyorian Academy\|Cyorian Academy]] students, alchemists, and mercenaries for work. The city's job boards frequently feature bounties for clearing out minor dungeon flora blocking water runoff pipes or exterminating giant rats and slimes that wander up from the deeper levels.

### Secret Infrastructure & Sabotage
Because of its vast, unmapped nature, The Hole is highly vulnerable to subterranean sabotage. The invading forces use its depths to their advantage:
* **Excavated Tunnels:** The invasion forces violently excavate secret tunnels using high-tier earth magic to bypass the city's surface wards.
* **Hijacked Trains:** Subterranean switch tracks have been secretly constructed by [[40_People/NPCs/Enemies/Sudomir Kandrei\|Sudomir Kandrei]]'s men to divert hijacked trains directly into the deeper levels of The Hole, smuggling in troops, war golems, and supplies for the invasion.
