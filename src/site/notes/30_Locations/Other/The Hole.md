# The Hole

> - **What it is:** A massive circular chasm on the west side of [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]] — the most prominent entrance to the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] and the only known Rank 9 [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]] in the world.

## Atmosphere

Standing at the edge of the Hole is like standing at the edge of the world. The chasm plunges straight down into darkness so deep that no light reaches the bottom. The air rising from it carries a constant, warm updraft that smells of stone, ozone, and something older — something that makes the teeth ache and the hair stand on end. Near the rim, raw [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] is so dense it becomes visible: faint shimmers and heat-haze distortions hang in the air like a permanent mirage.

The city has built railings, observation platforms, and even commercial districts around the Hole's perimeter. Dungeon delvers hire guides at kiosks. Alchemists sell protective wards. Students from the [[20_World/Society/The Academy System\|Academy]] dare each other to lean over the edge. But no amount of commercial normalization can fully tame the primal unease of staring into an abyss that contains entities older than human civilization.

## Key Features

### The Rank 9 Mana Well
As the highest known tier of [[10_System/Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]], the energy pouring from the Hole is so intense that it can spontaneously crystallize, warp local space-time, and sustain magical constructions that would collapse anywhere else. The well causes periodic **mana surges** in [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]], where the air itself hums with raw power. However, this overwhelming magical "noise" also masks other magical signatures — a fact the invaders exploit ruthlessly.

### Dungeon Access
The upper tiers descending from the Hole are the most heavily trafficked [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] access points in the world. City guards regulate entry at official gates. Permits are required. The shallow levels are used for training exercises, alchemy ingredient harvesting, and [[20_World/Society/The Dungeon Delving Industry\|commercial extraction]].
