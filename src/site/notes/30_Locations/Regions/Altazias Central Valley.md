# Altazia's Central Valley

The Central Valley is the historical, cultural, and political heart of the continent of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]].

## Geography
It is a vast, fertile plain bordered by:
- **North:** The Great Northern Forest and northern hills.
- **Southwest:** The Ishekatara Sea.
- **Northeast:** The open ocean.

## Strategic Importance
Control of the Central Valley is considered the key to dominating the entire continent. Historically the core of the Old Alliance, it is now contested by the "Big Three": [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]], [[30_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]], and [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinea]]. Any state that secures the valley could potentially re-establish a continental empire. 

## Economy
The valley is the most densely populated region of the world, housing major academies (like the one in [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]]), industrial centers, and the largest agricultural hubs.
