## Description

Koth is the jungle-covered southern region of the continent of [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]]. It is often referred to by northerners as the **"Green Hell"**. It is characterized by **dense, dangerous jungles** alongside areas cleared for cultivation. Many tropical diseases can be caught here by outsiders — usually not lethal but capable of leaving visitors bedridden for weeks. Locals are largely immune.

## History & Ancient Magic
In ancient times, Koth was a vital center of human civilization and a key province of the [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]]. However, its influence collapsed after the **Cataclysm** caused the desertification of [[30_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]], isolating the region.

The region is littered with ancient ruins featuring inscriptions in **Old Majara**, the precursor language to modern [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]]. Many rare plants and unique magical species flourish in its dense, mana-rich jungles.

## Key Features

- **The Taramatula Estate** — Home of the Taramatula noble family, including [[40_People/NPCs/Others/Daimen Kazinski\|Daimen's]] fiancée Orissa.
- **Green Hell** — A particularly dangerous patch of jungle that contains the **Imperial Orb**, hidden in a cave beneath a chameleon drake nest and guarded by a magically enhanced hydra.
- **Chameleon Drakes** — Native apex predators, along with murderous vines and other dangerous creatures.
