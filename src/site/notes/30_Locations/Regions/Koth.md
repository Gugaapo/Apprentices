> - **What it is:** The jungle-covered southern region of [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] — a mana-rich "Green Hell" littered with ancient [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian]] ruins and deadly tropical fauna.

## Atmosphere

Koth is a wall of green. The canopy is so thick that sunlight barely reaches the ground, filtering down as a sickly emerald glow. The air is hot, wet, and thick with the buzzing of insects — some mundane, some very much not. The jungle is alive in ways that forests elsewhere are not: vines move with purpose, flowers exhale narcotic pollen, and the eyes of predators glow between the roots of trees taller than castle towers.

Foreigners call it the **"Green Hell"** for good reason. Many tropical diseases can be caught here by outsiders — usually not lethal but capable of leaving visitors bedridden for weeks. The locals are largely immune, and their guides are worth their weight in gold. Beneath the canopy lie the ruins of an age when Koth was a vital center of human civilization.

## History & Ancient Magic

In ancient times, Koth was a key province of the [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]]. Its influence collapsed after the [[20_World/History/Timeline/04 The Cataclysm\|Cataclysm]] caused the desertification of [[30_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]], isolating the region from the rest of the Ikosian world. The jungle reclaimed what civilization abandoned.

The region is littered with ancient ruins featuring inscriptions in **Old Majara** — the precursor language to modern [[20_World/Society/Soul Magic Taboo\|soul magic]] research. Many rare plants and unique magical species flourish in its dense, mana-rich forests.

## Key Features

- **Chameleon Drakes** — Native apex predators that blend seamlessly with the jungle. Lethal ambush hunters.
- **Murderous Vines** — Carnivorous plants that actively hunt prey.
- **Ancient Ruins** — Ikosian-era structures containing inscriptions, traps, and sometimes functioning ward systems.
- **The Taramatula Estate** — Home of the Taramatula noble family, including [[40_People/NPCs/Others/Daimen Kazinski\|Daimen]]'s fiancée Orissa.
