# Altazia

> - **What it is:** The primary continent of the campaign — a patchwork of nations built on the ruins of [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian]] conquest, currently undergoing a magical industrial revolution.

Altazia is the continent where [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] and the various [[30_Locations/Regions/Splinter States\|Splinter States]] are located. It is the center of the modern world's political, economic, and magical activity.

## Geography

- **North:** Dominated by vast, monster-infested forests and mountain ranges. [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] is currently pushing colonization efforts (the Great Northern Push).
- **South:** The more settled and developed regions.
- **Heartland:** [[30_Locations/Regions/Altazias Central Valley\|Altazia's Central Valley]] — the most densely populated and developed region, home to [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]], [[30_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]], and [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinea]] (the "Big Three").

## History

Native Altazians were historically conquered by [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian]] refugees fleeing the desertification of [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] (the [[20_World/History/Timeline/04 The Cataclysm\|Cataclysm]]). This conquest brought the Ikosian language, the [[20_World/Society/The Noble Houses\|House system]], and advanced mana-shaping techniques to the continent. It also subsumed the indigenous magical traditions of the [[100_sources/Magic Users of Pre-Ikosian Altazia\|Witches, Morlocks, and Shifters]].

## Modern State

Altazia is a continent of fierce competition and rapid change:
- **The "Big Three"** — [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]], [[30_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]], and [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinea]] — are the dominant powers
- **The [[30_Locations/Regions/Splinter States\|Splinter States]]** — dozens of smaller nations formed after the [[20_World/History/Timeline/06 The Splinter Wars\|Splinter Wars]]
- **The [[20_World/Society/Technological Revolution\|Technological Revolution]]** — trains, firearms, and magitech are reshaping society
- **The [[20_World/Society/Spread of Magical Training\|Spread of Magical Training]]** — commoners learning magic in state academies
