# Hsan

Hsan is a mysterious and isolationist continent located to the west of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]].

## Society and Politics
Historically a major trading partner of the [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]], Hsan collapsed into stagnation following the empire's fall. It is currently unified under a single massive state known as the **Grand Dynasty**.

## Magic
Hsanic mages utilize a variant of the Ikosian casting system. However, due to centuries of isolation, their training methods and spell effectiveness are generally considered inferior to those of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] or [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]]. The continent is often the subject of wild rumors and legends among western mages.
