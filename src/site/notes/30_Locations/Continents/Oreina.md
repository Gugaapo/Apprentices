# Oreina

Oreina is the fifth major continent of [[30_Locations/Continents/Ersetu\|Ersetu]], located near the planet's south pole.

## Environment
The continent is an extremely inhospitable wasteland, almost entirely covered in thick sheets of ice. 

## Population
There are no native human populations on Oreina. Due to its extreme cold and lack of resources, it remains largely unexplored and has zero political or economic significance to the nations of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]]. It exists primarily as a geographical curiosity in maps of the world.
