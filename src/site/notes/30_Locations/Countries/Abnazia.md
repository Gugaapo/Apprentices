# Abnazia

The Kingdom of Abnazia is a [[30_Locations/Regions/Splinter States\|Splinter State]] in western [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]].

## Ambitions
Alongside [[30_Locations/Countries/Tetra\|Tetra]], Abnazia aims to compete with the "Big Three" powers. It lacks any single overwhelming strength but also has no glaring weaknesses, being somewhat similar to [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] but with superior diplomatic finesse.

## The Western Frontier
Abnazia's long-term strategy involves westward expansion beyond the **Winter Mountains** into newly colonized lands. They believe that by unifying these frontier states, they can stabilize a massive new territory and become a true continental heavyweight.
