# Sulamnon

> - **What it is:** One of the "Big Three" powers on [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] — a highly militaristic nation and [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]'s primary geopolitical rival.

Sulamnon is a nation built for war. Located in the south of [[30_Locations/Regions/Altazias Central Valley\|Altazia's Central Valley]], it possesses a deep-seated tradition of elite battlemages and views military strength as the foundation of national identity. Where [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] invests in academies and magitech, Sulamnon invests in soldiers.

## Character

Sulamnon is an expansionist power that has long sought to challenge [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]'s continental dominance. Its elite magical forces are among the finest on the continent, bolstered by the direct involvement of an Immortal.

## Notable Figures

The nation works closely with **[[40_People/Immortals/Meren Lynti Ermazi\|Meren Lynti Ermazi]]**, one of the [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]], who has deep ties to Sulamnon's military-magical establishment. **[[40_People/Immortals/Tehanna Arta\|Tehanna Arta]]** (another of the Eleven) has also personally trained their most elite forces.

## Foreign Policy

Sulamnon maintains an expansionist agenda, viewing smaller neighboring states as pawns in its geopolitical maneuvering. Relations with [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] are tense; with [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinea]], they are cautious and competitive.
