# Sulamnon

Sulamnon is one of the "Big Three" major powers on the continent of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]]. It is the primary rival of [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]].

## Character
Sulamnon is a highly militaristic nation, possessing a deep-seated tradition of elite battlemages. Located in the south of [[30_Locations/Regions/Altazias Central Valley\|Altazia's Central Valley]], it has long sought to challenge Eldemar's continental dominance.

## Notable Figures
The nation works closely with **[[40_People/Immortals/Tehanna Arta\|Tehanna Arta]]**, one of the "Immortal Eleven," who personally trains their most elite magical forces in preparation for future wars.

## Foreign Policy
Sulamnon maintains an expansionist agenda, often viewing smaller neighboring states like **Kalvare** as mere pawns in its geopolitical maneuvering.
