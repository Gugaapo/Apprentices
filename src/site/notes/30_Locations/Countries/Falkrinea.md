# Falkrinea

Falkrinea is the third of the "Big Three" major powers on [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]].

## Character
Falkrinea is a republic where political power is primarily held by the **Houses**. It is widely recognized as the continental leader in **[[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|Alchemy]]**, economics, and modern industrial technology.

## Military and Diplomacy
Compared to [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] or [[30_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]], Falkrinea is perceived as weaker in direct land warfare. They prefer to expand their influence through "diplomacy by accumulation"—gradually absorbing neighbors through economic and political pressure rather than outright conquest.
