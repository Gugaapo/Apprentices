# Tetra

Tetra is a powerful  [[30_Locations/Regions/Splinter States\|Splinter State]] located on the coast of [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]].

## Economy and Trade
Tetra is primarily a merchant nation, maintaining extensive trade networks with [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] and [[30_Locations/Continents/Hsan\|Hsan]]. 

## The Navy
The nation possesses a large and highly capable navy, granting it a near-monopoly on trade within the [[30_Locations/Regions/Shivan Archipelago\|Shivan Archipelago]] and along the [[30_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] coast. 

## Military
While their maritime forces are formidable, Tetra's land military is considered somewhat disorganized and untested in actual combat, despite recent efforts to militarize in response to shifting global politics.
