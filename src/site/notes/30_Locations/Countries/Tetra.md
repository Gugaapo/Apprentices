# Tetra

> - **What it is:** A powerful coastal [[30_Locations/Regions/Splinter States\|Splinter State]] with a dominant navy and extensive trade networks reaching [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] and [[30_Locations/Continents/Hsan\|Hsan]].

Tetra is primarily a merchant nation, maintaining extensive trade networks that span the known world. Its coastal position gives it natural advantages in maritime commerce.

## The Navy

Tetra possesses a large and highly capable navy, granting it a near-monopoly on trade within the [[30_Locations/Regions/Shivan Archipelago\|Shivan Archipelago]] and along the [[30_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] coast. Naval power is the foundation of Tetra's wealth and influence.

## Military

While their maritime forces are formidable, Tetra's **land military** is considered somewhat disorganized and untested in actual combat, despite recent efforts to militarize in response to shifting global politics.
