# Manani Weshekari

**Status:** Likely Deceased
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]]

Manani was a combat-oriented mage, gifted with large mana reserves and skilled with a diverse arsenal of combat spells. At some point he decided to start organizing expeditions into the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|deep dungeon]], as this had apparently been his dream since childhood. The first two expeditions went very well, but after he went down into the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|deep dungeon]] for the third time he was never heard from again. He and the rest of his expedition likely perished somewhere in the depths.
