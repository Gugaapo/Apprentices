# Lepi-Amurru Tusa

**Status:** Alive
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]]

Lepi is always seen with a pair of giant scorpions. Their significance is unknown, as he gets extremely angry whenever they are hurt or killed, yet seems to replace them with new ones without any issues afterwards. Though they look normal, the scorpions are magically enhanced with some kind of ritual that makes them deadly opponents, though they usually focus on simply defending their master. 

Lepi has an intense interest in ancient history, especially [[100_sources/Magic Users of Pre-Ikosian Altazia\|pre-Ikosian]] civilizations. His specialties are [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Ward\|Warding]] and [[10_System/Schools/Divination/Divination\|Divination]]. He is currently somewhere in the interior of [[30_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]], far away from any known human settlement. He tolerates solitude extremely well, and often interacts with obscure sapient creatures he finds in the wild corners of the world.
