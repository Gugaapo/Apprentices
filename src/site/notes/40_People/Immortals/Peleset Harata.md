# Peleset Harata

**Status:** Alive
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]], [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinean]] Academies (loose)

An [[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|alchemist]] and a [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|soul mage]]. She is believed to be one of the more crucial members responsible for the whole group becoming immortal. It is known she is bitter at the rest of the group for some unknown slight made against her. She likes to teach, and is known to be rather vain and fond of flattery. She is currently employed as an alchemy teacher at one of the [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinean]] academies, though she has no real loyalty to the state of [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinea]] itself, and does not intend to get involved in their wars.
