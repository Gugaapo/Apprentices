# Tehanna Arta

**Status:** Alive
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]]

Tehanna is most famous for specializing in a skill that most people consider insanely dangerous and impractical – casting her soul into the spiritual realms so she can explore the place. Since doing this places a mage into regular contact with [[70_Bestiary/Spirits/Demons\|demons]] while their soul is exposed to attack, this is generally considered a quick way to get your soul devoured, but Tehanna has seemingly found a way to reliably survive the experience. She spends most of her time in a heavily warded cave in the middle of the wilderness, exploring the spiritual planes while her body lays motionless on a special pedestal she made.
