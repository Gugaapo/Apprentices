# Isek Kalko

**Status:** Deceased
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]]

Isek was a specialist in [[10_System/Schools/Abjuration/Ward\|warding]] and magical artifice. He was assassinated by an unknown group while working on the warding scheme for a royal palace in one of the minor [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazian]] kingdoms. The group triggered a massive explosion that ended up collapsing half of the building, completely vaporizing Isek in the process. It is unknown what the motive for this attack was, or who was behind it.
