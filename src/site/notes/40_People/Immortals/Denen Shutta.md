# Denen Shutta

**Status:** Alive
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]]

Denen is a large, bearded individual who often wanders [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] in search of novel drink and food, or simply amusement. He gets angry quickly, but forgives just as fast. He is a [[70_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Shifters\|bear shifter]], though not a born one, and he doesn’t have a good relationship with the rest of the bear shifters. He is a master of [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Transformation\|Transformation]] magic and has considerable [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|soul]] and [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Blood Magic\|blood magic]] expertise.
