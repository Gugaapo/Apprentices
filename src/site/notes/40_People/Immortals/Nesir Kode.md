# Nesir Kode

**Status:** Unknown (Missing)
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]]

One of the more mysterious members of the [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]]. His abilities are unknown, though it is speculated they have something to do with information gathering, as other members often consulted him for advice and help. He has not been seen for several years, and his whereabouts are unknown, though other members of the [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]] believe he is still alive.
