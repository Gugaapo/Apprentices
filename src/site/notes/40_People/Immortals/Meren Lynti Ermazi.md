# Meren Lynti Ermazi

**Status:** Alive
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]], Kingdom of [[30_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]]

Meren is a generalist archmage, infamous for her extensive use of demon summoning and her brutal combat tactics. She is the most politically active of the surviving [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]], working closely with her home country of [[30_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]] to train their battlemages and prepare for future wars with [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] and [[30_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinea]]. She spends most of her time in her large, well-warded tower that the Kingdom of [[30_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]] built for her at their own expense.
