# Aidak Cren

**Status:** Alive
**Affiliation:** The [[100_sources/Immortals\|Immortal Eleven]]

Aidak spent a relatively short time in [[30_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] after the group returned there, and instead opted to return to [[30_Locations/Continents/Hsan\|Hsan]], where he presumably remains to this very day. He is known to be fond of martial arts, sword fighting, and other archaic, mundane forms of combat. In an actual life-or-death battle, however, he fights with powerful combat magic like any other [[30_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian]] mage. He is known to be an excellent [[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|alchemist]], and it is thought that he worked with [[40_People/Immortals/Peleset Harata\|Peleset]] to make the group immortal. His current whereabouts and activities are unknown, though some speculate he is studying [[30_Locations/Continents/Hsan\|Hsan]]’s unique magical traditions.
