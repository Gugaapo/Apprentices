# House Noveda

> - **What it was:** A military noble house deeply tied to the Crown — fierce, traditional, and honorable.
> - **What happened:** Decimated by the [[20_World/History/Timeline/06 The Splinter Wars\|Splinter Wars]], then wiped out by [[20_World/History/Timeline/07 The Weeping\|The Weeping]]. Only [[40_People/NPCs/Allies/Zach Noveda\|Zach]] survives.
> - **Why it matters:** It's the house of [[40_People/NPCs/Allies/Zach Noveda\|Zach Noveda]]

## History

House Noveda was traditionally a **military house**, deeply intertwined with the [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] royal family and known for fierce traditionalism. Service was a core pillar: every male member served in the military, and many female members enlisted as well.

The Novedas were staunch supporters of [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]'s military expansion during the [[20_World/History/Timeline/06 The Splinter Wars\|Splinter Wars]], suffering heavy casualties. The final blow was [[20_World/History/Timeline/07 The Weeping\|The Weeping]] — the magical plague killed every remaining main-line member and most branch lines, leaving only [[40_People/NPCs/Allies/Zach Noveda\|Zach]] as the sole survivor.

## Current Status

Because Zach was a minor, the Crown appointed **[[40_People/NPCs/Enemies/Tesen Zveri\|Tesen Zveri]]** as caretaker. He has systematically looted the estate:
- Paying himself exorbitant "caretaker fees"
- Selling priceless heirlooms to associates at suspiciously low prices
- Giving portions of the stolen wealth to the Crown to secure their silence
