# House Taramatula

> - **What it is:** The most prominent noble family in [[30_Locations/Regions/Koth\|Koth]] — masters of mind magic and giant spider domestication.
> - **What it controls:** Tremendous political and military power across the "Green Hell" through spider-based warfare and psychic influence.
> - **Key connection:** Orissa Taramatula is [[40_People/NPCs/Others/Daimen Kazinski\|Daimen]]'s fiancée.

## Description

House Taramatula wields tremendous influence across [[30_Locations/Regions/Koth\|Koth]] through mastery of **mind magic** and their domestication and breeding of **giant spiders** — a practice giving them both military and economic power in the hostile jungle environment.

**Orissa Taramatula** is [[40_People/NPCs/Others/Daimen Kazinski\|Daimen]]'s fiancée, creating a direct family connection for the players.
