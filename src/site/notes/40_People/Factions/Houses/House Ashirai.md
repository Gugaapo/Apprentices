# House Ashirai

The Ashirai are a notable and growing magical family in [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]], currently striving to gain official recognition as a House. 

## The Soul Bond Specialty
The Ashirai family consistently produces soul-bonded twins. Their magical research and heritage are centered entirely around these unique **soul bonds**. It is highly likely that they possess some of the most advanced knowledge of [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]] and soul manipulation outside of the [[40_People/Factions/Triumvirate Church\|Triumvirate Church]].

## Heirs
The current heirs of the family are the twins **[[40_People/NPCs/Students/Aneka Ashirai\|Aneka]]** and **[[40_People/NPCs/Students/Armie Ashirai\|Armie]]** Ashirai, who are classmates of the party.
