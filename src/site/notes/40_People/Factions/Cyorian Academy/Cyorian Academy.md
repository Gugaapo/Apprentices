## Description

The **Cyorian Academy** (formally the Royal Academy of Magical Arts) is the most prestigious magical institution in [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] and one of the most respected on the continent. Located in the heart of [[30_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]] the Academy trains students from age ~15 in a multi-year curriculum covering the fundamentals of shaping, spell formula, and at least one school of magic.

The Academy is both a school and a political institution — its graduates form the backbone of [[30_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]'s military and bureaucratic mage corps.

## Curriculum

- **First Year:** The Basic Three (Luminescence, Levitation, Ignition), shaping exercises, and introductory theory.
- **Second Year:** Specialization begins; students choose electives and a mentor.
- **Third Year:** Advanced shaping, combat magic (under [[40_People/NPCs/Teachers/Professor Kyron\|Professor Kyron]]), spell formula (under [[40_People/NPCs/Teachers/Professor Nora Boole\|Professor Nora Boole]]), and mentor-guided research.
- **Fourth Year:** Field work, advanced seminars, and preliminary state examinations.
- **Electives:** Nonmagical combat ([[40_People/NPCs/Teachers/Professor Nirthak\|Professor Nirthak]]), advanced mathematics, alchemy, and more.

## Campaign Significance

- The Academy is the players' **home base** at the start of every loop. Classes, mentors, and social life form the backdrop of the first week of each restart.
- Many quest hooks, allies, and clues originate from Academy faculty, students, and the Academy library.
- The Academy's defenses are tested and shattered during the **[[80_Campaign/Invasion of Cyoria\|Invasion of Cyoria]]** at the end of the month.
