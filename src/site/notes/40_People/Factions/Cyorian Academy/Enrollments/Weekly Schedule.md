# The Weekly Schedule Matrix

The [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Cyorian Academy\|Cyorian Academy]] offers its core discipline classes during the **Morning (Standard Studying)** Time Slot of the 5-day work week (Monday - Friday). Weekends are entirely free for students to do as they please.

To simulate the difficult choices of university enrollment, the 7 Core Schools and their associated Specializations/Electives are spread concurrently across the week. If a student wishes to take two classes offered on the same morning, they have a **Schedule Conflict**. They must choose one or the other for that specific time loop. 

*(Note: [[10_System/Schools/Soul Magic/Soul Magic\|Soul Magic]], [[10_System/Schools/Alteration/Blood Magic\|Blood Magic]], and [[10_System/Schools/Illusionism/Mind Magic\|Mind Magic]] are strictly forbidden or inherently restricted, and are not taught publicly at the Academy, nor are they on this schedule.)*

## How to use the Schedule

When a Loop begins, players must define their 2 Mandatory Majors and up to 3 Optional Minors.

**Example Conflict:**
- **Zorian** wants to study *[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Divination Class\|Divination]]*, *[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Alteration Class\|Alteration]]*, *[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Animation Class\|Animation]]*, and *[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Combat Magic Class\|Combat Magic]]*.
- He selects **[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Alteration Class\|Alteration]] (Wed)** and **[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Divination Class\|Divination]] (Thu)** as his Majors. He *must* attend class on Wednesday and Thursday mornings or risk expulsion.
- He selects **[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Animation Class\|Animation]] (Tue)** as his first Minor. 
- He looks at his schedule: **[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Combat Magic Class\|Combat Magic]] (Tue)** is his second choice for a Minor... but it shares a Morning Slot with his [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Animation Class\|Animation]] class. He has a Schedule Conflict. He must either drop one, or choose a different day for a Minor (like *[[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Projection Class\|Projection]]* on Monday).

**Strategic Free Time:**
If the student takes [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Projection Class\|Projection]] (Mon), [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Abjuration Class\|Abjuration]] (Tue), [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Alteration Class\|Alteration]] (Wed), and [[40_People/Factions/Cyorian Academy/Classes/Divination Class\|Divination]] (Thu), his **Friday schedule is completely empty**. He now has a "Free Morning" every single Friday to investigate cult activity without penalty.

## The Afternoon Hybrid Slot
Regardless of a student's Morning Schedule, the **Afternoon** is always a "Hybrid Slot." 
- During the Afternoon, players typically visit the library to earn generalized SP, spar in the Danger Room, or (most critically) go visit their specific **Assigned Mentor** (chosen at Character Creation) for specialized help or plot advancement.
