## Description

The **Splinter States** is a collective term for the numerous smaller, independent countries and kingdoms scattered across [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]]. These nations were born in the bloody aftermath of the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/History/Timeline/06 The Splinter Wars\|Splinter Wars]], when the Old Alliance fractured under [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]'s heavy-handed centralization.

Caught between the massive military and economic might of the "Big Three," the Splinter States survive through intense diplomacy, shifting alliances, and formidable defensive militaries. The invention of **firearms** during the Splinter Wars proved that smaller nations could hold off vast armies of traditional noble mages — permanently leveling the geopolitical playing field.

## History

Before the Splinter Wars, much of this region was united under the Old Alliance. When [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] attempted to aggressively centralize power, constituent states rebelled. The resulting century-long conflict shattered the alliance and gave birth to the fiercely independent nations of the modern era.
