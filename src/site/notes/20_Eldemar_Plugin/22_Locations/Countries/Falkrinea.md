# Falkrinea

> - **What it is:** The third of the "Big Three" powers on [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] — a republic and the continental leader in [[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|Alchemy]], economics, and industrial technology.

Falkrinea is the pragmatist's nation. Where [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] yearns for lost glory and [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]] builds armies, Falkrinea builds markets. Political power is primarily held by the **Houses**, who compete through commerce rather than conquest. The republic is widely recognized as the continental leader in **[[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|Alchemy]]**, modern economics, and industrial [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Technological Revolution\|magitech]].

## Military and Diplomacy

Compared to [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] or [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]], Falkrinea is perceived as weaker in direct land warfare. They prefer to expand influence through **"diplomacy by accumulation"** — gradually absorbing neighbors through economic pressure, favorable trade deals, and political marriage rather than outright conquest. Their alchemical exports and banking system give them outsized economic leverage.

## Notable Figures

The Immortal Eleven member **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/Immortals/Peleset Harata\|Peleset Harata]]** ([[10_System/Schools/Support/Alchemy\|alchemist]] and [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Soul Magic Taboo\|soul mage]]) teaches in Falkrinea, lending the nation immense prestige in alchemical research.
