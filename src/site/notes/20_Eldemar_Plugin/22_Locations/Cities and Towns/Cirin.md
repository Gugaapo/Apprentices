# Cirin

> - **What it is:** A small, quiet town in [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]

## Atmosphere

Cirin is everything [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]] is not. Small, unhurried, and unremarkable. The streets are narrow and cobblestoned, the houses are traditional timber-and-stone, and the biggest event each year is the harvest festival. There are no magical universities here, no mana wells, no dungeon entrances — just a minor noble family's modest estate, a handful of shops, and the quiet rhythms of provincial life.
