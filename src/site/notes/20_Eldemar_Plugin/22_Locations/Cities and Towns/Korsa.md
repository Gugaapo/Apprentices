# Korsa

> - **What it is:** The third-largest city in [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] — a bustling industrial and trade hub on the continental rail line.

## Atmosphere

Korsa is a merchant's city. It lacks [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]]'s academic prestige or its Rank 9 [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/Eldemar Glossary/Mana Well\|Mana Well]], but it makes up for it with sheer commercial energy. The streets smell of textile dyes, alchemical reagents, and coal smoke. The train station is a constant hive of activity — goods flowing in from across [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] and beyond and going out.

The city is known for its thriving **spider-silk textile industry**, sourcing raw silk from Aranean colonies (including the Sword Divers Web) and [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]]. Rising merchant families have built serious wealth here through modern commerce rather than noble inheritance, making Korsa a stronghold of the new middle class.

## Key Features

- **Major Train Station:** Hub on the continental line connecting [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]'s cities.
- **Alchemical Workshops:** Numerous accessible workshops serve both commercial and academic needs.
- **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] Access:** Like most major Eldemarian settlements, Korsa has a gate to the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]], fueling its economy through [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/25_Items/Materials/Crystalized Mana\|Crystalized Mana]] extraction.
