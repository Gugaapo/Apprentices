# Ziggurat of the Sun

> - **What it is:** An ancient stepped pyramid deep in the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] Desert, now occupied by a sophisticated tribe of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/26_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Sulrothum\|Sulrothum]] — sapient wasp-like beings favored by the gods.

## Atmosphere

The Ziggurat rises from the desert like a fever dream — a massive stepped pyramid of sun-bleached stone, half-buried by centuries of sand, shimmering in heat waves that make its edges blur and dance. The structure was originally an [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian]] temple, but it has been claimed and reshaped by the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/26_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Sulrothum\|Sulrothum]] — tool-using, sapient wasp-like beings who have built their hive into and around the ancient stone.

The air is thick with a low, constant buzzing. Sulrothum workers — each the size of a dog — move in disciplined columns along carved trenches in the stone. Their High Priest, adorned with gold and crystal, holds court in the apex chamber. Outside, the dunes shift and groan, and somewhere below the sand, something truly enormous moves.

## Key Features

- **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/26_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Sulrothum\|Sulrothum]] Tribe:** This particular tribe is significantly more advanced than others of their species. They maintain sophisticated social structures, limited tool use, and a complex relationship with the divine.
- **Divine Favor:** The tribe is guided by divine beings (gods or angels) who have provided them stewardship of a critical relic.
- **Seasonal River & Underground Lake:** A water source beneath the ziggurat sustains the tribe in the otherwise lethal desert.
- **Dungeon Access:** The Ziggurat provides an access point to a deep section of the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]].
