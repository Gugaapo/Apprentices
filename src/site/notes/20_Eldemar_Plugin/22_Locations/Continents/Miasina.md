# Miasina

> - **What it is:** The ancestral heart of human civilization and birthplace of the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]] — now split between the vast [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] Desert and the jungles of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Koth\|Koth]].

Miasina is the continent where the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]] was born and where it fell. Once lush and fertile, the northern half was devastated by the **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/History/Timeline/04 The Cataclysm\|Cataclysm]]** — a rapid, magical desertification that turned the imperial heartland into the uninhabitable [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] Desert and forced the Ikosian people to migrate to [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]].

- **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Koth\|Koth]]** — The jungle-covered south. Rich in ancient ruins, rare magical species, and inscriptions in **Old Majara** (the precursor language to modern [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Soul Magic Taboo\|soul magic]] research).

## Legacy

Miasina remains a site of immense historical and magical importance. The continent's two halves represent the world's past (the desert ruins of a fallen empire) and its wild, ungovernable future (the untamed jungles that have reclaimed what civilization abandoned).
