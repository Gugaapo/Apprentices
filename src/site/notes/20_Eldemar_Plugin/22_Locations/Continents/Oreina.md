# Oreina

> - **What it is:** The fifth continent of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Ersetu\|Ersetu]] — an uninhabited, ice-covered wasteland near the south pole with zero political or economic significance.

Oreina is an extremely inhospitable landmass almost entirely covered in thick sheets of ice. There are no native human populations, no known settlements, and no economic resources worth the lethal effort of extraction. It exists primarily as a geographical curiosity on maps.
