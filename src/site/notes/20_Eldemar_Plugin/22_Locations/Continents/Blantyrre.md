# Blantyrre

> - **What it is:** A diverse continent far from the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian]] heartlands, renowned for its unique sapient species and magical ecosystems.

Blantyrre is a vast continent that has remained largely independent of Ikosian influence. It is home to several non-human civilizations that have developed sophisticated cultures and magical traditions entirely separate from the human mage tradition.

## Sapient Species

- **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/26_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Murgur\|Murgur]]** — Elephantine mountain-builders with immense physical strength and stone-shaping magic.
- **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/26_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Mlok\|Mlok]]** — Tree-frog people who live in symbiotic plants, cultivating organic architecture.
- **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/26_Bestiary/Sapient_Species/Nantek\|Nantek]]** — Psychic chrysalises that control swarms of worker-caterpillars, operating as distributed hive-minds.
