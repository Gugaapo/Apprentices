# Ersetu

> - **What it is:** The name of the planet — a world shaped from the bound corpse of the Seventh Primordial Dragon, its veins forming the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] and its heart pumping raw [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] into the depths.

Ersetu is the world. Every continent, every ocean, every tunnel of the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] exists on or within it.

## Creation Myth

According to [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian]] mythology, the gods created the world from the remains of the **Seventh Primordial Dragon**. Unlike the first six dragons who were slain outright, the seventh was captured and bound — its body reshaped into the planet and its mind scattered into fragments.

## The Heart of the World

The [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] tunnels are believed to be the veins of the bound World Dragon, converging at the planet's core. There, the still-beating **Heart of the World** radiates the chaotic vitality that creates ambient [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] and births the increasingly alien monsters that inhabit the depths. Modern scholars dispute whether tunnels can physically reach the molten core — but they cannot explain what else maintains the Dungeon's impossible structure.
