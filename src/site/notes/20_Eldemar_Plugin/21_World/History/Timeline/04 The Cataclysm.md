# The Cataclysm

> - **When:** 1500–1520 STC (20 years of apocalyptic desertification)
> - **What happened:** The fertile heartland of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] turned into the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] Desert in 20 years, forcing the largest migration in human history.
> - **Why it matters:** This is why humanity lives in [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] now. The old world is buried under sand.

## The Desertification of Xlotic

Less than a century after the Silence of the Gods, a supernatural disaster of apocalyptic proportions struck the reeling [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]].

With unnatural, horrifying speed, the great plains of central [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] — the heartland of human civilization — began to dry out. Within **20 years**, an incredibly fertile region capable of sustaining tens of millions was reduced to the lifeless, sprawling wasteland known today as the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Xlotic\|Xlotic]] Desert.

## Global Migration

The absolute destruction of their agricultural base triggered mass starvation on a genocidal scale. Millions of Ikosian refugees fled north, crossing the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Shivan Archipelago\|Shivan Archipelago]] to conquer the "uncivilized" natives of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] and rapidly establishing modern human kingdoms.

The event **permanently shifted the center of human civilization** from [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Miasina\|Miasina]] to [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]]. The glorious Ikosian Empire was dead, buried forever beneath the sands.
