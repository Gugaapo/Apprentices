# The Modern Era

> - **When:** February 1st, 1800 STC — Day 1 of the campaign
> - **What's happening:** A tense geopolitical standoff, rapid technological revolution, and a secret invasion being planned beneath everyone's feet.
> - **Why it matters:** This is the era the players live in.

## The Big Three

The continent is dominated by three aggressive superpowers constantly maneuvering for hegemony:

| Nation | Role | Character |
|---|---|---|
| **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]]** | Wealthy, industrialized instigator | Trying to reunify the alliance by force |
| **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Sulamnon\|Sulamnon]]** | Fierce, magical counterbalance | Primary rival; home of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/Immortals/Meren Lynti Ermazi\|Meren Lynti Ermazi]] |
| **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Falkrinea\|Falkrinea]]** | Massive northern giant | Endless manpower; home of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/Immortals/Peleset Harata\|Peleset Harata]] |

Smaller [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Regions/Splinter States\|Splinter States]] like [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Abnazia\|Abnazia]] and [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Tetra\|Tetra]] survive through diplomacy and formidable defensive militaries. **Everyone expects another continental war** — it is simply a question of when.

## Technological and Magical Revolution

The integration of magic and machinery (**magitech**) has led to an explosion in civil infrastructure:
- Magical trains connecting the continent
- Telegraph systems enabling rapid communication
- Improved firearms reshaping warfare
- Mass literacy and a booming middle class

The [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Spread of Magical Training\|Spread of Magical Training]] to commoners has broken the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/Factions/Houses/Noble Houses\|Noble Houses]]' monopoly. Magic is no longer the domain of elites — it is an academic discipline taught in sprawling academies.
