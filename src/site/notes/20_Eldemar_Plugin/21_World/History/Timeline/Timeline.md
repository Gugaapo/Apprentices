# World History Timeline

> - **What this is:** The complete chronological history of the world, from creation to the modern era.
> - **Span:** From the Age of the Primordials (0 STC) to the Modern Era (1800 STC — Day 1 of the campaign).
> - **Why it matters:** Every major faction, conflict, and power structure in the campaign is a consequence of this history.

```timeline-labeled [line-3, body-2, active-color-text-accent]
date: Start of Time Counting (0 STC)
title: [[01 Age of the Primordials (The Creation Myth)|Age of the Primordials]]
content: The gods descended from a higher plane and waged a cosmic war against seven primordial dragons, slaying six to fashion the physical world and imprisoning the seventh (the World Dragon) deep within the planet's core.

date: 2 to 900 STC
title: [[02 Rise of Ikosia|Rise of Ikosia]]
content: Human civilization flourished under Emperor Shutur-Tarana Ihilkush, who conquered rivals using the Sovereign Gate. The Ikosian Empire dominated for 900 years, instituting the House System and a pragmatic State Mage Guild.

date: 900 to 1500 STC
title: [[03 Twilight and Silence|Twilight and Silence]]
content: The Ikosian Empire stagnated and decentralized. In 100 BC, the "Silence of the Gods" occurred, where all deities ceased communicating, shattering the state church and plunging the empire into a 60-year civil war.

date: 1500 to 1520 STC
title: [[04 The Cataclysm|The Cataclysm]]
content: The great plains of central Miasina rapidly dried out into the lifeless Xlotic Desert within 20 years. This forced a massive global migration, shifting the political and magical center of the world to Altazia.

date: 1520 to 1600 STC
title: [[05 The Necromancers War|The Necromancers' War]]
content: Former Ikosian nobility attempted to conquer the fragmented human settlements using armies of the dead. The living won a desperate war, exiling the surviving necromancers to the monster-infested island of Ulquaan Ibasa.

date: 1700 to 1795 STC
title: [[06 The Splinter Wars|The Splinter Wars]]
content: Eldemar attempted to centralize power in Altazia, provoking open rebellion. The smaller states used mass-produced firearms to decimate the traditional mage armies of the Great Houses, ending the era of undisputed mage supremacy.

date: 1796 to 1799 STC
title: [[07 The Weeping|The Weeping]]
content: A terrifying, incurable magical plague swept across Altazia, killing up to 10% of the population and wiping out many of the remaining Noble Houses. This forced magical academies to admit commoners, democratizing magic.

date: February 1st, 1800 STC
title: [[08 The Modern Era|The Modern Era]] (Day 1)
content: Altazia is defined by a tense geopolitical standoff between Eldemar, Sulamnon, and Falkrinea. A technological and magical revolution has led to magitech infrastructure, mass literacy, and the widespread training of commoner mages.
```
