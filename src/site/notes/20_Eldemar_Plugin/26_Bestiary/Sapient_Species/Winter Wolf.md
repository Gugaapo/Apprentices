# Winter Wolf

**Category:** Sentient Magical Beast / Canine

## Description
Giant, highly intelligent wolves with thick, frost-covered white or silver fur.

## Abilities
- **Innate Ice and Cold Magic**: They can breathe freezing gusts, manipulate snow, and possess enhanced physical stats and human-level intelligence.
