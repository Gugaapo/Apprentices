# Troll

**Category:** Magical Beast / Humanoid

## Description
Hulking, brutish humanoid monsters with thick, warty skin and immense muscular builds.

## Abilities
- **Rapid Regeneration**: Trolls can heal from almost any physical wound in a matter of seconds, including regenerating lost limbs.

## Lore
A common but deadly threat in the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]]. Battle mages are specifically trained to use fire or acid magic against them, as these elements are the only way to arrest their regenerative abilities.
