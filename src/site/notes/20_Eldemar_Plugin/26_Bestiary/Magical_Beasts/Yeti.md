# Yeti

**Category:** Magical Beast / Humanoid

## Description
A towering, cold-resistant humanoid.

## Abilities
Immense physical strength and an innate resistance to extreme cold and ice magic.

## Lore
Adapted to snowy mountainous regions above the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]], they are highly territorial and dangerous to travelers.
