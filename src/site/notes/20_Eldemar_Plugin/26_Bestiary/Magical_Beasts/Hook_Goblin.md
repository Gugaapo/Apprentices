# Hook Goblin

**Category:** Magical Beast / Humanoid

## Description
Subterranean pack-hunting humanoids.

## Abilities
Known for their vicious, hooked claws or weapons which they use to ensnare and tear apart prey.

## Lore
They roam the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] in scouting parties, using numbers and ambush tactics to overwhelm larger foes.
