# Hook Goblin

**Category:** Magical Beast / Humanoid

## Description
Subterranean pack-hunting humanoids.

## Abilities
Known for their vicious, hooked claws or weapons which they use to ensnare and tear apart prey.

## Lore
They roam the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] in scouting parties, using numbers and ambush tactics to overwhelm larger foes.


:::hidden

## Fictional Mechanics
**Resilience:** High (Takes severe or coordinated Wounds to kill)

## GM Moves
*(When the players roll a Miss (6-), or look to you to see what happens, use these moves):*
- **Hook Slash:** Inflicts a Combat Wound.
- **Pack Tactics:** When another Hook Goblin is engaged with the same target, they attack with Advantage.
- **Scavenger's Escape:** When injured, the goblin immediately scrambles away to safety, repositioning without exposing itself to further harm.

:::
