# Noble Houses Overview

> - **What this is:** The political aristocracy of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] — magical families with wealth, power, and Council seats.
> - **What's happening:** Their monopoly on magic is crumbling due to public academies and the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Spread of Magical Training\|spread of magical training]].
> - **Why it matters:** Noble politics provide quest hooks, allies, enemies, and the social fabric of Cyorian life.

## The Council of Elders

The most powerful Houses hold a **permanent seat** on the Eldemar Council of Elders — a senior advisory and legislative board exercising immense influence over the Crown and national policy.

## Waning Influence

Despite their historical dominance, the Houses' influence is in **decline**. The [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Technological Revolution\|Technological Revolution]] and [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Spread of Magical Training\|Spread of Magical Training]] through public academies have broken their monopoly on magical knowledge. Public libraries and state-sponsored education mean high-tier magic is no longer a guarded family secret.
