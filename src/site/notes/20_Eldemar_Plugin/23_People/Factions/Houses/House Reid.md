# House Reid

> - **What it is:** An Eldemarian Noble House with alleged ties to organized crime and the black market.
> - **What it offers:** Access to **restricted alchemical ingredients** and the Cyorian underworld.
> - **Key member:** [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/NPCs/Students/Kopriva Reid\|Kopriva Reid]] — a classmate and potential gateway to grey-market resources.

## Description

House Reid is a noble family in [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Cities and Towns/Cyoria\|Cyoria]] with a shadowy reputation. They are alleged to have ties to **organized crime** and are known to deal in **restricted alchemical ingredients** under the table.
