# House Boranova

> - **What it was:** A prestigious Noble House renowned for extreme fire magic proficiency through their secret **Ignition Ritual**.
> - **What happened:** Decimated by the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/History/Timeline/06 The Splinter Wars\|Splinter Wars]] and [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/History/Timeline/07 The Weeping\|The Weeping]], leaving [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/NPCs/Students/Veyers Boranova\|Veyers]] as one of the few survivors.
> - **Why it matters:** The flawed reconstruction of their secret ritual made Veyers dangerously unstable — connecting to the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/NPCs/Enemies/Red Robe\|Red Robe]] conspiracy.

## The Ignition Ritual

The main line practiced a secret, powerful **Ignition Ritual** that permanently enhanced their fire magic far beyond standard mages, making them some of the most feared battlemages on the continent.
