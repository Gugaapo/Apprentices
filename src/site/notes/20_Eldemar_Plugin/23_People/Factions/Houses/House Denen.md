# House Denen

> - **What it was:** A small, minor House of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Countries/Eldemar\|Eldemar]] pushed to the brink of extinction.
> - **What happened:** The [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/History/Timeline/06 The Splinter Wars\|Splinter Wars]] and [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/History/Timeline/07 The Weeping\|The Weeping]] destroyed its main branch.
> - **Why it matters:** [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/NPCs/Enemies/Red Robe\|Jornak Dokochin]] was its closest living relative — and was stripped of his inheritance by an impostor.

## Description

House Denen was a relatively minor House that suffered catastrophic losses during the continental upheavals. The main branch was decimated, creating a succession crisis.
