# House Ashirai

> - **What it is:** A growing magical family seeking official Noble House recognition — their specialty is unique soul-bonded twins.
> - **What makes them unique:** Every Ashirai birth produces **soul-bonded twins** — making them holders of advanced [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Soul Magic Taboo\|soul magic]] knowledge outside the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/Factions/Triumvirate Church\|Triumvirate Church]].
> - **Why it matters:** Their soul bonds are observable, non-taboo examples of soul magic — invaluable for research.

## The Soul Bond Specialty

The Ashirai family consistently produces soul-bonded twins. Their magical research and heritage are centered entirely around these unique bonds. They likely possess some of the **most advanced soul magic knowledge outside the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/Factions/Triumvirate Church\|Triumvirate Church]]** — though this knowledge is framed as "family heritage" rather than forbidden study.

## Heirs

The current heirs are the twins **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/NPCs/Students/Aneka Ashirai\|Aneka]]** and **[[20_Eldemar_Plugin/23_People/NPCs/Students/Armie Ashirai\|Armie]]**, classmates of the players. Their soul bond can be detected via spell, the same as familiar soul bonds.
