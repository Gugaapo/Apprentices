# Triumvirate Church

> - **What it is:** The dominant religious organization across [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]] — once the most powerful institution in the world, now a diminished but still critical force.
> - **What it controls:** The global, undisputed legal monopoly on [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Soul Magic Taboo\|soul magic]] and undead hunting.
> - **Why it matters:** The only institution legally permitted to study soul magic — making them essential allies and dangerous gatekeepers.

## Description

The **Triumvirate Church** worships a triumvirate of deities and maintains an extensive network of temples, priests, and specialized warriors across [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Continents/Altazia\|Altazia]]. Originally established globally by the ancient [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/22_Locations/Other/Ikosian Empire\|Ikosian Empire]], their immense historical power stemmed from a true, tangible connection with the gods — who frequently intervened in mortal affairs.

### The Silence of the Gods

This divine connection was **shattered** during the [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/History/Timeline/03 Twilight and Silence\|Silence of the Gods]], when all divine communication inexplicably ceased forever. No prayers were answered; no miracles performed. Stripped of divine validation, the Church fractured — losing nearly all its political power and reverting to centuries of brutal in-fighting and schisms. The failure of their healing arts during [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/History/Timeline/07 The Weeping\|The Weeping]] further eroded their public goodwill.

### The Monopoly on Soul Magic

Today, the Church holds a global monopoly over the **legal practice of [[20_Eldemar_Plugin/21_World/Society/Soul Magic Taboo\|soul magic]]**. Because soul magic is considered deeply unethical and the darkest of all magics, **no one** outside the Church is legally permitted to study or wield it. This gives the Church institutional power over:
- **Undead hunting** — dedicated battle-priests skilled in fire magic and soul perception
- **Necromancy enforcement** — legal authority to execute rogue soul mages and necromancers
- **State cooperation** — even the Big Three powers are forced to cooperate with the Church on these matters
