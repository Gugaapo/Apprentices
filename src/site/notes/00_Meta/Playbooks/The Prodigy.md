## 3. Playbook Moves
*Choose exactly 2 of the following Playbook Moves at character creation.*

- [ ] **Raw Intuition:** When you cast Unstructured Magic on the fly, roll `+Finesse` instead of Willpower. On a 10+, you completely bypass the mandatory +1 Tick Tax and do not take a Wound. On a 7-9, you take the tax, but the spell is wildly devastating.
- [ ] **Copycat:** When you observe an enemy cast a spell, roll `+Memory`. On a 10+, you instantly memorize its Formula and can cast it for the rest of the scene as if it were a Mastered Spell.
- [ ] **Volatile Core:** When you overcharge a spell on a Strong Hit (10+), you may choose 3 standard options instead of 2. However, the resulting magic will always destroy a piece of the surrounding environment uncontrollably.

## 4. NPC Connections & Bonds
*Connections tie your Sanity to the world. A strong Bond acts as an anchor during the adventures.*

**Format: "I have a bond of [Emotion] with [NPC Name] because [Reason]."**
*   Someone here covers for my accidental destruction because...
*   Someone here is terrified of my potential, and I feel guilty because...

**Connection Move (Anchor):** Once per week, when you **spend a Time Slot reaffirming your Connection with an NPC**, roll `+Social`.
- **10+:** You heal 1 Sanity, and your Bond grants you a unique piece of insight/leverage for the current problem.
- **7-9:** You heal 1 Sanity, but something unexpected creates a complication or demands your immediate help.
- **6-:** They reject you or distance themselves. Take -1 ongoing to Social rolls against them for a week.
