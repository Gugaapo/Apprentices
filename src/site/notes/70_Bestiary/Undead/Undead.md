# Undead

**Category:** Undead

## Description
Reanimated corpses of humans or beasts (such as skeletons and zombies), stripped of their original souls and driven by necromantic magic.

## Abilities
Tireless, immune to pain, and capable of following precise commands from their creator. They do not rely on natural [[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] assimilation in the same way living magical creatures do, but are sustained by the death magic animating them.

## Lore
Created through illegal necromancy (a mix of soul and death magic). They are widely used by dark mages and the Ibasan invaders as cheap, expendable shock troops and laborers.
