# Phase Spider

**Category:** Magical Beast / Arachnid

## Description
Large, stealthy spiders with an otherworldly, slightly translucent appearance.

## Abilities
- **Ethereal Shifting**: They can "phase" out of normal reality into the ethereal plane, allowing them to pass through solid objects, completely avoid magical and physical attacks, and ambush prey from alternate dimensions.

## Lore
A highly dangerous ambush predator. Their silk is heavily sought after by mages for crafting dimensional and spatial artifacts.
