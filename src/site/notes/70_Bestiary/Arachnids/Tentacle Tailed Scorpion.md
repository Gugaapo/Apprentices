# Tentacle-Tailed Scorpion

**Category:** Magical Beast / Arachnid

## Description
A terrifying arachnid variant of the traditional scorpion.

## Abilities
Its traditional stinger is replaced or supplemented by a prehensile, venomous tentacle, allowing for unpredictable and highly dangerous strikes.

## Lore
A highly dangerous predator in the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]].

:::hidden
## Mechanical Stat Block

**Health:** 7 Boxes
**Mind Defense:** 2
**Armor:** 3 Temporary Health Boxes (Heavy carapace)

### Key Attributes (Dice Pools)
- **Physical:** Strength 4, Athletics 3, Acrobatics 2, Hand-to-Hand 5, Stealth 3
- **Mental/Magical:** Readiness 4, Willpower 3, Finesse 3
- **Social:** Intimidation 4

### Attacks & Abilities
- **Pincer Crush:** Roll: Strength + Hand-to-Hand (9 dice). Damage: 2 + Successes. If the Scorpion scores 3+ successes, the target is Grappled.
- **Tentacle Sting:** Can be used against a Grappled target automatically, or as a normal attack (Roll: Finesse + Hand-to-Hand [8 dice]). Damage: 1 + Successes.
- **Neurotoxin:** A target hit by the Tentacle Sting must roll Strength + Medicine (Difficulty 6). Failure inflicts 2 additional damage and a -1 penalty to Finesse and Calculation for the rest of the scene.
:::
