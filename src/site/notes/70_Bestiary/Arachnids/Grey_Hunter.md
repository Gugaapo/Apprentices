# Grey Hunter

**Category:** Magical Beast / Arachnid

## Description
A massive, ash-grey spider with a terrifyingly sleek and predatory build. 

## Abilities
- **Absolute Magic Resistance**: Spells simply slide off its carapace. 
- **Physical Prowess & Venom**: It possesses blinding physical speed, can cut through magical wards with its legs, and carries a highly lethal, fast-acting venom.

## Lore
Considered one of the most dangerous non-sentient predators in the upper [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]]. Mages fear them immensely because traditional combat magic is useless against them. Their eggs and silk are worth a fortune on the black market.
## Mechanical Stat Block

**Health:** 8 Boxes (Surprisingly durable for a spider)
**Mind Defense:** Immune (Absolute Magic Resistance)
**Armor:** 2 Temporary Health Boxes (Chitin)

### Key Attributes (Dice Pools)
- **Physical:** Strength 4, Athletics 6, Acrobatics 6, Hand-to-Hand 5, Stealth 6
- **Mental/Magical:** Readiness 6, Willpower 6 (Absolute), Finesse 5
- **Social:** Intimidation 6

### Attacks & Abilities
- **Lethal Bite:** Roll: Strength + Hand-to-Hand (9 dice). Damage: 2 + Successes.
- **Grey Hunter Venom:** If the bite deals damage, the target must roll Strength + Medicine (Difficulty 8). Failure results in instant paralysis for 1 hour; critical failure results in heart stoppage (death in 3 rounds without immediate medical magic).
- **Absolute Magic Resistance:** The Grey Hunter is completely immune to all direct magic. Spells that target it fail automatically. It can only be harmed by physical weapons, environmental hazards (like a collapsing ceiling), or indirect physical projectiles (like a boulder thrown by golemancy).
- **Blinding Speed:** The Grey Hunter always acts first in a combat narrative unless surprised by a non-magical ambush. Attackers suffer a -2 penalty to hit it with ranged weapons.
