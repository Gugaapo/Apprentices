# Aranea

## Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Sapient Species (large jumping spiders) |
| **Size** | 40–50 cm (torso-sized) |
| **Communication** | Innate telepathy |
| **Social Structure** | All-female tribal *webs* led by matriarchs |

| **Lifespan** | Up to 45 years (55 = "ancient") |
| **Maturity** | Sexual maturity at 2 years; adulthood at 6+ years |

---

## Description

Aranea are a species of large, sapient **jumping spiders** native to the continent of Altazia. All aranea possess **innate telepathic powers**, which they use as their primary method of communication and self-defense. They naturally congregate into small tribes called **webs**, each maintaining territory in the shallow sections of the Dungeon near human communities.

They exist in a secretive symbiosis with local humans — serious threats are neutralized with human help, but they rarely reveal themselves to humans at large, preferring to interact with a select few trusted individuals.

---

## Biology

- Two large, forward-facing pitch-black eyes give excellent vision, supported by three pairs of smaller eyes for 360° fuzzy vision.
- Impressive jumping ability and great reflexes.
- Giant fangs capable of piercing a human skull.
- Can sense vibrations through their feet and "taste" things through leg hairs.
- Mostly black-colored, though brown hues and albinism exist.
- **Extreme sexual dimorphism** — males are much smaller and sub-sapient. Despite this, aranea do not look down on males of other species.

---

## Culture

- Names are **concepts and ideas** rather than words, leading to long-winded translations (e.g., "[[40_People/NPCs/Allies/Spear of Resolve\|Spear of Resolve]] Striking Straight at the Heart of the Matter").
- Deeply **prejudiced against non-psychics**, calling them *"flickerminds"* — to aranea, being non-psychic is like being fundamentally crippled.
- Reading surface thoughts of non-psychics is **not considered an invasion of privacy**.
- Trade relationships with humans: they sell **silk and knowledge** in exchange for manufactured goods they cannot produce.
- Feed primarily on rats and stray dogs — they do not eat humans.
- Maturation ceremony at age 9 (in the Cyorian web).

---

## History

Originally a struggling species, aranea were saved by the arrival of the **Ikosians** on Altazia:
1. Ikosians shattered **morlock civilizations**, removing the aranea's main competitor.
2. Ikosians pushed back dangerous magical monsters, making the shallow Dungeon safer.
3. Ikosian magic proved **easy to adapt** for aranean purposes.

The **Cyorian web** was the first to translate human magic for aranean use, and they used this advantage to rapidly expand across the continent.

---

## Known Magic

- **Webcraft** — Their own method of spell formula, inspired by Ikosian spell formula but highly complex and would require years of study for a human mage to comprehend.
- **Magic rituals** — Usable but require significant adaptation due to anatomical differences.
- **Innate telepathy** — Their primary tool, used for communication, self-defense, and information gathering.
