# Iron Beak

**Category:** Magical Beast / Avian

## Description
A durable magical avian.

## Abilities
Possesses a razor-sharp metallic beak capable of piercing armor and magical wards.

## Lore
A dangerous aerial predator, occasionally tamed or hunted for its valuable beak.

:::hidden
## Mechanical Stat Block

**Health:** 3 Boxes (Small, fragile avian)
**Mind Defense:** 1
**Armor:** 0

### Key Attributes (Dice Pools)
- **Physical:** Strength 1, Athletics 4, Acrobatics 5, Hand-to-Hand 3, Stealth 2
- **Mental/Magical:** Readiness 4, Willpower 1, Finesse 3
- **Social:** Intimidation 1

### Attacks & Abilities
- **Piercing Dive:** The Iron Beak dives from the air, rolling Strength + Hand-to-Hand (4 dice) +1 die for momentum. Damage: 2 + Successes. This attack ignores up to 3 points of Armor (Temporary Health Boxes).
- **Flock Tactics:** If 3 or more Iron Beaks attack the same target in a round, they combine into a Swarm attack (Roll: 8 dice, Damage: 3 + Successes, ignores up to 5 points of Armor).
:::
