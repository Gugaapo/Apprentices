# Iron Beak

**Category:** Magical Beast / Avian
**Tags:** [Solitary], [Terrifying], [Magical Beast]

## Description
A durable magical avian.

## Abilities
Possesses a razor-sharp metallic beak capable of piercing armor and magical wards.

## Lore
A dangerous aerial predator, occasionally tamed or hunted for its valuable beak.
