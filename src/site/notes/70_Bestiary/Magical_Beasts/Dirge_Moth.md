# Dirge Moth

**Category:** Magical Beast / Insectoid

## Description
A magical moth found in subterranean environments.

## Abilities
It emits a magical sound or dust, often causing despair, sleep, or necrotic damage to those exposed.

## Lore
Often encountered in swarms or near high-[[50_Magic/Glossary/mana\|mana]] flora in the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]].

:::hidden
## Mechanical Stat Block

**Health:** 4 Boxes (Fragile wings and body)
**Mind Defense:** 2
**Armor:** 0

### Key Attributes (Dice Pools)
- **Physical:** Strength 1, Athletics 3, Acrobatics 4, Stealth 5
- **Mental/Magical:** Readiness 3, Willpower 2, Finesse 3, Illusionism (Innate) 4

### Attacks & Abilities
- **Dirge Dust:** As an action, the Moth releases a cloud of necrotic/mind-altering dust. Everyone within 5 meters must roll Readiness + Acrobatics (to dodge) or Willpower + Mind Defense. Failure results in 2 damage and a -1 die penalty to all actions for the rest of the scene due to despair.
- **Evasive Flight:** Attackers suffer a -1 penalty to ranged attack pools against the moth due to its erratic flying patterns.
:::
