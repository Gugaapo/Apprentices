# Hook Goblin

**Category:** Magical Beast / Humanoid

## Description
Subterranean pack-hunting humanoids.

## Abilities
Known for their vicious, hooked claws or weapons which they use to ensnare and tear apart prey.

## Lore
They roam the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] in scouting parties, using numbers and ambush tactics to overwhelm larger foes.

:::hidden
## Mechanical Stat Block

**Health:** 5 Boxes
**Mind Defense:** 2
**Armor:** 1 Temporary Health Box (Scrap armor)

### Key Attributes (Dice Pools)
- **Physical:** Strength 2, Athletics 3, Acrobatics 4, Hand-to-Hand 3, Weapon Combat 3, Stealth 4
- **Mental/Magical:** Readiness 3, Willpower 2, Finesse 3
- **Social:** Intimidation 1

### Attacks & Abilities
- **Hook Slash:** Roll: Strength + Weapon Combat (5 dice). Damage: 1 + Successes.
- **Pack Tactics:** If another Hook Goblin is engaged in melee with the same target, the Hook Goblin gains +1 die to its attack pool.
- **Scavenger's Escape:** As a reaction to taking damage, the goblin can immediately move up to 3 meters away without provoking attacks.
:::
