# Eyebeast

**Category:** Magical Beast / Aberration
**Tags:** [Solitary], [Terrifying], [Magical Beast]

## Description
A terrifying aberration covered in multiple eyes of varying sizes.

## Abilities
Capable of firing various debilitating magical rays from its many eyes, producing effects like paralysis, confusion, or energy damage.

## Lore
A bizarre and highly lethal creature of the deep [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]].

:::hidden
## Fictional Mechanics
**Resilience:** High (Takes severe or coordinated Wounds to kill)



## GM Moves
*(When the players roll a Miss (6-), or look to you to see what happens, use these moves):*
- **Eye Rays:** The Eyebeast fires a magical ray from one of its many eyes. Choose one effect per round:
  - *Paralyzing Ray:* The target is Paralyzed for 1 round.
  - *Searing Ray:* Inflicts a Combat Wound (Fire).
  - *Disorienting Ray:* The target suffers Disadvantage on all rolls until the end of their next turn.
- **360-Degree Vision:** The Eyebeast cannot be flanked or surprised.
:::
