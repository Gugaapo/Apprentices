# Eyebeast

**Category:** Magical Beast / Aberration
**Tags:** [Solitary], [Terrifying], [Magical Beast]

## Description
A terrifying aberration covered in multiple eyes of varying sizes.

## Abilities
Capable of firing various debilitating magical rays from its many eyes, producing effects like paralysis, confusion, or energy damage.

## Lore
A bizarre and highly lethal creature of the deep [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]].

:::hidden
## Fictional Mechanics
**Resilience:** High (Takes severe or coordinated Wounds to kill)



## GM Moves
*(When the players roll a Miss (6-), or look to you to see what happens, use these moves):*
- **Eye Rays:** The Eyebeast rolls Finesse + Projection (9 dice). The successes determine the effect (choose one per round, or split successes across multiple rays):
  - *Paralyzing Ray:* Target rolls Strength + Athletics vs successes. Failure paralyzes them for 1 round.
  - *Searing Ray:* Deals Tier 3 magical fire damage (3 + excess successes).
  - *Disorienting Ray:* Target suffers -2 to all pools next turn.
- **360-Degree Vision:** The Eyebeast cannot be flanked or surprised. Its Readiness is always considered active.
:::
