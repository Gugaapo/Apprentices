# Cranium Rat

**Category:** Magical Beast / Swarm

## Description
Oversized, mutated rodents with exposed, glowing, pulsating brains protruding from their skulls.

## Abilities
- **Hive-Mind Telepathy & Psionics**: A single cranium rat is barely smarter than a normal rat, but as they gather in swarms, their processing power and magical abilities scale exponentially. A massive swarm can cast complex mind magic and coordinate with terrifying tactical precision.

## Lore
Often found infesting the upper layers of the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]] and city sewers. They are frequently bred, controlled, or used as spies by dark mages and invaders due to their collective intelligence.

:::hidden
## Mechanical Stat Block

**Health:** 2 Boxes (per individual rat) / 10 Boxes (per Swarm)
**Mind Defense:** 1 (Individual) / up to 8 (Large Swarm)
**Armor:** 0

### Key Attributes (Dice Pools - Swarm)
- **Physical:** Strength 2, Athletics 3, Acrobatics 3, Stealth 4, Hand-to-Hand 3
- **Mental/Magical:** Readiness 4, Calculation (scales with swarm size, 2 to 6), Willpower 4, Mind Magic (Innate) 4

### Attacks & Abilities
- **Swarm Bite:** Roll: Strength + Hand-to-Hand (5 dice). Damage: 1 + Successes.
- **Hive-Mind Assault:** A swarm can cast Tier 2 or Tier 3 Mind Magic spells (like mental blasts or illusions) using Calculation + Mind Magic. As the swarm's health decreases, its Calculation and Mind Magic pools drop.
- **Telepathic Network:** Immune to flanking or surprise while part of a swarm.
