# Crystal Ooze

**Category:** Magical Beast / Ooze (Basilisk variant)
**Tags:** [Solitary], [Terrifying], [Magical Beast]

## Description
An amorphous, shifting entity composed of jagged, translucent crystalline structures rather than traditional organic slime. 

## Abilities
- **Petrification/Crystallization**: They can shoot beams or use physical contact to transmutate organic matter into solid crystal. Their crystalline bodies make them highly resistant to slashing weapons and many forms of magic due to their innate [[10_System/Glossary/mana\|mana]] resistance.

## Lore
Found in the deeper, mana-dense regions of the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]]. They digest the organic matter they crystallize, leaving behind beautiful but macabre crystal statues of their victims.
