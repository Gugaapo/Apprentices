# Hydra

**Category:** Magical Beast / Reptilian
**Tags:** [Solitary], [Terrifying], [Magical Beast]

## Description
A colossal reptilian beast with a thick, armored body and multiple long necks ending in vicious, snapping heads.

## Abilities
- **Extreme Cellular Regeneration**: If a head is severed, two more will rapidly grow in its place unless the stump is immediately cauterized with fire or acid.

## Lore
Hydras are incredibly difficult to kill and require specialized, coordinated tactics. A notable hydra is Princess, a divinely enhanced beast kept by the noble Noveda family.
