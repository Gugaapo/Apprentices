# Hydra

**Category:** Magical Beast / Reptilian

## Description
A colossal reptilian beast with a thick, armored body and multiple long necks ending in vicious, snapping heads.

## Abilities
- **Extreme Cellular Regeneration**: If a head is severed, two more will rapidly grow in its place unless the stump is immediately cauterized with fire or acid.

## Lore
Hydras are incredibly difficult to kill and require specialized, coordinated tactics. A notable hydra is Princess, a divinely enhanced beast kept by the noble Noveda family.

:::hidden
## Mechanical Stat Block

**Health:** 15 Boxes (Colossal beast, requires heavy damage)
**Mind Defense:** 4
**Armor:** 5 Temporary Health Boxes (Scales)

### Key Attributes (Dice Pools)
- **Physical:** Strength 6, Athletics 3, Acrobatics 2, Hand-to-Hand 5
- **Mental/Magical:** Readiness 4, Willpower 5, Finesse 2
- **Social:** Intimidation 6

### Attacks & Abilities
- **Multi-Bite:** The Hydra rolls Strength + Hand-to-Hand (11 dice) as a single attack, but its successes can be split across up to 3 targets in melee range. Damage: 3 + Successes (per target).
- **Extreme Regeneration:** At the start of every turn, the Hydra heals 2 Health Boxes unless it took Fire or Acid damage in the previous round. If a head is severed (taking 4+ damage in one hit), two grow back unless the stump is cauterized.
- **Boss Action:** The Hydra gets 1 independent action per round due to its multiple heads.
