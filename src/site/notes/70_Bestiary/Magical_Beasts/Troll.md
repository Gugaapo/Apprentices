# Troll

**Category:** Magical Beast / Humanoid

## Description
Hulking, brutish humanoid monsters with thick, warty skin and immense muscular builds.

## Abilities
- **Rapid Regeneration**: Trolls can heal from almost any physical wound in a matter of seconds, including regenerating lost limbs.

## Lore
A common but deadly threat in the [[30_Locations/Other/Dungeon\|Dungeon]]. Battle mages are specifically trained to use fire or acid magic against them, as these elements are the only way to arrest their regenerative abilities.

:::hidden
## Mechanical Stat Block

**Health:** 10 Boxes (Large humanoid)
**Mind Defense:** 2
**Armor:** 2 Temporary Health Boxes (Thick hide)

### Key Attributes (Dice Pools)
- **Physical:** Strength 5, Athletics 4, Acrobatics 1, Hand-to-Hand 4, Weapon Combat 3
- **Mental/Magical:** Readiness 3, Willpower 4, Finesse 1
- **Social:** Intimidation 4

### Attacks & Abilities
- **Slam / Club:** Roll: Strength + Weapon Combat (8 dice). Damage: 3 + Successes.
- **Rapid Regeneration:** Heals 1 Health Box per round. This regeneration is halted if the Troll takes Fire or Acid damage.
- **Frenzied Charge:** If the Troll moves more than 5 meters before attacking, add +2 dice to its attack pool, but it suffers a -2 penalty to its defense (Mind Defense/Evasion) until its next turn.
:::
